His majesty, etc. . . Opǵeya has sent one camel from the herds. Again in spring according to the klaseṃna regulations a camel from the herds is to be released. When this sealed wedge-tablet reaches you, forthwith [. . .] likewise an aṃtaǵi animal is to be provided by the klaseṃcis. Just as formerly an aṃtaǵi animal was provided by the klaseṃcis, so now it is to be provided. If it has not been provided formerly it is not to be provided now.
