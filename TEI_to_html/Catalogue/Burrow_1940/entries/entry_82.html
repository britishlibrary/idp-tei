82

Section: Obv.
[Manuscript in the National Library of India, New Delhi: not currently available on IDP.]

On the seventh day of the sixth month, from the viṃśpa of Suḡ́uta, and the śata of Cimǵe, one camel with its keeper for toṃgha Apeṃna, one camel with its keeper for Yapǵu.

Note: Śata "hundred" occurs frequently as an administrative division. Viṃśpa which occurs here would seem to be something similiar. It cannot in form correspond to the Sanskrit viṃśa, but could only represent viśva, which is unsuitable.
