Section: Obv.
Yapgu's tax has been paid (consisting of) two felt garments (namata) and in place of the ghee, one felt garment. The hire has not been paid, nor the keep (pac̄avara).
Of Pleya, one koj́ava has been given instead of his consignment. The hire has not been paid, nor the keep.
Of korara Rutraya, the hire of the camel has been paid (to the extent of ) 1 muli. Left over are muli 2.
Of Kaṃcaka, hire of the camel has been paid (in the form of) 1 posara. Left over are muli 2.
