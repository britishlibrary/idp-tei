Or.8211/1438

Section: Obv.
His majesty, etc. . . The cozbo Bhimas̱ena reports that he has a slave called Opuǵe who is employed in the village of Kaṃcaǵa this is the tenth year. Concerning him he has sent instructions two or three times but he does not come.

Note: For ga[na] read probably [vara]. The letters are smudged.
