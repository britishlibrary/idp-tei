In the 30th year, 5th month, 8th day, at that time the witnesses of Taṃcǵe appeared concerning the land in Tsaga, Namatǵa and [. . .]. Paṃcama says that to Taṃcǵoya belong a vineyard and twelve kuthala in the miṣi-land. I have heard it from the mouth of kāya Suḡ́iya.
