Section: Obv.
(To cozbo Soṃjaka.)
His majesty, etc. . . . The kala Puṃñabala informs us that there a man called Kunǵeya is ploughing lands in Navaǵa av́ana. And so he has taken from the farm. If this wedge and seal arrive there, forthwith careful investigation is to be made with oath and witness, (and) if it is really so, these lands are to be handed over to the people of Navaǵa av́ana as their property.
