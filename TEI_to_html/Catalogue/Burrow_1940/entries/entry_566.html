His majesty, etc. . . Kupṣuta and Tilutamaae inform us that they have lost seven strings of pearls (mutilata), one mirror, a lastuǵa made of many coloured silk, and a suḍ́i ear ornament. The tsaṃghina Moṣḍhaya, when apprehended before the magistrates spoke thus: It is true that I stole these objects from Kupṣuta and Tilutamaae. I sold them to Konumae. I have received no payment.
