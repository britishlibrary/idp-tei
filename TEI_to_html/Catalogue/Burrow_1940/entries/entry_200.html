To his dear neighbours, etc. . . . the secret agent Catǵila sends health, etc. . . . and thus I write. Six milima of corn belonging to me was taken and deposited at your door. Now we have sent Koleta and Tami there concerning this corn. Tami's camel is to be loaded with three milima of corn, and my camel too is to be given a load of three milima. Also I have a claim (?) to (?). Certainly you, Upasena, must send it.

Note: muleya ṣa s̱odhake pala is completely obscure.
