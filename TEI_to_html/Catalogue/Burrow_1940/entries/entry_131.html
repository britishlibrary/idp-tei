(1) Of Apñiya and Rutraya the packed (corn) is three milima. Of Apñiya there are three sheep and three khi of ghee. Of Rutraya there are three sheep and three khi of ghee.
(2) Of Apeṃna, three milima of packed (corn), two sheep, and three khi of ghee, etc., etc.
