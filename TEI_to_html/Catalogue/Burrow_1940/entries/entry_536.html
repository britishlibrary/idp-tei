Section: Obv.
From Saṃghuti one milima of corn, etc. , etc.


Section: Rev.
Kaṃcǵeya, Kunǵeya, and aṣǵara ni Moǵecika, these eleven people sowed the kuthala belonging to Yiruṃḍhina (av́ana) in this land.

Note: The term aṣǵara also occurs as a kind of surname without the affix ni. Possibly it is a place-name of some kind, in which case aṣǵara ni moǵecika, would mean Moǵecika of Aṣǵara.
