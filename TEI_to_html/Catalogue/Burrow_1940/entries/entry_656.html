In the 5th year, 8th month, 26th day of his majesty, etc. . . Pepiya there is a man Cimola by name. He arose and gave from his own property a present to Taṃcǵa [. . .]. There the capacity for seed is two khi. Tamcǵa to his father Cimola [. . .] a return [. . .] in front of the magistrates (including) kitsaitsa Piteya. Witnesses to this are [. . .] the apsu Karaṃtsa, the ṣoṭhaṃgha Taṃcǵa, [. . .] son of the śadavida Yapǵu, [. . .] Taṃcǵa has ownership [. . .] whatever he likes to do [. . .] at the command of the magistrates.
