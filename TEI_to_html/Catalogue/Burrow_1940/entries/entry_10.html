His majesty, etc. . . . Lýipe informs us that he is a klaseṃci in Peta-avana by heredity and not an arivaǵa (guide). When this sealed wedge-tablet reaches you (if ) this Lýipe is not an arivaǵa, he must be released (from the duty) according to law.

Note: For complaints of people who have too many duties thrust upon them, cf. 430, 439, 562, in all of which documents it appears that these offices were assigned by heredity.
