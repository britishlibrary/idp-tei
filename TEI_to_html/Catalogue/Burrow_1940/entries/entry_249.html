Note: Most of the preserved portion consists of the usual introductory formula of a letter. Of interest is the phrase occurring in the last line, . . mṛdhena parichitama. This is obviously identical with the phrase occurring in 385 taha ahu uparyaṃ mṛdhena pratich́ami (to be read paḍ́ichami). What mṛdhena is, is not clear.
