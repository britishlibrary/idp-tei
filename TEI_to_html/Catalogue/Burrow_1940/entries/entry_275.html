Or.8211/1556

Section: Obv.
His majesty, etc. . . . Since formerly from there the year's tax in Ajiyama-avana was assessed, twenty years have gone by. You have appropriated this tax there. When this sealed wedge-tablet reaches you, forthwith this tax — we have sent an account-document from here — with that account-document this tax is to be quickly sent here complete by Lepata, the yatma and the aǵeta; it is not to be kept back, it is not to be sent short.
