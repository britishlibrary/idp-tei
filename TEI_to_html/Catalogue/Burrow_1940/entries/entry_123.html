In the 30th year, 1st month, 7th day, at that time Kalyanadham̄a brought news of the army from Sāca. On the same day we gave a horse to Yitaka to keep watch in Sāca. Now it is the second day and he is unwilling to go to Sāca. The ṣoṭhaṃgha Lýipeya has been made witness, the toṃgha Apeṃna, Kartsa, Lýipana, Ripṭi, Rutraya, Ap[. .] Aṣena, and the ari Lýipena.
