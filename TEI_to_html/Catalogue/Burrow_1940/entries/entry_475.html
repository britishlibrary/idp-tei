Section: Obv.

To the beloved of men and gods, etc. . . . And thus I inform you. These people of my household should certainly by all means be under your care. As regards the camel brought here by patseṃne Bhagus̱ena, certainly instructions about this are to be given to Cinaśa. This camel is to be properly looked after.
