Or.8211/1574

Section: Obv.
His majesty, etc. . . . (3) Formerly from there (the tax) of kuv́ana corn was assessed at 350 milima. The yatma Porkota has been sent there on the understanding that they will bring one-(third) part of it into the capital. When this letter of command reaches you, forthwith that corn is quickly to be collected. Forty camels are to be given loads of three milima each (each load consisting) of two parts juṭhi and one part sahini (The remaining) two-thirds are to be deposited at Pisali. Fifteen camels whichever are ready first are to be taken out to (transport) the wine. These camels are to be taken from the military people.

Note: (3) For chiṃditi read ciṃditi. (5) For the unintelligible prathame va ajho tre read prathame va aṭh́ov́e "the ones ready first".
