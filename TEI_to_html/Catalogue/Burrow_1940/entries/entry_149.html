Section: Obv.
[Manuscript in the National Library of India, New Delhi: not currently available on IDP.]

In the 9th year, 1st month, 28th day, the fugitive Maṣaǵa says: The property taken from me consists of four roughly woven cloths, three woollen cloths, one silver ornament, 2,500 māṣa, two jackets, two soṃstaṃni, two belts, and three Chinese robes.

Note: This document is treated by Lüders, Textilien im alten Turkestan, pp. 21 ff.
