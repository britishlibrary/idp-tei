His majesty, etc. . . Kuuṃta reports that formerly he made a complaint to the vasus in Paǵina-avana. He did not take part (?) in the dispute of the cozbo Lýipeya, nor make a complaint. [. . .] came here. He, in the dispute [. . .] I have no claim against Kuuṃta [. . .] he beats (him) and seizes him by the face.
