His majesty, etc. . . . Lýipeya complains that the apsu Uyaǵe and V́apo took one cow of his without just cause. On the second occasion they took a sheep. Also Ciṃga (slave ?) of Saǵapeya was beaten and taken without payment. This dispute, etc. . . .

Note: For uyaǵev́a poṃca, read the words, separating them differently, as uyaǵe v́apoṃ ca. Read varo for aro.
