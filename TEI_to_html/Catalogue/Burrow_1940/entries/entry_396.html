Section: Obv.
His majesty, etc. . . . Cimaka and Ṣekla inform us that there the people in the state service disobey the cozbo Soṃjaka, they (?), and do not act quickly at his bidding. When this wedge and seal reach you, forthwith there, the people in the state service are not to disobey the cozbo Soṃjaka, or transgress his commands.
