541
Or.8211/1428

Section: Obv.
To the feet of the great cozbo Taṃjaka, etc. . . . the ṣoṭhaṃgha Lýipeya sends his respects, etc. . . . and thus with(bowed) head I report. I have sent a scout from here to keep watch against the Supis. Whatever news there is there, I must be informed of it.

Note: The texts on each side of this tablet are identical.
