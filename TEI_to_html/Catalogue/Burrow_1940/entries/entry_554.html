Or.8211/1443

Section: Obv.
The cozbos Kranaya and Lýipeya write, they give instructions to the apsu Opǵeya, the tasuca Caṣǵeya, and the monk Bharsavaḍhi. From there, from Puṃni, all the officials, householders, monks, brahmans, and vurcuǵas, these people are to be quickly brought here by you today. If you do not bring these people here to-day, fifty strokes (is the penalty).
