Concerning sheep from Kapǵeya, to be kept by Suḡ́uta.
This is the seal of the cozbo Soṃjaka.
In the 11th year of the great king Jiṭuga Mairi, son of heaven, in the 2nd month, 9th day, in this reign. Suḡ́uta and Kapǵe made an agreement outside (the court). I Kapǵeya give ten sheep to Suḡ́uta as his property. Suḡ́uta shall have ownership over the sheep, to do what he likes with them. Whoever at a future time raises the question (again) of these sheep [he shall be without] authority, and a penalty will be incurred. Witnesses to this are the toṃga Vuǵato, the vasu Opǵeya. (It was written) at the request of Kapǵeya. Its authority is as long as life.
