His majesty, etc. . . By me the great king in Kroraina, the household of Carakā was granted to this kaṃjakara Kalaṣḍha. From that household of Carakā there is a woman called [. . .]. That woman has settled in the house of Kapǵeya's vasu. When this sealed wedge-tablet reaches you, forthwith that woman is to be taken from the house of Kapǵeya and delivered into the hand of Kalaṣḍha's brother Acila.
