In the 10th year of his majesty the great king Jiṭugha Vaṣmana, son of heaven, in the 6th month, 10th day, in this reign, the army people came from the capital — the guśura Kuṣanas̱ena, Caruveta, the sṕeṭha Vidhura, the cvalayiṃna Puṃñavaṃta, the cozbo Naṃtipala, and Paluǵeya. Corn for maintenance (aṃna nisaǵa) was disbursed among these — for the guśura Kuṣanas̱ena 4 milima and 10 khi of corn for one month and three sheep; for the cozbo Naṃtipala 4 milima and 10 khi of corn and three sheep for one month.
