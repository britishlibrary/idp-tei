Section: Obv.
[Manuscript in the National Library of India, New Delhi: not currently available on IDP.]

To the feet of the guśura Leśvaṃna, etc. . . the cuv́a layina and Atamsiyae send their respects, etc. . . And thus we report. Atamsiyae here has survived the pains of child birth in safety and good health. A son has been born. You must all be pleased. So it will not be long before we (again) send (?) health to your feet. Also what from there in the hand of Poniǵana [. . .
. . . .]1 dhane, 3 dhane of pepper (marica), 1 drakhma of ginger, 2 drakhma of pepper (pipali), 1 dhane of tvaca, 1 dhane of small cardamoms (suṣmela), 4 sadera of sugar.

Notes: O. (3)aro is apparently a mistake for aro < ǵena >.
O. (7) saṃdhis̱ec̄h́yama remains obscure.
