Section: Obv.
[Manuscript in the National Library of India, New Delhi: not currently available on IDP.]

His majesty, etc. . . .Taṃcǵeya complains that his slaves gave [. . . ] to the cozbo Ṣamas̱ena, and he is trying to prove that it is his own property. On that matter they produce witnesses, Suḡ́i, Platǵa, and Naṃḍhaka (Namsuka). When this wedge-tablet, etc. . . . The statement is to be written down truthfully and sent here.

Note: For cozbo ṣamas̱ena as̱ati[. .]ti, read probably cozbo ṣamas̱enaas̱a ti[da]ti. The sentence still remains partly mutilated.
