Section: Obv.
[Manuscript in the National Library of India, New Delhi: not currently available on IDP.]

His majesty, etc. . . . Lýipeya reports that he dispatched a camel to me the great king according to the klaseṃna arrangements, and its load was stolen. I did not acquire by hire a camel (for carrying) a load. This Lýipeya has been favoured by me the great king and let off (paying) the hire. There you must think where the hire of that camel is to come from.
