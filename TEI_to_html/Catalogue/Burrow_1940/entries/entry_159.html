Section: Rev.
From there I hear nothing from you and the authorities. Considering this fact I have now sent two jars in the hand of the śadavida Kolýis̱a. One is to be delivered to Sacha and the other taken to Caḍ́ota. When he brings them there, immediately one jar is to be quickly sent to Caḍ́ota, and a document of account is to be quickly brought to my knowledge (telling) how much this year's ghee is. At whatever day of the month a letter of command goes there concerning the ghee, at that time the ghee is to be quickly sent here to the king's court. Do not allow any slackness here. This letter has been forwarded through a series of people, so do not mind about a present.
