To be given to the cozbo Taṃjaka.
His majesty the king writes, he instructs the cozbo Taṃjaka as follows: Lýipeya makes a complaint here now that soldiers of Saca carried off two cows of his. One cow they sent back, one they ate. This dispute must be carefully investigated by you in person and a decision made according to law; if you are not clear about it there, they must be sent here in custody.
