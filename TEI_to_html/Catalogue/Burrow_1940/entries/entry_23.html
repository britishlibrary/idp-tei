Section: Obv.

. . . . . . . .] of the camels, a camel paṃma has been kept back with Sarpe and Kaḱe. These camel paṃma must be sent here in the hand of the yatma Porkota. Here, in our presence, there will be a decision.

Note: The meaning of the term paṃma, which occurs nowhere else, is completely obscure.
