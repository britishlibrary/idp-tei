In the 8th year of his majesty the great king Pepiya . . . son of heaven, in the 2nd month, 24th day, in this reign, there is a man resident (kilmeci) in Yav́e av́ana called Apcula. He arose and sold land. Kalýiǵeya bought it. In that land [the capacity for seed] is 1 milima 10 khi The price was given, one horse three years old (worth) 30 muli, and was received.
