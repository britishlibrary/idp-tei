Section: Obv.
His majesty, etc. . . .The cozbo Ṣamas̱ena informs us that he has a dispute with various people (? jana janena) concerning a transaction concerning camels and horses. In the [. . . .]th year they are to be waited for (while) in the charge of this man, after the fourth year they are to be handed over to this Ṣamas̱ena. When this sealed wedge-tablet arrives there, a decision is to be made thus.
