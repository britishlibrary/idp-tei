Section: Obv.
[Manuscript in the National Library of India, New Delhi: not currently available on IDP.]

His majesty the great king writes, he gives instructions to the ṣoṭhaṃga Lýipeya; you must have knowledge of what I write [. . .]. Here the tasuca Ph́um̄as̱eva says that he has a slave man called Moti[..]. That (man) is to be sent here in the hand of Apta, (?) is to be handed over to the tasuca Ph́um̄as̱eva.
