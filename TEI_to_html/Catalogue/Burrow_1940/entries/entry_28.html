Section: Obv.
[Manuscript in the National Library of India, New Delhi: not currently available on IDP.]

His majesty, etc. . . . When this sealed wedge-tablet reaches you, the kulola of the kala Purnabala called Kulbhu must be sent here in the hand of such a letter-carrier as will get here quickly.

Note: The term kulola applied to an individual occurs only here and its meaning is unknown.
