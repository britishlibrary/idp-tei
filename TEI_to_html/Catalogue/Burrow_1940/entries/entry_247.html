Section: Obv.
To the master, etc. . . . and thus I inform you; all is in (the sphere) of your divine knowledge. My wine is to be received [. . . .]. It is now the third year that you have not sent this wine here. My [. . . .] a man promised a horse here. The wine in his possession is to be taken. The wine must now without fail be sent in the hand of the śadavita Suḡ́ata. It is to be sent under seal, it is to be sealed with this seal which is the seal of the cozbo. [. . .] in the hand of Priyaśaya. Also [. . .] that is to be quickly sent here, and a letter in answer must be sent without fail.
