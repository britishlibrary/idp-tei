His majesty, etc. . . . The suv́eṭha Bhimas̱ena makes a representation that the sister of Yapǵu who is kilmeci in Yav́e av́ana was taken as wife by the śramaṃna Saṃgapala, kilmeci in Catis̱a devi avana. Neither mukeṣi nor lote have been taken. When this wedge and seal reach you, careful inquiry must be made. If she was taken in lawful wedlock, (the inheritance) is to be divided in equal portions between her sons and daughters. If, however, he has not made mukeṣi and lote, there will be a decision here.
