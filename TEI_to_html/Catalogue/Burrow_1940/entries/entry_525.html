Section: Obv.
You Sotaya must come here quickly. Koleta's hand-letter is to be sought by the aṣǵara Lýimsu, and the ari Aptatǵi by the tasuca Caṣǵeya, and brought here. By the apsu Opǵeya Vurḍhiya, Ucaḍhina and Peco are to be brought here. The monk Piteca, the trepe Jivamitra, the pulaya Sunaṃta, and the ari Salveta — these people are to be brought here by you Caṣǵeya when the cock crows.

Note: For ǵ.[ḍa]ma[na], read possibly [ku]j́ama[na] and cf note on No.86.
