Or.8211/1441(B)

Section: Obv.
His majesty, etc. . . Kuunǵe an Oǵana are klaseṃcis for four armies and you, further, make them keepers of the royal camels. They are doing a job in the state service which added to their army (work) makes five. (Inacting) thus, you do not do right, you do differently from the command of me the great king. When this sealed wedge-tablet reaches you, forthwith, whatever suitable people there are, the camels are to be handed over to them from Kuunǵe. Kuunǵe and Oǵana have to perform their original duties of klaseṃna [. . .] in addition to that no state duties are to be performed.

Note: ajhi (azi) also (U.O. 2) written asi = Skt. adhi. is here used as a post-position "in addition to".
