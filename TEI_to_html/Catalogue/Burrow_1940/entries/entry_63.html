Section: Obv.

His majesty, etc. . . . Lýipeya reports that they took out three witch-women. They killed only the woman belonging to him, the remaining women they released. About this matter you received a command from Apǵeya that recompense was to be made to Lýipeya for this woman. When this sealed wedge-tablet, etc. . . . you must inquire, and according as you the cozbo received oral instructions here at the king's court, in such manner recompense must be made to Lýipeya for this woman.

Note: The same dispute appears in 58.
