Section: Rev.
Also Kapǵeya (son) of Cimola owes a camel to the arivaǵa Camaka. Let that Camaka be supplied (?) therefrom. Also Pǵeca took three camels; by all means let them be under your care. Also orders are to be given that the wine which is on loan with (various) people, is to be delivered.
