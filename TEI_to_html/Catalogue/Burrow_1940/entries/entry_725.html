[. . .] Express instructions are to be given to the vasu Opǵeya. This tax is to be sent here in the hand of the aǵita Kuuṃta and Sarpiǵa. Also a careful inquiry and reckoning of the tax is to be made by the kaṃzavalis. It is to be sent here complete. Cows and sheep are to be taken by the royal cowherds and shepherds, the woman Kroae, etc. . . Having read this, it is to be given to the vasu Opǵeya.
