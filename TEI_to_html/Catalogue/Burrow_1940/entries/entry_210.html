(A list of names with payments.)

Note: The payments mentioned behind the names consist of 3 muli, 1 muli, and 1 muli followed by twenty-eight payments of 5 khi (of corn) each. At the end the total is given as 12 muli. It is clear therefore that 12 muli = 5 muli + 140 khi, i.e. 7 muli = 140 khi = 7 milima, since there are 20 khi to the milima. The muli therefore as a unit of value is the equivalent of 1 milima of corn.
