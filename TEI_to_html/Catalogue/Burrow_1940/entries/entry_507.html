The cozbo Soṃjaka and the detective Opǵeya made a decision. Witnesses there were the noble (people), the cozbo Yitaka, Namarazma, Lýimsu, the v́asu Kakeya, the arivaǵa Rutraya, the frontier-guard tasuca Ciṃgha and Ṣekra. It was written by me the scribe Vuǵaca, son of the ṣoṭhaṃgha Luṭhu, at the command of the authorities, and at the request of Pgis̱a and Suḡ́uta. It has authority in all places.
