When this sealed wedge-tablet reaches you, forthwith careful inquiry must be made into this matter, and just as Rutraya and Paṃcama have had this Patraya working for them, so this Patraya is to be employed for eight years by the suv́eṭha Bhimas̱ena (260) and during these eight years Rutraya and Paṃcama are not to take possession of this Patraya. Note: The two numbers 256 and 260 clearly form part of the same document, and so have been translated together.
In 256 (2) supply [sarva] bh[a]ǵ[a] kiḍ́ati, a phrase used for the division of property inherited in common (cf. 18); or [s̱arva sama] bhaǵa as in 187.
The word cuḍ́o in 260 (2) is obscure. It presumably refers to Patraya.
Insert yati before puna in 260.
