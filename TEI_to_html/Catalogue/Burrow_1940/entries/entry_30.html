His majesty, etc. . . . Kanasaǵa of Khotan reports that Tuṣana did duty in the tseǵe in place of Apiǵo, and in his place Kanasaǵa of Khotan did duty. When this sealed wedge-tablet, etc. . . . wages are to be sent her from Apiǵo to Tuṣana.

Note: For bheǵeśami read tseǵeyami and compare 484, 505, and 556. The meaning of the term tseǵe has not been ascertained.
