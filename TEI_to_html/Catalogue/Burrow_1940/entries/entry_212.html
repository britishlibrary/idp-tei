Section: Obv.
[Manuscript in the National Library of India, New Delhi: not currently available on IDP.]

His majesty the great king writes, he gives instructions to the cozbo Soṃjaka. Now here Opǵeya complains that Kaḱe and Lýipe let loose some mares in his miṣi(-field) to pasture. The Supis carried them off from there. Now they are making a claim against him for these mares.
