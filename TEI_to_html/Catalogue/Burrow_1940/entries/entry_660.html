Again after the time of his return from the capital 2 paṃdura (?) rolls of silk were paid out.
From Puṣǵari they sent 1 roll of royal silk.
Cetrakirti took 1 sānapru.
Rāṭhapala took 1 palaǵa varna.
Dāruǵe took 1 roll of silk.
Micǵae bought a new palaǵa varna.
Kapotae took 1bound palaǵa varna.
Puṃñasena took 7 rolls of silk.
Of Moǵaya they bought 1 new baṃdhaǵa kremeru.
The mountaineers took 2 rolls of silk.
Namilǵaae took 1 roll of sanapru silk.
