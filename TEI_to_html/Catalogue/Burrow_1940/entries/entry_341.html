His majesty, etc. . . . Also there the camels from the herds such as are three years old, and from that age up to eight years are to be sent here in the hand of the ṣoṭhaṃga Kolýis̱a and Calmas̱a. On the tenth of the eleventh month, Pisali [. . . ] (4) the camels are not to be sent. A letter of information is to be sent first from Pisali. When you have read these (letters) the letter is to be sent from Pisali.
