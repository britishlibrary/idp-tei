His majesty, etc. . . . The four express (aṇtaǵi) camels (provided for) the caṃkura Vajeśa from here are to be sent back from Samarsa. From Samarsa four other camels are to be provided. They are to be sent back from Snuna, and from Snuna four other camels are to be provided. They are to be sent back from Pis̱ali.
