In the 21st year of his majesty the great king Jiá¹­ugha Mayiri, son of heaven, in the 6th month, 20th day, in this reign the woman Laroae took a mare in exchange from the royal (stables). Now there has been a review of the mares in the king's court. Laroae took one mare, and delivered over two. The daughter of that mare [. . .] two, (and) three mothers. All have been handed over to the royal (stables). A decision (has been reached).
