Or.8211/1675

Section: Obv.
To his dear brother cozbo Soṃjaka dear to men and gods, the cozbo Kolýis̱a pays his respects and sends the health of his divine body, much, very much. And thus (he says): Now they have brought twenty royal camels from the king's court. [They are] very ill. We conducted the pracukamaṃ nagara from this kingdom to the open lands (laṭhanami) They came here. (When) this letter reaches you, forthwith quickly you must send people. The pracukamaṃ nagara are to be brought into the enclosed lands (kabhoḍhaṃmi). Pǵena went to the mountain. Kuuna brought the camels there

Note: pracukamaṃ nagara remains obscure.
