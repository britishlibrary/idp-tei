His majesty, etc. . . . Yapǵu informs us that Śruṭhe received tarvardha from him. When this wedge and seal reach you, the witnesses, his friends, are to be immediately examined in your presence, whether it is really so. If Śruṭhe has received tarvardha from them, by whom the house of Śruṭhe has been received, from him tarvardha is to be taken by them.If there is any dispute, etc. . . .

Note: The word tarvardha which occurs only here is obscure. Nor is the reading perfectly certain. See the notes in the edition.
