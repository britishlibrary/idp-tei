His majesty, etc. . . . Now the cuv́alayina Ph́um̄as̱eva has had to go on a mission to Khotan. When this sealed wedge-tablet reaches you, straightaway this Apita must go on the mission, and the cuv́alayina Ph́um̄as̱eva must go with him to Khotan. As regards the two express camels of cuv́alayina Ph́um̄as̱eva, they must be given (him), (likewise) the one camel of Apita must be given (him). Also a suitable man is to be given them as guide (arivaǵa), who will go in front of them. This guide must go on his own beast. Just as formerly you have provided fodder and water for envoys, so now they are to be given to these envoys.
