In the ninth year, 4th month, 10th day, in this reign, the tseǵecis are in the capital. The tseǵes are the ekhara Moǵiya and Tsuǵeṣla. He hired a camel for the sum of thirteen. Tsuǵenaṃma received provisions (consisting of ) 2 milima 15 khi of meal, and 5 khi of maḱa, and one vest (kavaśi). The total of provisions (is) three, of clothes (chataǵa) one; also one knife (katari) — this Tsuǵenaṃma received. Now Tsuǵenaṃma has no claim to possession against the ekhara Moǵiya. Tsuǵeṣla received one karoma. A second karoma Tsuǵenaṃma received in the capital.
