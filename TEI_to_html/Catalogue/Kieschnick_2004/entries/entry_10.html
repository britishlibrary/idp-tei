Incipit ( T09n0262_p0017b04(00) ): 令漏失爾時窮子
According to library records,
purchased in Dunhuang for the Institute by Xiang Da in September, 1944.
A related letter from Xia Ding to Fu Sinian and Li Ji at that time suggests
that the manuscript came from “workers” who, according to rumor, had discovered
a cache of Six Dynasties manuscripts inside a late Qing clay image in the Cave of a Thousand Buddhas.
