Stein Photo 8/1-2: 1st Central Asian Expedition, 1900-1901: photo-theodolite views and object photographs
Collection of 89 loose half-plate printing-out paper prints, inserted in slits in loose pages as in Stein Photo 8/1. This part can be broken down into two sections. Prints 1-14 are photo-theodolite views of the Oxus Valley and the stupa of Mauri-Tim; prints 15-89 are close-up views of objects excavated in the course of the expedition at Niya, Aksipil, Yotkan, Dandan-Uiliq and other sites.
