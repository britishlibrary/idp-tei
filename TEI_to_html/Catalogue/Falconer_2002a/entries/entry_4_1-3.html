Stein Photo 4/1-3: Three photograph albums, 1890s
Three uniformly bound half-leather albums, with quarter-plate printing-out paper prints inserted in aperture windows, containing respectively 95, 91, and 91 prints (plus some loose duplicates). Apart from a few pencilled captions, the majority of the photographs are uncaptioned, but from the few dates supplied this material clearly represents some of the earliest photographs taken by Stein.
