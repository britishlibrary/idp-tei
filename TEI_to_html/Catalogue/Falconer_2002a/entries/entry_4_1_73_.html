Stein Photo 4/1: Views of India, 1890s
Red half-leather bound album containing 95 (plus two loose) prints, mainly on printing-out paper, inserted in aperture mounts, c.1895. Subject matter includes views of Indian architecture at Gwalior, Agra (Taj Mahal, Itimad-ud-Daulah's Tomb), Sikandra (Akbar's Tomb), views of Stein's camp at Mohand Marg and surrounding mountain scenery, views at Srinagar, views at Lahore, and a European bungalow.
