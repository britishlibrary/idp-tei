River valley in mountains. [P.250]
Stein Photo 3/2: Views of Rawalkot, Battakundi and Kashmir (including Mohand Marg), May-June 1905
One of 42 quarter-plate printing-out paper prints. Most of this material is duplicated in album in the Stein Collection in the British Library (OIOC Photo392/23), similarly uncaptioned but with the overall title 'Rawalkot Battakundi May-June 1905'. Subjects include Stein's camp at Mohand Marg and mountain river scenes.
