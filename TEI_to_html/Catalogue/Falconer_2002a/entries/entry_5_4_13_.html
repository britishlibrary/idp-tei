Stein Photo 5/4: 1st Central Asian Expedition, 1900-1901
14 loose half-plate printing-out paper views, extracted from and duplicating material in Stein Photo 5/2 and 5/3. The photographs show the site and excavated sculptures at Rawak, taken during the 1st Central Asian Expedition 1900-1901. This material includes a number of views similar to, but varying from images reproduced in Ancient Khotan.
