Stein Photo 3/3: Views of Mohand Marg, c.1905
53 uncaptioned quarter-plate and 14 half-plate printing-out paper prints, mainly views of Stein's camp at Mohand Marg, mountain scenery and surrounding areas. The quarter-plate views have 'S' series negative numbers, but many are duplicates of prints in Stein Photo 3/2 (S.19 is the same image as P. 224, for example), and suggest that these 'S' reference numbers were later superseded.
