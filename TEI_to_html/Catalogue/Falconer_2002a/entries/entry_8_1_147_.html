Stein Photo 8/1-2: 1st Central Asian Expedition, 1900-1901
A collection of 263 printing-out paper prints inserted in corner slits on loose paper mounts, probably orginally in album form, with pencilled captions on mounts. The material comprises views taken during the 1st Central Asian Expedition and includes a number of photo-theodolite views.
