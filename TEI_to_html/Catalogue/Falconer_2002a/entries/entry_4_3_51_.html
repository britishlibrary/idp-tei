Stein Photo 4/3: Views of India, c.1898-1899
Blue half-leather album containing 91 prints, mainly on printing-out paper, inserted in aperture mounts, c.1898-1899. Subject matter includes archaeological remains, views of Kashmir, particularly Srinagar and Mohand Marg, views on the Jhelum, porters, and Bodh Gaya.
