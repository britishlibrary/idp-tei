Stein Photo 3/5: Photo-theodolite views, probably from the 1st Central Asian Expedition, 1900-1901
17 quarter-plate printing-out paper prints of photo-theodolite views of mountain scenery, uncaptioned, but probably taken during Stein's 1st Central Asian Expedition. The subjects have not been identified. Negative numbers are assigned by Stein.
