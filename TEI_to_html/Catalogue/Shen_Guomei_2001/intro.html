《国家图书馆藏敦煌遗书研究论著目录索引（1900–2001）》以中国国家图书馆藏敦煌遗书编号为纲，著录有关每一敦煌写卷的研究文献，共计8576条。序号前加＊者为后增加条目。

"Bibliography of Dunhuang Manuscripts in NLC Collections: 1900–2001" is a bibliography of research papers relating to the Dunhuang manuscripts in the National Library of China collections, sorted according to the pressmark of the item to which they refer. There 8576 entries in total. Entries preceded by * were later additions to the catalogue.

EDITORIAL NOTE

Please note that some qianziwen (千字文) numbers in the original catalogue contain errors that have been corrected here and linked to the relevant images in the IDP database. They are as follows: 天75 has been corrected to 裳75, 天99 to 宿99, 位99 to 让20, 商45 to 字45, and 商66 to 字66.

The original catalogue also contains entries listed under qianziwen numbers that do not exist in the NLC collection, which have been retained in the online catalogue for continuity but do not link to any images in the database. These entries are 养88, 岂88, 和72, 仁33, and 图14, 44 and 45.
