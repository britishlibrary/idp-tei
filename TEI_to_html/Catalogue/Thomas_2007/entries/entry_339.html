IOL Tib M 758

Section: 1
A left-hand fragment: ll. 6 recto + 6 verso of ordinary dbu can xylograph, smallish size; border lines; marginal title and number almost entirely lost.
Mentions 'brug sgra (recto, 5), Dīpaṃkara, (verso, 1), Atiśa (verso, 4).
Apparently from a work by chos smra ba (dharma-bhāṇaka) Btsun pa dge 'dun rgya mtsho (verso, 5), the IInd Dalai Lama (1479-1541 A.D.).
