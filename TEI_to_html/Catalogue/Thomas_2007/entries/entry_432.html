A complete fol.: ll. 7 recto + 7 verso of elegant dbu med script; marginal number, ka nyer bzhi, 'Vol.(?) L, 24'.
Relates to some mantra(?) diagram, mentioning (recto, 4-5) a pupil (slob ma): partly verse.
recto, 3- de yang ci ltar gdab na/
/'phra na nad myed chad na rlag/
/bkra myi shes na rnos (dngos) grub med/
/g.yas su bskor na slob ma rlag/
/des na mnyam bzhag thig gdab bya/
'That also, how is it to be deposited? If with a struggle ('phra na) ...; if inauspiciously, there is no attainment; if with a rightward turn, the pupil is ruined; therefore the bindu (thig) is to be deposited with composure (mnyam bzag)'.
