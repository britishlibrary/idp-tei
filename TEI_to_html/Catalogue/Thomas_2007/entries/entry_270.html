(a) 019.h (IOL Tib M 657), nearly complete: marginal numbers gsum, '3' and in MS. gsum brgya zhe brgyad, '348'.
From a Buddhist Tāntrik work: mentions various Tāntrik divinities (Yamāntaka and Vajradharakīlā, verso, 6) and phur bu (Kīla) (recto, 1, etc.), and gives mantras.
(b) 019.i (IOL Tib M 658), marginal numbers re bdun, '67' and in MS. gnyis brgya re drug, '266'.
From a Buddhist sūtra. Buddha speaks with Ānanda of an aged woman who in a future birth will be his mother: refers (verso, 3 and 6) to a BuddhaByang chub kyi yan lag gi me tog byed (Bodhyaṅga-kusuma(pus pa)-kara).
(c)019.j (IOL Tib M 659), nearly complete: marginal numbers recto, Tshi bzhi, Tshi, verso, rngi(?) and in MS. bzhi brgya so drug, '436'.
(1) (recto, 5) end of - Gnod sbyi 'A pa rA dzi ta'i gzungs (Yakṣa-Aparājita-dhāraṇī, cf. Cordier_1909_1915: II (III), pp. 91-2, Rgyud, 72, fol. 303-, Mahā-yakṣāparājita-sādhana-tantra).
(2) (recto, 6) beginning of- Gnod sbyi gter bdag drug gi gzungs (Yakṣa-ṣaṇ-nidhi-pati-dhāraṇī).
(d) 019.k (IOL Tib M 660), nearly complete: marginal numbers gsum rnga bra, '3 Rnga bra', and in MS. bzhi brgya re brgyad '468'.
Gser 'od dam pa'i rnga sgra (Suvarṇottama-dundubhi-ṣvara).
Tāntrik formulas and verse, mainly referring to the text itself.
(e) 019.l (IOL Tib M 661), right half.
Mention of various harmful beings, srog 'phrog pa rnams and a recurrent phrase concerning what they severally devour.
Zas su lhag ma za ba rnams, (recto, 1).
Kaumārī, Vārāhī, Indrī, Agni, Yama, Nairṛti, Kālarātrī, etc. (verso, 1-2).
(f) 019.m (IOL Tib M 662), right half.
From a Buddhist Tāntrik work (Dag pa gser gyi mdo thig, verso, 2?).
Mentions (verso, 1) - rkong rtse lha yi rgyal po ('itch, or ringworm, deva-rāja?).
