IOL Tib M 55

Section: 1
Fragmentary at l. yellowish; the last fol. of a MS.: recto ll. 3 of good, neat dbu med script of medium size + l. 1 of punctuation marks + l. 1 in a smaller hand; verso in a different hand ...'o...gu du(su?) 'god pa la kho bo //
A Sūtra, concluding phrase and part of the colophon, giving the title as Chos spyod thams cad kyi man ngag // //mngon par rtogs pa.... Chos spyod added below the line in a smaller hand.
Sarva-dharma-[caryā]-upadeśa. Abhisamaya.. cf. Cordier_1909_1915: I (II):298, Rgyud, 68, Sarva-dharma-caryopadeśa-abhisamaya-tantra-vyākhyā.
The last two lines of the concluding verse give perhaps the locality where the MS. was written and the scribe.
//mdan ma dben tsha dpal bya ba yin //
/lag pa 'phrul kyi lde myig lags //
namely the retreat Mdan ma dben tsha dpal (hermitage) and the scribe (lag pa, 'hand') 'Phrul kyi lde mig (a strange name for a person).
