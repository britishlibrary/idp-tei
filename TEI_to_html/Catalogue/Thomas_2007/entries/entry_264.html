E.G.015.l (IOL Tib M 642), m (IOL Tib M 645); 016.l (IOL Tib M 646), j (IOL Tib M 647), w (IOL Tib M 648); 019a (IOL Tib M 650); 024.a (IOL Tib M 685), b (IOL Tib M 689), c (IOL Tib M 690). Parts of foll. 8 from one or more copies of a collectaneous work: ll. 9 recto + 9 verso of ordinary dbu can xylograph, rather pointed; border lines; marginal numbers, Tibetan and Chinese, on those foll. of which the l. margins are preserved.
(a) 015.l (IOL Tib M 642)
End (verso, 5) of - Rdo rje gcod pa Pha rol tu phyin pa'i byang chub kyi lam (Vajra-cchedikā-Pāramitā-bodhi-mārga).
Beginning (verso, 6) of - A rya A kA sha garbha nAma mahA yA na sU tra (Ārya-Ākāśa-garbha-nāma-Mahā-yāna-sūtra)
The discourse by the Buddha is delivered on the Khalatika hill (see Monier Williams' Dictionary) and the sūtra is therefore, no doubt, identical with that noted in Beckh's catalogue Beckh_1914: p. 56 (Mdo. 22, foll. 333 sqq).
(b) 016.l (IOL Tib M 646), marginal number, recto, ka brgya zhe drug gong, Ka, '146a', verso similarly '146b' (also in Chinese).
End of chapter 16 (Lha mo dpal gyi le'u, Śrī-Devī-parivarta) of the Gser 'od dam pa md[o sde'i dbang po'i rgyal po] (Suvarṇa-prabhāsottama-sūtrendra-rāja), and beginning of c. 17.
The well known sūtra: for the Tibetan version see Beckh's catalogue of the Bka' 'gyur, Beckh_1914: p. 102 (Mdo 13, foll. 194.sqq., 14 foll. sqq). In the Sanskrit text, as edited by Nobel_1944-50, the chapters are VIII and IX.
(c) 016.j (IOL Tib M 647), fragmentary at l. and r.
(1) Beginning (verso, 1) of- 'Phags pa Bar du gcod pa thams cad sel ba'i gzungs, Arya sarba antara saM grA sa dha ra Ni mantra (Ārya-Sarvāntarasaṃgrāsa-dhāraṇī).
For the work see Beckh's catalogue of the Bka' 'gyur, Beckh_1914: p. 116 (Rgyud, 15, fol. 101).
(3) Beginning (verso, 8) of- 'Phags pa Spyan ras gzigs dbang phyug gi gzungs, Arya A ba lo ki te shva ra nA ma dhA ra Ni (Ārya-Avalokiteśvara-nāma-dhāraṇī).
A work of this name is given by Beckh_1914: p. 124 (Bka' 'gyur, Rgyud, 17, fol. 13).
(d) 016.w (IOL Tib M 648), fragmentary at r. and elsewhere; marginal number partly lost, recto 5 (Chinese) and Sdud ba gong, Sdud ba a, verso thu drug, Thu '6'.
(e) E.G.019.a (IOL Tib M 650), nearly complete: marginal number and title, recto 'a gya bzhi Bcings grol gong, 'a, '84a' Bcings grol, verso similarly 'a, 84b' (also Chinese).
End (recto, 4) of - 'Phags pa G.yul las rgyal ba zhes bya ba'i gzungs (Ārya-samara-vijaya-nāma-dhāraṇī) and beginning (recto, 4) of - Lus ngag yid gsum brtsings (bcings) pa las rab tu rgyal bar byed pa zhes bya ba'i gzungs, KayA ba kicipra ta stambha na bi dza yA dhA ra Ni (Kayā-vāk-citta-stambhana-vijaya-dhāraṇī).
(f) 024.a (IOL Tib M 685)
Fragmentary in middle; marginal numbers and titles, recto, pho brgya re drug bkra shis gong 'Pho, 166a., Bkra shis', verso, 'Pho 166b. Span bla cho ga.
End (recto, 6) of a collection of Maṅgalas (bkra shis) addressed to various divinities.
L. 1, reference to Indian and Chinese translators (rgya bod lo tabsha) and the year shing pho stag ('Wood tiger', 48th of the Sixty years cycle).
Beginning (recto, 7) of - De bzhin gshegs pa bdun gyi sngon gyi smon lam gyi khyad par rgyas pa'i gzungs klag pa'i cho ga 'Method of reading the specially expansive former vows of the Seven Tathāgatas' stated to come from the Mdo sde, (Sūtra-piṭaka).
Begins (after salutation to the Seven Tathāgatas) - (Sapta-tathāgata-pūrva-praṇidhāna-viśeṣa-vistāra-dhāraṇī-vācana-vidhi, cf. Cordier_1909_1915: I (II), p. 369, Rgyud, 69. foll. 349-)
Perhaps deals with work cited by Beckh_1914: p. 96. Rgyud, Vol. 11, foll. 333. sqq., under the title Ārya-Sapta-Tathāgata-pūrva-praṇidhāna-viśeṣa-vistara-nāma-mahāyāna-sūtra
(g) 024.b (IOL Tib M 689), fragmentary at l.
Apparently Tāntrik ritual, with a story of a Vasuta and a Garuḍa.
(h) 024.c (IOL Tib M 690)
Fragmentary at l.
From a Buddhist Sūtra.
Refers to Ānanda (l. 3), a stag cho ra ma can gyi nags khrod (Vyāghra āraṇya, l. 3), certain flowers ka ya tsa dur, (Kaya-catura?) etc., and to creatures who had become tigresses (recto, 2) and are converted ? apparently the conclusion of the story.
