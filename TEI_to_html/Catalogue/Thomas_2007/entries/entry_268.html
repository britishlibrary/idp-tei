E.G.019.d (IOL Tib M 653), e (IOL Tib M 654), s (IOL Tib M 667), pp (IOL Tib M 684), parts of foll. 4 from one or more copies of a collectaneous work: ll. 6 recto + 6 verso of ordinary dbu can xylograph, very black ink; border lines; marginal numbers (smaller type), recto number and title at l., the same in Chinese at r., verso number only (in Chinese also).
(a) 019.d (IOL Tib M 653), marginal number and title 'Tha 13, 'Phags pa Stobs po che', verso 'tha, 13'.
Rig sngags kyi rgyal po stobs po che'i snying po
Dialogue between Buddha, Vajrapāni and Subhūti: cf. Beckh_1914: p. 129, Rgyud 18, foll. 163-, Mahābala-nāma-mahāyāna-sūtra: cf. E.G.019.ii (IOL Tib M 680).
(b) 019.e (IOL Tib M 654), marginal number and title, recto 'Ri, 4, Nor bdag Ku ber', verso 'Ri, 4' (also in Chinese).
End of - (recto, 1) gnod sbyin rnams kyi gzungs (Yakṣa-dhāraṇī, with wpecial reference to Kuvera?).
(c) 019.s, fragmentary at r.: marginal number and title, recto, 'Di, 9, Khro bo phyir zlog pa', verso, 'Di, 9' (Chinese number nearly all lost). From a Phyir bzlog pa ngan sngags kyi 'khor lo (verso, 3) (Pratyaṅgirā-mantra-cakra).
(d) 019.pp, fragmentary at r.; Chinese number at r., and title.
