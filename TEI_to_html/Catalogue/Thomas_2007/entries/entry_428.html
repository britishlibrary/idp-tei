IOL Tib M 952

Section: 1
A nearly complete fol.: ll. 8 recto + 8 verso of elegant dbu med script, small size, with l. 1 recto in a minute hand; marginal number, Ka nyi shu(?), 'vol.(?) I, 20'.
From a ritual work mentioning a Tong pa (recto, 5), a black person seen in a dream (5), a peacock umbrella (verso, 1), a prince (2-3), and a prayer for a meeting in a later birth (6): perhaps also 'a great pandit' (pan pi ta chen po, 8).
