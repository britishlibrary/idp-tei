E.G.019.qq (IOL Tib M 108), ss (IOL Tib M 110); 024 (IOL Tib M 125). Fragments, different sizes from one or more copies: ll. 8 recto + 8 verso of dbu can xylograph, ordinary size, well spaced; border lines.
(a) 019.qq (IOL Tib M 108). Verses concerning royalty and the state.
recto, 1 rgyal po yul rnams na/
/nyes byed yal bar 'dor bar tshe/
verso, 1 /yul na 'jigs pa mi zad pa/
/de 'dra gzhan du yod ma yin/
(b) 019.ss (IOL Tib M 110). Dialogue between Buddha and a Devī (verso, 8). Speaks (a) of various things, such as fine clothes, food, gold, dies, jewels, etc., and refers (verso, 6) to the Suvarṇa-prabhāsa-uttama-sūtrendra-rāja.
(c) 024 (IOL Tib M 125). From a Buddhist sūtra: mentions (recto, 6) 'all the pāramitās', (verso, 2) 'high royal banner', (recto, 8), 'king's special brilliance'.
