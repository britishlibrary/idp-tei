Fragmentary at r.and l.: ll. 8 recto + 8 verso of good, rather flowing, dbu med script, of medium size; interlinear glosses in a minutu hand od similar type.
A commentary upon a work in verse concerning the service (sten) of Kalyāṇa-mitras, etc.: cf. Stein_1928: Pl. CXXXIII and p. 1089 and Beckh_1914: p. 62, Mdo, 27, foll. 379 sqq., Kalyāṇa-mītra-sevana-sūtra.
Begins - ldan bshes gnyen rab tu sten byed za bring min /zhes bya ba sten ba'i gnas yin te chos kyi bgo skal la spyod pa la brten nas/...
The further comments deal with the phrases -
recto, 3, chos sgo rjes 'gro phyi dang
recto, 4, nga rgyal dang bcas pa dang nga rgyal med pa'i yid dang ldan bas ...
recto, 5, verse 7, rang gi sems kyis bshes gnyen mchog sten pa chos la mos...
recto, 6, brten ba'i tshangs pa mi 'thun spangs /rnal par mi rtog...
verso, 1, /bsten ba rten dang gzhi dang ni
/rgyu mtshan dang ni yongs snga do
/rgyu dang ye shes zh[e]...
