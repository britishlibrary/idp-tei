Fragmentary at r.: ll. 6 recto + 6 verso of rather neat dbu can xylograph; in margin recto ca chos spyod.. bu cu gnyis and dkod, 'Vol. (?) V, chos spyod, 12', verso the fol. number bcu gnyis 'og, '12b'.
A poem addressed to Buddha, a fragment: verse of seven syllables (cf. 016.n (IOL Tib M 18)).
Begins - /rten 'byung mthong ba legs par sngags/
/de yang kun tu med pa dang/
/rang bhzhin gyis ni yod bas min/
/ltos med nam mkha'i me tog bzhin//
The marginal mchod spyod is perhaps not a title: cf. spyod (016.b (IOL Tib M 11)) and chos spyod (016.e (IOL Tib M 12), etc.)
