Section: 1
E.G.016.a.xii (IOL Tib M 859); 016.ff (IOL Tib M 789); 017.b (IOL Tib M 787), g (IOL Tib M 783), k (IOL Tib M 784), m (IOL Tib M 857); 023.a.xv (IOL Tib M 788) and xvi (IOL Tib M 1000)
Five fragments of probably several volumes: ll. 5 recto + 6 verso of normal dbu can xylograph; border lines.
(1) 016.a.xii (IOL Tib M 859), mentions the '75 lords' (recto, 4), 'black horse' (recto, 2), 'enemy' (verso, 3). No doubt Tāntrik ritual
(2) 016.ff (IOL Tib M 789), marginal title and number Chos spyod brgya bcu(?) bzhi, 'Chos spyod 114'.
From a 'Chos spyod' (Dharma-caryā).
Mentions the daśa-śīla (recto, 5), Bka' 'dams (recto, 6), and prayer to 'Bebs(?) mkhas Bskal bzang rgya mtsho.
On the quasi title Chos spyod see under 016.e (IOL Tib M 12).
(3) 017.b (IOL Tib M 787). Ritual. Recto, 5 has a colophon mentioning a blessing (maṅgala) composed by Bha va chen Khyung po?
(4) 017.g (IOL Tib M 783), fragment, much smudged. Prayers etc. Mentions (verso, 5) Mkhas grub Chos kyi rgyal po Nor bzang rgya mtsho
(5) 017.k (IOL Tib M 784), a left-hand fragment; marginal title and number Rnam sras lnga, 'Rnam sras 5'.
Ritual, perhaps connected with Vaiśravaṇa. Mentions amṛta (recto, 2), mudrā (recto, 4), siddhi (recto, 5), vajra-dhara (recto, 6), nātha (mgon po) (recto, 5, verso, 2, 4), ne'u le (verso, 5).
(6) 017.m (IOL Tib M 857), a left-hand fragment; marginal title and number [Chos] spyod brgya nyer gsum, 'Chos spyod, 123'
A Chos spyod (Dharma-caryā)
(7) 023.a.xv (IOL Tib M 788), mentions 'black Kubera' (recto, 2), Yakṣa (recto, 4), nātha (mgon po) (verso, 2), Vaiśravaṇa (verso, 4).
(8) 023.a.xvi (IOL Tib M 1000), blurred, l. 6 lost. Mentions ne'u le (recto, 3), kuṇḍalinī (recto, 2), siṃhāsana (verso, 3)
