A right-hand fragment: ll. 6 recto + 6 verso of very bad dbu can xylograph, sub-normal size; border lines.
End of a work in verse by Btsun pa Blo bzang chos kyi rgyal mtshan (Sumati-dharma-dhvaja, the XIth Pan chen of Bkra shis lhun po, 1569-1662 A.D.), with colophon by the scribe snyan ngag mkhanTshangs sras bzhan pa'i rdo rje, who refers to Blo ldan bskal bzang rgya mtsho (the VIIth Dalai Lama, 1705-1758 A.D.)'s kalyāṇa-mitra.
