IOL Tib M 760

Section: 1
A practically complete fol: ll. 2 recto + 6 verso of ordinary dbu can xylograph, smallish size; marginal number gcig, '1'.
Khyab bdag rdo rje 'chang chen po Ta 'i kau shrI btsan po Smin grol no mon han gyi sprul ba'i sku rin po che gang 'di nyid kyi sku skyes pa'i rab las gtso bor brtsams te gsol 'debs shing brtan
A prayer relating to the great Rdo rje 'chang (Vajradhara) Ta 'i kau Srii btsan po, the No mon han of the Smin grol monastry, (perhaps not that in Tibet (Waddell_1895: 277), but a namesake in Mongolia) (cf. 022.z (IOL Tib M 169)) and his reincarnations.
Begins - OM swa sti/gdong nas rnam par dag pa chos sku'i mkhar/lhan grub mtshan dpe'i gzi byin 'bar ba'i nyi
