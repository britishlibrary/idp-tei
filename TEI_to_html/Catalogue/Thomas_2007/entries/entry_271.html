IOL Tib M 663

Section: 1
A practically complete fol.: ll. 7 recto + verso of good, rather pointed dbu can xylograph; border lines; marginal numbers recto, kha rma chen po nyer gnyis, 'vol.(?) Kha, Rma chen po, 22' (also Chinese), verso Chinese.
From the Rig Sngags ki rgyal mo rma bya chen mo (Vidyā-rājñī-Mahā-Māyūrī).
On this work see the article by M. Sylvain LŽvi in Journal Asiatique, (Beckh_1914: pp. 102-3, Rgyud, 14, foll. 25.a. sqq).
The fol. gives part of the list of Yakṣas.
