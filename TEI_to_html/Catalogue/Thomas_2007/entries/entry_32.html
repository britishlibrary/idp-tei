(a) Da na pa ti (Sanskrit Dā(Dha?)-na-pa-ti)
Phyugs nad thams cad rab tu zhi bar byed pa'i gzhungs, 'Dhāraṇī allaying all cattle diseases', in the heading and colophon entitled ParNa sha ba ri'i phyugs nad zhi bar byed pa'i gzungs and ParNa sha ba ri'i gnag gzungs dang phyugs gzungs.
Parṇaśabarī ... dhāraṇī.
'A dhāraṇī to Parṇaśabarī allaying all cattle diseases': cf. Beckh_1914: p. 104, Rgyud 14, fol. 259, and Cordier_1909_1915: II:13, Cordier_1909_1915: III:47, Rgyud 71, foll. 50, 233.
Begings (a. 1) - /lha mo parNa sha ba ri la phayg 'tshal lo//'di ni phyugs kyi lha yin te//phyugs ma rnams la bsrung bar mdzod/...
