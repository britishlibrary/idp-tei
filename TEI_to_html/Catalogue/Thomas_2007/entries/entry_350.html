Section: 1
E.G.016.a.xiii (IOL Tib M 776), xiv (IOL Tib M 775), ll. 5 recto + 5 verso of normal dbu can xylograph; marginal number, in xiii, '3'.
The two foll. are consecutive; but as they were originally folded in concertina form, the text verso may be different from that exemplified recto.
Recto: Me long ye shes kyi tshigs bcad rkang pa dang bcas bcu (Darpaṇa-jñāna-kārikā, pāda-sahita daśa?)
Mentions the gaṇḍī (xiii, recto, 3).
xiv, recto, 1, - praise of Mañjughoṣa - /'jam dbyangs chen po sgra che pa/
/'jig rten gsum na sgra gcig pa/
/nam mkha'i mtha' klas sgra sgros pa/
/sgra dang ldan pa rnams kyi mcho ba (mchog)/
verso: mentions various diseases and divinities: also Siṭātapatra, vajra-uṣṇīṣa-mahā-pratyaṅgirā (xiii, verso, 6).
