Ll. 5 of dbu can xylograph, ll. 3 (part)-5 of smaller size.
Mentions Smin grol [gling] monastery. l. 3 (cf.016.jj), Phyogs las rnam rgyal dpal bzang po and gzims chung (l. 5), i.e. Dig-vijaya-śrī-bhadra (a person, or monastery?) and 'abode' ('retreat').
The Smin grol gling monastery may be that described by Waddell_1895: 277.
