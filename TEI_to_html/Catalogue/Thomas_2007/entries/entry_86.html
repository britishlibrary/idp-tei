IOL Tib M 100

Section: 1
A small fragment: ll. 8 recto + 8 verso of good xylograph: border lines: marginal numbers, recto, 1, re gcig gong, '61a', verso - (only Chinese 61b survives).
Mentions the 500 cities of the Nāgas (recto, l. 2) and the 'royal caste', 'noble caste', 'people caste', 'Caṇḍāla caste' (rgyal rigs, rje rigs, dmangs rigs, gdol rigs).
