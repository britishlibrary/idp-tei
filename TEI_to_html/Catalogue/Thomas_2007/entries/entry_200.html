E.G.024.a.x-xiii (IOL Tib M 266-269). Four right-hand fragments: ll. 6 recto + 6 verso of fair dbu can xylograph, normal size; border lines; marginal numbers in Chinese at r.
E.G.024.a.x (IOL Tib M 266) marginal number '67 a and b'.
E.G.024.a.xi (IOL Tib M 267) marginal number '68 a and b'.
E.G.024.a.xii (IOL Tib M 268) marginal number '69 a and b'.
E.G.024.a.xiii (IOL Tib M 269) marginal number '66 a and b'.
From a stotra or strotras, addressed perhaps to a 'Jam dbyangs Chos rje (52, verso, l. 4; 53, recto, 6).
Mentions Dignāga (50, verso, 2), Blo bzang dge 'dun grub (51, verso, 6), the predecessor (1391-1478 A.D.) of the Dalai Lamas (?), Nāgārjuna (53, verso, 4).
Perhaps the title was (51, verso, 3) - Mi dbang gtsug gi nor bu byams chen chos kyi rje ShAkya ye... (Narendra-śikhā-maṇi-mahā-maitrī-dharma-rāja-śākya-ye-śes....).
