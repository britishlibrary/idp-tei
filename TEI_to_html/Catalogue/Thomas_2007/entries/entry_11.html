Section: 1
Somewhat fragmentary in margin, writing space complete, discoloured: ll. 6 recto + 6 verso of ordinary, square, dbu can xylograph: in margin spy[o]d, zhe brgyad, 'Spyod, 48'.
Apparently a prayer in verse (gsol ba 'debs pa'i tshigs su bcad pa) addressed to Tsong kha pa under his personal name, Blo bzang grags pa'i dpal.
Begins - /tshig spyi'i bsad sgrub rtags kyi dge slong gis//thub bstan ming tsam kyang ma bphyung [pa]'i dus// bslab gsum lung rtogs rgyal ba'i dam chos kun//khyod kyis nyin mod mdzad la gsol ba 'debs/
Verso, 5- /thub bstan phyogs dus kun tu rgyas mdzad pa'i//blo bzang grags pa'i dpal gyis bkra shis shog//ces rgyal ba thams cad kyi mkhyen brtse gcig tu bsdus pa'i ngo bo/lnga brgya me rgyu(?) mas kyi gtsug [-]i [-]o bya/dpal ldan bla ma dam pa rje btsun pa cab pa chen pa gnyed kyi rnam par pa mdo tsam zhig brjed pa'i gro nas gsol ba 'debs pa'i tshigs su bcad pa 'di na//sde grong nas dge sbyor sdom brtson ...
