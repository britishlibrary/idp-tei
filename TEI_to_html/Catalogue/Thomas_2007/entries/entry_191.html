A left-hand fragment: ll. 6 recto + 6 verso of good dbu can xylograph: border lines: marginal title and number, recto ca chos spyod brgya zhes bdun gong, i.e. 'vol.(?) V, chos spyod, 147a' verso '147b'.
From a Chos spyod (Dharma-caryā). Salutations to various Tathāgatas, etc. The text may be akin to that in 016.p (IOL Tib M 21).
