A medical work in verse.
recto, 1 - /khyi lce [d]kar po bsdungs la gtang/
/lce chung ba tha rkang (read hang?) theb kyi/
/spu nag [steng du ]...btsal bsdig/
/des kyang 'phan b[de] ma gyur na.../
The use of a 'dog's tongue, white', or 'great toe' (or 'thumb', theb), a 'black hair' (spu nag), and also the suggestion of a further remedy in case of failure ('phan bde ma gyur na), recur in such literature.
