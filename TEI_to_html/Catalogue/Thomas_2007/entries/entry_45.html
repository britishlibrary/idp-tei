IOL Tib M 59

Section: 1
Fragmentary at r.: ll. 5 recto + 5 verso of excellent, neat, dbu med script, of rather large size; interlinear notes in a rather minute hand.
A ritual text, referring also to a Rdo rje'i glu (Vajra-gītā).
The MS. has been described in Stein_1928: 1089 (see also Stein_1928: Plate CXXXII), where see quotation of beginning.
The verso begins -
ma ra ga ta lta bu /phyag g-yas na sbrul /g-yon na thod pa bsnams shing drag po mnan pa lta bu blta' bar bya 'o /dgra sta bsnams shing /gshin rje mnan pa rnam par bsam mo /
