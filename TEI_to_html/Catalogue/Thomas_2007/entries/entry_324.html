IOL Tib M 740

Section: 1
A nearly complete fol.: ll. 7 recto + 7 verso of neat dbu med script of a late type, small size; marginal number dgu, '9'.
recto, 1 /nga dud gro dang/yi dwags nyid dang myal bar skye ba dang/rgyal ba'i bka' med pa dang mtha' 'khob tu /kla klor skyes dang glen gling lkugs pa nyid/
'[May] I [escape from] birth as a beast or a wild animal or an infernal being or one without Buddhas Word or a foreign dwarf (mtha' 'khob), a Muslim (kla klo), from mute stupidity (glen gling lkugs pa nyid), etc., etc.'
Refers apparently to states of rebirth, and mentions Tibet as a religious country, -
Bod 'di chos tshig gi dbang du byas pa'i yul dbus (verso, 2).
