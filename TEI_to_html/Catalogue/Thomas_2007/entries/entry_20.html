IOL Tib M 21

Section: 1
Fragmentary at r. and at bottom ll. 6: recto + 6 verso of ordinary dbu can xylograph; in margin chos spyod and the fol. number so drug, 36.
Chos spyod (Dharma-caryā), partly in verses of nine syllables.
In l. 4 recto ends a colophon 'translated by Dge 'dun ryga mtsho'i dpal' (the IInd Dalai Lama (1479-1541 A.D.)?
In l. 5 verso is a mention of Tshul khrims rgya mtsho, the Xth Dalai Lama (1817-1837 A.D.)?
The new section, recto l. 4, begins - //mkha' mnyam 'gro la mkhye...mi mdzad zhing gi dpal gyur ba/ followed by namaskāras to Sākya-muni, Nāgārjuna, etc.
Concerning the quasi title Chos spyod see under 016.e (IOL Tib M 12), where the text is somewhat similar; perhaps also in 016.q (IOL Tib M 255).
