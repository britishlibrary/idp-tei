UNKNOWN PHOTOGRAPHER, Kabul in the 1920s: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of War, The Lycée Habibullah, the Persian Legation.
The buildings constructed during Amanullah’s reign (1919–29) embody his attempts to reform and modernize Afghanistan. Taken by an unknown photographer, on the top left is the Stor palace (now the Ministry of Foreign Affairs); top right is the Ministry of War (since demolished); bottom left is the Habibia Lycee (partly demolished) and bottom right is the Persian legation (demolished).
Collotype; British Library.
