JOHN BURKE, Jalalabad, the Peshawar Gate and surroundings, 1879.
Although the large timber doors at the Peshawar gate, on the east side of the walled city of Jalalabad, seem to be intact, the mud walls themselves seem to be in a poor state of repair in this photograph from 1879.
Albumen print, British Library.
