JAMES RATTRAY, Oosbegs of Moorand Bey, 1841
Rattray identifies these Uzbek men as Mirza Abdulluq and Rustum Beg, whose chief was the Mir of Qunduz. Rattray sketched this pair in the Laghman fort in August 1841.
Lithograph from James Rattray, Scenery, Inhabitants and Costumes, Afghaunistan (London, 1848), British Library.
