BENJAMIN SIMPSON, Baba Wali Kotal showing position of Ayoub’s guns, Kandahar, 1881.
View towards the pass of Baba Wali Kotal, to the west of Qandahar city, on the far side of which Ayub Khan’s camp was located. After his victory over the British at the Battle of Maiwand, Ayub Khan besieged the British garrison at Qandahar. His defeat in September 1880 marked the end of the Second Afghan War.
Albumen print; British Library.
