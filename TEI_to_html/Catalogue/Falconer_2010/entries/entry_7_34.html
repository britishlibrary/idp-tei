BENGAL SAPPERS AND MINERS, Official in the machine yard of a Kabul workshop, 1879–80.
This photograph shows a member of Amir Sher Ali’s court seated beside some machinery that seems to be in the process of being installed. The photograph might have been taken during the construction of the ammunition factories close to Andarabi.
Albumen print; British Library.
