CLARENCE COMYN TAYLOR, Afghan Sirdars at Lord Mayo’s Durbar, Ambala, March 1869.
This photograph of members of the Afghan delegation at the Ambala durbar was taken by an Indian Army officer, Clarence Taylor, who was at that time Lord Mayo’s aide de camp, and was an enthusiastic amateur photographer.
Albumen print, British Library.
