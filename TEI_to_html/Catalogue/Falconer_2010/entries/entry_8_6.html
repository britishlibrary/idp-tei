BENJAMIN SIMPSON, Chilzina and old Kandahar from Picquet Hill, 1880–81.
Taken from a hill west of modern Qandahar, this view looks south towards the old city at the far end of the ridge in the distance. The Chilzina, or ‘Forty Steps’, are situated at the foot of the nearer end of the ridge.
Albumen print; British Library.
