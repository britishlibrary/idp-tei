BAKER AND BURKE, Amir Shir Ali Khan, with Prince Abdullah Jan and Sirdars, Jamrud, April 1869.
In this group, Sher Ali is seated in the centre of his sirdars, with his son Abdullah Jan standing beside him. The boy seated on the right in the front row is the young Nasrullah Khan, who was later briefly proclaimed king after the assassination of Amir Habibullah in 1919.
Albumen print, British Library.
