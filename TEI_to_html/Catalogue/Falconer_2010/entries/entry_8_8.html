BENJAMIN SIMPSON, Chilzina, or the Forty Steps, Kandahar, 1880–81.
Vendors of fruit seated in front of the ridge on which lies the rock-cut chamber known as Chilzina or Chehel Zina – ‘forty steps’ – which the Emperor Babur caused to be excavated and which contains inscriptions describing his conquests.
Albumen print; British Library.
