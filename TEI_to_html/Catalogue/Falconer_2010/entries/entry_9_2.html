JOHN BURKE, Amir Abdur Rahman’s escort of Uzbek Cavalry, Rawalpindi, April 1885.
Amir Abdur Rahman was accompanied to Rawalpindi by an escort of over 2,000 men, including his own Uzbek Cavalry. As Rudyard Kipling put it, ‘The Usbeg lancers, in their mustard-hued coats, shaggy caps and strange accoutrements, would make an artist’s fortune’.
Albumen print; British Library.
