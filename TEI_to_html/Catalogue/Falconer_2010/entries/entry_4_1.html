EMILY EDEN, Dost Muhammad Khan with members of his family, at Barrackpore, near Calcutta, 1841.
This watercolour was made by Emily Eden when Dost Muhammad was a guest of her brother, the Governor-General of India Lord Auckland, in June 1841. The other figures in the group are Haidar Khan, former Governor of Ghazni, Muhammad Akram Khan and Abdul Ghani Khan.
