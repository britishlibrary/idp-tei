JAMES ATKINSON, The Cabaul Cantonments from the bridge, near the fort of Mahmood Khan Byab, 1840.
This sketch shows the British cantonment in the Kabul plain, viewed from the bank of the Logar river, to the east of the citadel of Bala Hissar.
Watercolour, British Library.
