JOHN BURKE, Major Cavagnari and Chief Sirdars, Jalalabad, 1879.
Photographed in Jalalabad in early 1879, Cavagnari is seen here with prominent local leaders: beside him on the left is the powerful chief, Kunar Syed. On his other side is Sirdar Kala Khan, who acted as an emissary between Yaqub Khan and the British in the lead-up to the Treaty of Gandamak.
Albumen print, British Library.
