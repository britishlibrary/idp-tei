JOHN BURKE, The Upper Bala Hissar from the gateway above the British Residency, Kabul, 1880.
An encampment of Afghan soldiers in the lower enclosure of the Bala Hissar, with the ramp and gate to the upper enclosure on the right. Dating from the Mughal era, this gate was originally arched and decorated with tilework, hence its name Darwaza Kashi.
Albumen print, British Library.
