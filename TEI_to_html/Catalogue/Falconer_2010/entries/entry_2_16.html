JAMES RATTRAY, Meerze Fyze, an Oosbeg Elchee or ambassador, c. 1841-2.
Rattray sketched the figure of Mirza Faiz in conversation with a British officer in local dress, at Laghman fort.
Lithograph from James Rattray, Scenery, Inhabitants and Costumes, Afghaunistan (London, 1848), British Library.
