JAMES RATTRAY, Atmaran, Prime Minister of Koonduz, 1841.
Originally from Peshawar, the highly-talented Atmaran served as Chief Minister to Murud Beg, Mir of Qunduz. This portrait, made on the roof of a traditional courtyard house in Kabul, was made by Rattray in September 1841.
Lithograph from James Rattray, Scenery, Inhabitants and Costumes, Afghaunistan (London, 1848), British Library.
