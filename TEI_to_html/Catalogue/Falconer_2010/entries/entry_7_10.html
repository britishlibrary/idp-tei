BENGAL SAPPERS AND MINERS, Close view of the ruined British Residency in the Bala Hissar, Kabul, 1879–80.
As a result of the attack on the Residency in 1879, British troops re-occupied Kabul as the conflict intensified. Here, photographed from inside the courtyard, one can see the burnt-out remains of Cavagnari’s living quarters in the Residency.
Albumen print, British Library.
