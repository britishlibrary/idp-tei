JAMES ATKINSON, The Balla Hissar, Cabaul, 1840.
This sketch of a family group on the roof of a house within the lower enclosure of the Bala Hissar shows part of the gardens around the royal palace, with a mosque that was dedicated by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan on the right.
Watercolour, British Library
