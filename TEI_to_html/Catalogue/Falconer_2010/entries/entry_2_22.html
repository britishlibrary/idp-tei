JAMES RATTRAY, Kandahar lady of rank, engaged in smoking, c. 1841-2.
This portrait of a Barakzai lady shows her seated in family quarters of a wealthy home in Qandahar, with elaborate plaster decoration. The tray of fruit in the foreground is testament to the rich produce cultivated in the fields that can be seen through the open window.
Lithograph from James Rattray, Scenery, Inhabitants and Costumes, Afghaunistan (London, 1848), British Librar.
