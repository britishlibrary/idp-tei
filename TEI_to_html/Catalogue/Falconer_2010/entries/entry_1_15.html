CHARLES MASSON, Surkh Pol over the Surkh Rud between Gandamak and Jigdillik, built according to inscriptions by Ali Merdan Khan, c. 1828.
Jigdillik, built according to inscriptions by Ali Merdan Khan, c. 1828. Masson visited and sketched the Surkh Pol or Red Bridge a few marches on from his visit to Bala Bagh. Like his drawing of the town, this may have been made during his first visit to Afghanistan in 1828.
Pen and ink drawing; British Library.
