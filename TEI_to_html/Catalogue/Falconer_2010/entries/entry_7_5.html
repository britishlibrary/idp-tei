JOHN BURKE, Panorama of Kabul from Siah Sang, 1879–80.
Burke’s panoramic view from the summit of Siah Sang to the east of the city shows the sweep of the Kabul valley. On the left is the Bala Hissar, with a British military encampment in the foreground and the cantonment at Sherpur on the right.
Albumen print, British Library
