JOHN BURKE, Babur Shah’s Masjid, Kabul, 1879–80.
The white marble mosque built in 1676 by Shah Jahan, Babur’s great-great-grandson, to mark his conquest of Balkh and Badakhshan, is seen here in poor condition. It was restored in 2004–6, as part of a wider rehabilitation programme of the garden.
Albumen print; British Library.
