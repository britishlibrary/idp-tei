BAKER AND BURKE, Amir Shir Ali Khan at Jamrud, April 1869.
On his return to Afghanistan after the Ambala durbar, Sher Ali gave permission for the professional photographers Baker and Burke to take a further series of sixteen portraits of his entourage at Jamrud. This is one of several studies of Sher Ali, which became commercially available as a result of these sessions.
Albumen print, British Library.
