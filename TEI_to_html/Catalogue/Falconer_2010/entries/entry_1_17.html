Section: 1
CHARLES MASSON, The Kandahar [or Kabul?] Gate of the Citadel, Ghizni, 1832.
Although Masson suggests that this drawing is of the Qandahar gate in the citadel of Ghazni, it seems in fact to be of the Kabul gate. At the time of his visit, clearly a great deal more of the stone fortifications were standing than is the case today.
Pen and ink drawing; British Library.
