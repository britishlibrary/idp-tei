The ruins of the British Residency, Kabul, 1880.
Following the Treaty of Gandamak, Sir Pierre Louis Cavagnari arrived in Kabul to take up his post as British Resident on 24th July 1879. Only a month later, the Residency was attacked by rebellious Afghan soldiers and all occupants killed. The damage caused to the Residency can be clearly seen on this photograph.
Albumen print, British Library.
