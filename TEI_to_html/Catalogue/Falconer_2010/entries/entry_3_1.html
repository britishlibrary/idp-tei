JAMES ATKINSON, Jellalabad from the north near the Cabaul River, 1840.
This is one of preliminary sketches from which Atkinson had intended to produce a second series of views of Afghanistan. It shows a group of travelers making their way through the valley of the Kabul river, north of Jalalabad.
Watercolour, British Library.
