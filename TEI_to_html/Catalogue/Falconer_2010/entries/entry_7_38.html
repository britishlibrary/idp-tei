JOHN BURKE, Murad Khan and other chiefs at Kabul, 1880.
Between 1878 and 1880, Burke produced a series of portraits which form the first systematic photographic record of individuals, social groups and occupational types. Here we see Murad Khan and other elders of Kabul, with a tray of tea.
Albumen print; British Library.
