CLARENCE COMYN TAYLOR, Prince Abdullah Jan at Ambala, March 1869.
Sher Ali’s second son Abdullah Jan would have been aged about seven years old when he accompanied his father to Ambala. The major reason for his presence at the durbar was Sher Ali’s wish to gain British recognition of Abdullah Jan as his successor.
Albumen print, British Library.
