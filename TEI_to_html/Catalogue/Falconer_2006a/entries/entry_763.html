Kirghiz at Uch-Turfan. No. 426-431.
Group of six men. Numbers refer to Stein's anthropometric data. 'I...reached by May 8th the picturesquely situated small town of Uch-Turfan...I was able to use a brief stay here for interesting anthropometrical work among the Kirghiz, who have supplied so important an element to the racial composition of the present population of the Tarim Basin, especially in its north-western portion' (Ruins of Desert Cathay, vol. 2, p. 423).

Location:
China; Uch-Turfan; Xinjiang
