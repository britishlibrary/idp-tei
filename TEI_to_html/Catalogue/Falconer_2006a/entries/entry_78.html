Reception by Hindus at Bigil bridge, Yarkand.
Large group posed in front of square tower (pao-t'ai). 'Four miles outside Yarkand City, by the side of one of those Pao t'ais or brick-built square towers which along the main Chinese high roads in Turkestan mark the roughly measured distances of 10 Li (circ. two miles), the whole body of Hindus in Yarkand...gave me a solemn welcome...' (Ruins of Desert Cathay, i.130). Similar to print 78.

Location:
China; Shache; Xinjiang; Yarkand
