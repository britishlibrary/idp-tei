Ziarat of S. Jalaluddin Baghdadi, Kelpin.
The published version of this print makes no mention of this tomb being at Kelpin (Kalpin), and its placing at this point in the albums and in the published volumes suggests that it might was close to Kashgar. Kalpin is nearly two hundred miles north-east of Kashgar. PUBLISHED: 'Ruins of Desert Cathay', vol. 1, pl. 41: 'Muhammadan shrine and cemetery on road to Kashgar.'

Location:
China; Kashgar; Kashi; Xinjiang
