Shirin Dil Khan, etc. [Pitkhar.]
Group of Shirin-dil Khan and officers, posed in front of felt tent: '...I was soon met on my way by gallant old Colonel Shirin-dil Khan, who, mounted on a fine Badakhshi and in full uniform, had galloped ahead with a crowd of horsemen to receive me. He had been sent up from Badakhshan by Prince Nasrullah, brother of the Amir and General Commanding in the Oxus Provinces, to assure my safe passage through the Afghan territory on the Pamirs. After patiently waiting for me at Sarhad for over four weeks he now offered me the warmest of welcomes...' (Ruins of Desert Cathay, vol. 1, p. 65). Also in the group is Hakim Mansur Khan, Governor of Upper Wakhan.

Location:
North-West Frontier Province; Pakistan; Pitkhar
