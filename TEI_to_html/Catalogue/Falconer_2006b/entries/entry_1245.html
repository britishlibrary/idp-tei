Group at Ulugh-mazar. Ibrahim Beg, Chiang, self, Jasvant Singh, Lal Singh, Ram Singh.
PUBLISHED: 'Ruins of Desert Cathay', vol. 2, pl. 296: 'My companions and myself at Ulugh-mazar, in the desert north of Chira. From left to right, sitting: Chiang-ssu-yeh, myself with 'Dash', Rai Bahadur Lal Singh. Standing: Ibrahim Beg, Jasvant Singh, Naik Ram Singh.' 'Serindia', III, fig. 320, 'Group of my party taken at Ulugh-mazar. From left to right: Ibrahim Beg; Chiang Ssu-yeh; self with 'Dash II'; Jasvant Singh; Rai Bahadur Lal Singh; Naik Ram Singh. Dead tamarisk cone in background.' This is one of the best portraits of Stein with his assistants in the collection.

China; Ulugh-mazar; Xinjiang
