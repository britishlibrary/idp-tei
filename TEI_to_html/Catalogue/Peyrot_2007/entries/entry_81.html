H.149.292

Lines 2-4 of the verso contain a karmavācanā formula as in §84 of Härtel.
The recto is in Turkic, but not according to the normal spelling conventions for brāhmī. Line 7 of the verso is in Turkic, too, whereas line 5 of the verso is in Tocharian B. Although all margins are torn, at least the Turkic text on the recto is continuous − it was clearly written after the leaf was torn. Lines 1-4 of the verso written in a rather small hand, lines v5-6 in a rather large one, line v7 in between. Transliterations and transcriptions follow Maue (Maue_2008).
