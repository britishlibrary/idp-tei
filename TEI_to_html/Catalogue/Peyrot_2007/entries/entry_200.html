writing excercise
The character following "100" in line 3 of the recto is very difficult. Initially, I thought of a signature and transcribed "rghȗots" or "rźots". However, Dieter Maue suggested to me that it is another number sign, which fits very well with the numbers preceding, and he identified it with Sten Konow's Fremdzeichen number 8, which I have adopted below: "X₈" ("X8"). If "X₈" really were a number, though, we would never expect a vowel mark on it, nor a virāma stroke to the following "ts".
