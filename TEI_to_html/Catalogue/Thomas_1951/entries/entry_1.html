Ch.83.vi.5 (vol. 70, fol. 32: 27 x 7 cm.: ll. 3 of clear, rather small dbu can script).
[1]...Sha cu Rgya /Stong sar dang/Rgod sar stong sde gnyis kyi //glang gi lo dang/stagi lo'i/Drug chun gy[i] thag//-s [2]...Kwa cur 'dren bar chad nas//blon G.yu bzhre [note 1: Compendious for bzher, as often. [note gyi stsang /Kwa cu gzhi na mchis pa dang/Sha cu [3]....las//lnga bcu rkang//Khong Sban tse'i tshan/Wang Bun tsong la nas khal gcig/Cang...
"...Chinese Sha cu, Thousand districts of Stong sar and Rgod sar, Ox year and Tiger year, having been ordered to be conveyed to Kwa cu after being ground by the (?) mills of the Drug chun, from the corn of Councillor G.yu bzher, wich was on the Kwa cu estate, and from...Sha cu, fifty bundles (rkang). Account (tshan) of Khong Sban tse; to Wang Bun tsong one load of barely; [to] Cang..."
l. 1: Rgya, perhaps an error for rkya, "crop."
l. 1: on stong sde"district of 1000 (estates ?)" see above, p. 25 sqq. (ITJ 1253) and infra, pp. 315 sqq., vol. i, Thomas_1935: pp. 282-3.
Drug chun(g) = "Little Dru gu" (see pp. 30: M.I.xxviii.001, IOL Tib N 875, pp. 274-6: M.I.iv.57, Or.15000/312-316, M.I.iv.81, IOL Tib N 227, M.Tagh.a.vi.0031, IOL Tib N 1782).
l. 2: stsang: see. pp. 17 (Fragment.66, IOL Tib J 897), 19 (Fragment.67, IOL Tib J 1126), and Index.
l. 3: rkang, "bundle", as in p. 240 (M.Tagh.a.iii.0063, Or.15000/91); 92.A2 and elsewhere.
tshan"account": see p. 91 (Ch.73.viii.5, IOL Tib J 1357) below.
From this fragment it appears likely that the Sha cu division consisted mainly of two thousand-districts (stong sde), Stong sar and Rgod sar, those, in fact, of which we mostly hear elsewhere. But below (p. 45: Ch.79.xvi.7, IOL Tib J 914, p. 58: Ch.82, IOL Tib J 1018) we have Snying tsoms, Spyi tshogs and Tshas stobs.
The personal names will appear in the consolidated list (infra, pp. 113 sqq.).
