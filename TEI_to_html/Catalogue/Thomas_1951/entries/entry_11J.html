"By Councillor Ldong bzang, who is anxious, ordered to be despatched in haste to Spa zha. Whereas the Slungs'O ma Bu lud(ng?) and Snying tsoms people, Zing Rin cen and Bag bre Dpal 'ngud and 'Dza lda 'A-lum, these three, have been sent to Nob ched po, first and last several times even a reply by message tablet has not come."
On Slungs see supra p. 52 (M.I.xxviii.0036, Or.15000/496), and infra, pp. 296-7 (M.Tagh.a.iv.0092, IOL Tib N 1708; M.Tagh.a.vi.003, IOL Tib N 1755; M.I.xv.0020, IOL Tib N 749). The district Snying tsoms has already been mentioned. For the surnames Zing, Bag(Bgla?) bre, 'Dze('Je? 'Dzi?) lda no citations seem available. Spa zha also, as place-name (?), is apparently unknown. On Nob ched po see infra, pp. 115 sqq., and on phrin byang supra, p. 58 (IOL Tib J 999).
