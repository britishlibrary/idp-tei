The subject being similar to that of No. 2, and the persons partly the same, little comment is needed.
l. 4: The completion of the proper name is obvious from the receipt (verso).
l. 8: gthugs seems to belong to gtug pa, thug pa, "reach", "agree", and to mean "having compared"; cf. thug chad, "agreement", and de dang gtugs ching, p. 74.16 (IOL Tib J 1254).
