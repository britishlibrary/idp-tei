This catalogue covers 1251 loose prints (100x75mm) from the Stein Collection, Third Expedition, 1913-16. The series can be broken down chronologically as follows:

Prints 1-50 July-Aug 1913.

Prints 51-100 Aug 1913.

Prints 101-150 Aug-Sept 1913.

Prints 151-200 Sept 1913.

Prints 201-250 Sept-Nov 1913.

Prints 251-300 Nov-Dec 1913.

Prints 301-350 Dec 1913-Jan 1914.

Prints 351-400 Jan-Feb 1914.

Prints 401-450 Feb-Mar 1914.

Prints 451-500 Mar-Apr 1914.

Prints 501-550 Apr-Jun 1914.

Prints 551-600 Jun-Oct 1914.

Prints 601-650 Oct-Nov 1914.

Prints 651-700 Nov 1914-Jan 1915. Missing prints 666 and 694.

Prints 701-750 Jan-Mar 1915. Missing print 721.

Prints 751-800 Mar-May 1915.

Prints 801-850 May -? 1915.

Prints 851-900 1915.

Prints 901-950 1915.

Prints 951-1000 1915-16.

Prints 1001-1162 Dec 1915-Jan 1916.
