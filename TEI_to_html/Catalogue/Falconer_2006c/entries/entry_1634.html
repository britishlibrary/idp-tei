Fourth section of six-part panoramic view. The complete panorama comprises prints 135-140. PUBLISHED: 'A third journey of exploration...', fig. 5: 'Panoramic view taken from Karambar Saddle (circ. 14,050 feet above sea level) dividing headwaters of Karambar and Yarkhun, or Mastuj Rivers. The view extends from north-east, on extreme left (main Hindukush watershed towards Oxus), to west-south-west, on extreme right (uppermost Yarkhun Valley).' Topographical features also marked on the published version are: East Karambar Glacier, with bifurcating drainage; main Karambar Glacier feeding Yarkhun River; Glaciers above Murghach; peaks above Darkot and Chatiboi Glaciers.

Location:
Karambar Glacier; Karambar Pass; North-West Frontier Province; Pakistan
