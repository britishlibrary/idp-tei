Ustads at work, Chini-bagh [Kashgar].
Showing wooden crates for transporting artefacts in front of the British Consulate General at Kashgar. During this visit to Kashgar (31 May-6 July) Stein supervised the repacking of eighty heavy camel loads of material for the journey to India. (I.A.Map 2, D2).
