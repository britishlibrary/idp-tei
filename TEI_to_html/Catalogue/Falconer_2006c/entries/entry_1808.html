Photo 392/28(286)


Section: 1
Toghrak-mazar site from west.
General view of site, with diggers in background. Toghrak-mazar, not marked on map, is about a mile south-west of Kotaz-langar (I.A. Map 14, A2). Stein writes that he visited the Toghrak-mazar site on 29 November (i, 100), but his negative notebook records the date of this photograph as 28 November.

Location:
China; Toghrak-mazar; Xinjiang
