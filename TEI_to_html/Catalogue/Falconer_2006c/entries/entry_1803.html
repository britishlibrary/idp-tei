Section: 1
Rawak Stupa from west-north-west (Afrazgul).
Poor quality print. General view of stupa. Photograph taken by Afrazgul Khan: 'From Islamabad I detached Afraz-gul Khan to examine ruins reported in the desert east of the Yurung-kash river. His survey proved them to be identical with those of the Rawak and Jumbe-kum sites already explored by me in 1901 [see Ancient Khotan, fig.59]...' (i, 94). (I.A. Map 14, A2).

Location:
China; Rawak; Xinjiang
