Coalpits of Miao [Mou-wo] from north-east.
'There we found Lal Singh duly encamped by the side of one of the two wells dug in the dry bed passing below the coal-pits. Two or three houses half in ruins marked the quarters of the people from Chin-t'a and Mao-mei who until recent years used to come here for short periods in the winter to gather coal from the shallow diggings. Of this coal specimens were secured...The pits extend irregularly for about a mile to the west of the stream-bed and seem to be worked to a depth of 15 to 17 feet from the surface, in narrow ditches.' (i, 524). (I.A. Map 42, B3).

Location:
China; Gansu; Mou-wo
