Section: 1
Ak-bash's Town, west gate seen from north.
'Close to Akhtur-bazar, the large market centre of this tract, I was able to examine en route a ruined town site to which my attention had first been drawn by a reference in the Report of the Forsyth Mission of 1873-4. The 'town of Ak-bash Khan', as it is locally called, proved a walled enclosure of roughly quadrangular shape and manifestly of a comparatively late date...' (i, 65). (I.A. Map 5, A2).

Location:
Akhtur-bazar; China; Xinjiang
