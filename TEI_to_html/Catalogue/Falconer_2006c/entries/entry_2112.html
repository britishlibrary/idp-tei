Section: 1
Torgut Beili at Dashoba [should read 'at Tawun-tora'].
Full-length seated portrait, with two Begs standing behind. The caption on the reverse lists the photograph as having been taken at Dashoba, which Stein did not visit until the following day. Stein's negative notebook does not list the location, but it must have been at Tawun-tora, where the Beili visited Stein on 24 May. See note at print 505. PUBLISHED: 'Innermost Asia', vol. 1, fig. 227: 'Chief of Etsin-gol Torguts, with Mongol headmen.' (I.A. Map 45, C1).

Location:
China; Tawun-tora; Xinjiang
