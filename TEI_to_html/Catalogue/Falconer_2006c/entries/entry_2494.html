Awkward corner aboveTanimaz River, beyond Bashur Nullah, above Rukhj.
View looking along the path above the river. 'It thus became possible on August 12th to push on to the Bartang valley after a night spent at the small Tajik hamlet of Pasor...The track, difficult in places for laden animals, kept close to the river wherever the steep banks overhanging it left room, or else crossed high terraces. On one of these the small village of Rukhj was passed; its green fields presented a refreshing contrast to the utterly bare slopes around.' (ii, 853). PUBLISHED: 'Innermost Asia', vol. 2, fig. 369:' Crossing rock debris thrown down by earthquake near Palez, Tanimaz Valley.'

Location:
Palez; Tajikistan; Tanimaz (river)
