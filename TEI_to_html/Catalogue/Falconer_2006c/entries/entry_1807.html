Section: 1
Badruddin Khan at Tümür Shah Beg's house, Lop. [near Khotan.]
Full-length standing portrait. 'Lop' here presumably refers to Lop Bazar to the east of Khotan (I.A. Map 14, A2). Badruddin Khan, 'my ever-willing old friend', brought in for Stein a number of artefacts gathered from Yotkan and other desert sites around Khotan.

Location:
China; Lop-Bazar; Xinjiang
