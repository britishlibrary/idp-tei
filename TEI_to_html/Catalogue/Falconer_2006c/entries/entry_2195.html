Wadi with water ten miles north-west of Ta-hsi-kou (Musa).
Stein's guide identified Ta-hsi-k'ou as the same location of camp 215 (I.A.Map 37, D4). The discovery of 'wadi-like nullah', which contained 'deliciously fresh water at a depth of less than a foot', is described at i, 531. (I.A. Map 37, C3).
