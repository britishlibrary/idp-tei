T. III. Line of wall towards T. VII (Kabil). [South-east of Toghrak-bulak.]
View from hillside looking along the line of the wall, with Kabil standing in the foreground. PUBLISHED: 'Innermost Asia', vol. 1, fig. 191: 'Line of Limes wall stretching eastwards from watch-tower T.III.' (I.A. Map 35, C4-D4). 'Fig. 191 shows the stretch of the Limes wall where it descends from the gravel plateau near T.III and extends in very fair preservation for a distance of about three miles across the depression towards the next watch-tower T.VII.' (i, 344, note).

Location:
China; Dunhuang Limes; Toghrak-bulak (Su-lo-ho); Xinjiang
