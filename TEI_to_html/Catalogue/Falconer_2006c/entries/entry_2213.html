Bai, Eski-shahr, north gate and wall.
Showing portion of wall. This is presumably the fortified area examined by Afrazgul Khan on Stein's instructions: '...the ruined fort found close to the south-western end of the cultivated ground...was obviously meant to guard the approach from that side. From the report and the photographs brought by Afraz-gul, whom I sent for a preliminary inspection, it appeared manifestly Chinese and of no great age...' (i, 534). (I.A. Map 37, C2).

Location:
Bai; China; Xinjiang
