Ponies returning with baggage to [Ayub] Mirab's house [Maral-bashi].
While in Maral-bashi, Stein stayed in 'my old camping-place in Ayub Mirab's garden, east of the tumble-down Bazar that marks the town of Maral-bashi.' (i, 74). (I.A. Map 8, A1). This photograph would have been taken on his return from Lal-tagh.

Location:
China; Maral-bashi; Xinjiang
