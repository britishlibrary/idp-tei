Rafaks [pathway carried on terraces] on cliffs above river at Cenit, two miles above Sendiw.
'It was about two miles beyond Sendiw that we encountered the first and only serious obstacle to traffic within Shakh-dara. Here a very steep descent of some 200 feet had to be made over 'Rafaks' down to the left bank of the river, making it necessary to unload ponies.' (ii, 882)
