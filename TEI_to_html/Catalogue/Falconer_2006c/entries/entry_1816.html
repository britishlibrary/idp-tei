Belkum (Rawak) Qadim-shahr from south-south-east (Afrazgul).
View from above looking down onto site. This is possibly the 'remains of a previously unsurveyed structure, about 48 feet square, probably also a temple...its walls..almost eroded to the ground' (i, 94), which the shifting dunes had disclosed since Stein's visit in 1901. See also note at print 281. (I.A. Map 14, A2).
