Section: 1
Vash-shahri men on guard and my own at Pailu [Vash-shahri].
PUBLISHED: 'Innermost Asia', vol. 1, fig. 106: 'Men of Vash-shahri guarding route from Charchan.' 'After leaving Tatran we did not meet with a single wayfarer, which struck me as strange at the time and left me in doubts as to the situation we might find at Charkhlik. But when approaching on January 6th the jungle belt which from the west screens the ruined site of Vash-shahri...we found the route guarded by a large party of armed Muhammadans who at first from a distance mistook us for a fresh batch of 'revolutionaries' and were preparing to resist...Fortunately the mistake was promptly cleared up...' (i, 159). (I.A. Map 26, C3).

Location:
China; Vash-shahri; Xinjiang
