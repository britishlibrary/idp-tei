Lao-chun-miao and T. XL.c beyond Su-lo-ho defile.
View looking towards Su-lo-ho, with temple in left distance. PUBLISHED: 'Innermost Asia', vol. 1, fig. 213: 'Ruined temple on left river bank, Wan-shan-tzu defile.' (I.A. Map 40, A4). 'On the left bank of the river, at a point a little below our Camp 122, we found the mouth of a small Nullah that descends from the Ean-shan-tzu spur occupied by a ruined temple, well built and of recent appearance...Its name was subsequently given as Lao-chun-miao...It had apparently been destroyed during the Tungan rebellion.' (i, 374).

Location:
China; Dunhuang Limes; Lao-chun-miao; Xinjiang
