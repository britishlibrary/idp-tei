Ferokh-sangau Glacier, below Shitam Pass, from east.
General view of glacier, with horseman in foreground. ' According to the headman's statement the glacier had considerably advanced since his father's youth, and so also had the smaller glacier of Ferokh-sangau...which was passed at an elevation of some 1,700 feet below the snout of Shitok-lazar. It stretches down from WSW into a perfectly level basin, about half a mile long and a quarter across.' (ii, 884). PUBLISHED: 'Innermost Asia', vol. 2, fig. 420: 'Ferokh-sangau Glacier below Shitok-lazar, Roshan.'

Location:
Ferokh-sangau Glacier; Shitam Pass; Tajikistan
