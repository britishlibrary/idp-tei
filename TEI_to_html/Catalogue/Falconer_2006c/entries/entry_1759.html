Kasim Akhun, Tokhta Marghen, Tokhta Akhun of Islamabad and Dash, hunters at Sarghan-och.
PUBLISHED: 'Innermost Asia', vol. 1, fig. 86: 'Kasim Akhun, Aziz Pawan, and Tokhta-Akhun.' Kasim Akhun standing on left: 'Ever since my expedition to Dandan-oilik in 1900 I had learned to value and trust the pluck, sense of locality, and true desert instinct that a lifetime spent in hunting trips and other lonely wanderings in the Taklamakan had bred in this wiry and ever resolute man.' (i. 82). The different identification of the central figure in the published version appears to be incorrect, since the central figure is not the same man portrayed in print 270 and there identified as Aziz Pawan. Sarghan-och has not been traced on the map, although it must be in the vicinity of Maral-bashi.

Location:
China; Sarghan-och; Xinjiang
