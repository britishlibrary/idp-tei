L.F. Grave i [corrected in pencil to 2] with skeleton between boards. [Fort on Mesa L.F., Lop Desert.]

PUBLISHED: 'Innermost Asia', vol. 1, fig 171: 'First grave opened on Mesa L.F., Lop Desert, with Loplik diggers.' (I.A. Map 32, A3). The published version has been reproduced in reverse. The confusion as to numbering seen in the caption appears to relate to the fact that while this was the first grave opened, its occupant was ascribed the reference number L.F. 2.
