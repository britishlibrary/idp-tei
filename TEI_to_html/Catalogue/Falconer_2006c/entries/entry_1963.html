Section: 1
Camels drinking at Alam-khoja-kol [or Uzun-kol].
PUBLISHED: 'Innermost Asia', vol. 1, fig 122: 'Camels being loaded with ice at Uzun-kol.' Alam-khoja-kol and Uzun-kol are close to one another: 'At the close of the first march from Abdal we passed our old camping place of 1906 near Alam-khoja-kol...finding this depression now quite dry, we had to move about a mile and a half to the east in order to find a lagoon, the Uzun-kol, containing ice...and here we took up our water-supply. Thick slabs of solid fresh-tasting ice were cut overnight, and packed in stout woollen bags, and nineteen of our camels were loaded with them in the morning...' (i, p. 181). (I.A. Map 30, C1).

Location:
China; Uzun-kol; Xinjiang
