Chini-bagh, loggia and towers [British Consulate, Kashgar].
View from garden of portion of Chini-bagh, the British Consulate at Kashgar. The new Consulate building had only recently been completed when Stein took this photograph. Lady Catherina Macartney, wife of the Consul-General Sir George Macartney and her two children, Eric and Sylvia, stand on the terrace. (I.A. Map 2, D2).

Location:
China; Kashgar; Kashi; Xinjiang
