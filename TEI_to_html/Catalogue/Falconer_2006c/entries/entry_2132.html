Tkhegol in flood, seen from north, with Obo of Bayin-bogdo. [From Durwuljin?]
View looking along the river towards Bayin-bogdo hill. The Tkhegol does not appear to be marked on Stein's map, but is presumably one of the watercourses running off from the Etsin-gol around Bayin-bogdo and the photograph may well have been taken from Bahan-Durwuljin (see print 532). (I.A. Map 45, B2).

Location:
Bahan-durwuljin; China; Tkhegol (river); Xinjiang
