Or.11252.29: KT II, p. 25 (4)

Section: 1
Cf. Or.11252/7.

transliteration:
l.1: bulāni rmąmi śäʾräna parau pira-vārā va nūvari jsārä śtāk[a
l.2: 50 8 khara u 6 ṣaṃga drrai śega nūvarä khu hā ni [note a: B.: khahāni.]
haurāta ttī maṃB.: ttīmä. ttye jsārä /
l.3: u guñi haysāka hvaṃḍi tta hā jsārä jseṇi hauḍa 60 4 khara kṣi ṣaṃga 2 śe[ga

translation:
(1) Order from Councilor rMąmi Śäʾrä:
New grain is needed for the silk-worm raisers, (2) 58 kharas, 6 ṣaṃgas, three śiṃgas. If you do not deliver new (grain), then here of that grain ... (3) and the men who send sack (cloth). Thus deliver the grain to them precisely 64 kharas, six ṣaṃgas, 2 śiṃgas!



(OS)
Kha. i.13, numbered consecutively by Hoernle(?) as fol. 1 to fol. 15. The IOL plate labels have Kha. i.13/1, Kha. i.13/2, etc., with the superscript indicating the sequence of plates, but there are no superscripts on the ms.
