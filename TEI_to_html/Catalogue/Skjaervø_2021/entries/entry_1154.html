l.r1: I, the teacher Seṃdū ask after the teacher Vasädatta. If things are well with you (and) healthy, I
l.r2: consider it an honor for me. As for me here, then, things are well (and) healthy. For me the (only) sorrow ... that
l.r3: I have not seen you (all) here. For my mother here, then, things are well. I have heard that you have become so ill. Thus

l.r5: you did not receive my men (who were going) to you. I here ...
