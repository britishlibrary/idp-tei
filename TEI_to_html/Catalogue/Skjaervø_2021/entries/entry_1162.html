l.1: || kaspakyi vī bisai veṃjilakä jsārä nātä nau kūsi saṃ ttī kūsa nau 20 2 mye kṣuṇä mūtca-

translation:
l.1: Veṃjilaka resident in Kaspakā received grain nine kūsas only then nine kūsas: of the 22nd regnal year
l.2: on the 18th day of the month of Mūtcaca in this account six kūsas was that of the resident of the court 4 cattle, and 2 cattle
l.3: belonged to the ṣau Śuresa.
