transliteration:
l.r1: @ spāta vimalīdattä tta parī — śūrapuñä va-
l.r2: ra vaña ma nuṃ śū hīvī haḍa — hvaḍā nāma pajisti ttye
l.r3: ʾauna hajsāmi hauḍādi 1000 500 mūr[ä] u tta jsā ma miṣḍā ḡya-
l.r4: sti hīya bī nā ni ya ʾau[na] pātcä hadāra pajistā[ṃ]di.
l.v1: 1000 200 mūrä ttye pracaina paśā avaśāna vina muḍā śe dva
l.v2: ttye haṃbā himye 20 9 mūrä ttvī hvaḍä 9 mūr-e himārä
l.v3: 200 60 2 thyau hiysdä yana

translation:
l.r1: The spāta Vimaladatta orders thus: To Śūrapuña.
l.r2: ... Now, here Nuṃ Śū requested the names of the men belonging to his *messenger. For that
l.r3: shipment they gave (paid) 1500 mūrās. And thus ...
l.r4: being in the .. of His Gracious Lordship. Then they requested upkeep:
l.v1: 1200 mūrās. For that reason you shall send without delay without ... Accordingly
l.v2: the total amount of it was 29 mūrās. Your men (are) nine. Your mūrās will be 262. [note a: Actually, 9 x 29 = 261.]

l.v3: Make them *available fast! VI
