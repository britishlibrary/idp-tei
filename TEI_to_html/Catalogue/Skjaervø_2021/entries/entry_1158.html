Metrical text dated in the 16th regnal year of Viśaʾ Kīrtta (see Skjærvø, 1991a, p. 266). Praise of Viśaʾ Kīrtta and the Tibetan overlords. Ser., pl. CLI. Cf. Konow, 1939c.

translation:
l.1: (1) When that good time was—at the time when that bhadrakalpika great (2) gracious Lord took birth here, Viśaʾ Kīrtta, by the power of (his) merits. 1.
l.2: (There is) abundance here in every thing because of the merits of the king, as well as because of the Master Tibetans, who are guarding this land of Khotan. 2.
l.3: (3) His sixteenth regnal year has passed. Great respect has arisen for him.Because of the honor of the watchers for the sake of promoting the Law 3
l.4: (4) he with faith (and) in love has invited thither, to Gara, two reverend ones. He at this time, at the beginning of the month of Rrāhaja 4
l.5: for the sake of the protection of the land (...) (5) (So) strive therein the temple for one year well (and) *uninterrupted, so that all sufferings disapppear! 5.
l.6: Utilities as many (6) as they need, all of them *he has gathered for them here.
