translation:
l.4: In the Year of the Ox ...
l.5: Report by the seven princes to the court ...
l.6: With outstanding, heavy ... one birth, kinsman, son [note a: Kumamoto, 1993, p. 157.]
...
l.7: With outstanding, heavy extensive, merits ...
l.9-12: I do reverence to with a mind of warm love near the feet of the great Sī Thū, the dashi mārgōpa¬deśaka traipiṭika, the reverend Prajñendrākaravardha, endowed with exalted, heavy merits (and) extensive kuśalamūlas, and asking about his health, I, his lowly servant, make a report:
l.12-14: How have I, his lowly servant, become separated from the view in the land of the traipiṭika Prajñendrākaravardha. So far, I have no suffering because of my merits and kuśalamūlas (?).
l.15-18: I do reverence to with a mind of warm love near the feet of the great Sī Thū, the dashi mārgōpa¬deśaka traipiṭika, the reverend Prajñendrākaravardha, endowed with exalted, heavy merits (and) extensive kuśalamūlas, and asking about his health. *Why do I not die ... (?). And my suffering has no remedy (?).
l.(19):
l.20-22: I, miserable, make a report at the feet of my dear father, the ṣau Staḍesä(?), and my mother, the lady Vidyehe. and my own sister, the lady Paḍa Tcū Kye, and the lady Kha Cū Lye, asking about their health.
