translation:
(1) Suradatta son of Khadara from Āskūra paid the people of Birgaṃdara 893 mūrās for wheat in the māśa field. The āsala has received them. (2) Virsa from Birgaṃdara delivered wheat 31 kūsas ... -vāsala has received them. Suśdaṃ from Khāhya delivered wheat 10 kūsas 4 khas. (3) Suśdaṃ *canceled wheat (4) 2 kūsas 7 khas. (5) Virsa *canceled 6 kūsas.
