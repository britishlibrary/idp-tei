translation:
l.194-198: The saṃgaka Śāṃ Khṅä Óvāṃʾ ordered (this) to be written in love of the the most supreme buddha-bodhi. May his life increase, his sufferings disappear! In the Year of the Hare, on the first day of the 5th month, in the nakṣatra of ārdra, in the town of Ṣacū, it was completed and finished. [note a: Year 943. See Hamilton, 1979, pp. 53-54; Guide2, p. 22.]
