Or.11344.7: KT II, p. 35 (5)

Section: 1
For the first doc. see Or.11344/3b7-10, for the second see Hedin 20 (20th Braṃkhaysja yr. 36, 3 months earlier?); Or. 11344/17 (Skarhvārā).

transliteration:
l.1: @ vaña vā miṣḍāṃ ḡyastina parau ā skarhveri māśti 10 5mye haḍai si vaña āṃ vā khyeṣa [note a: B.’s reading; the “ṣa” is on a detached piece; on Khyeṣa “Kashgar” [?] see KT VII, p. 50-54.]
vī /
l.2: rṣṭhi dramaji kūsi kaʾjīṃdä strihi vyaulä ṣṭe —

translation:
(1) Now, an order has arrived from the gracious Lord, of the month of Skarahvārā 15th day, that:
Now Khyeṣa ... (2) [when?] ... they beat the drum from Drama(?), [note a: Cf. Or.11252/18.] there is violent alarm. [note b: Cf. Or.11344/3c, Hedin 20.16 (KT IV, pp. 124-25).]

transliteration:
l.3: || spāta sudārrjuṃ tta parī pharṣa sāṃdari vara u p- [... tva?]-
l.4: śdāṃʾ va khu parau pvīʾrau cu burau hva hva piṣkala u [note a: B.: ña.]
auya hvaṃḍi ‘īde bi[ś-
l.5: khv-e tta śau hve harsī [note b: B.: vā tsī.]
u stūri nva vyasthāṃ garkhi āri daṃḍi byehi skarihveri /

translation:
(3) The spāta Sudārrjuṃ orders thus: To the pharṣa Sāṃdara and ...
Now, ... (4) for the ...-s.
When you hear the order, as many discrete sections and village men you have, [transfer them?] all ... (5) If you have even one man left there and a pack-animal, (then) according to the *rule you will incur heavy (punishment for) crimes (and get) the stick.
[The x-th day of the month of] Skarhvārā [the order went out to you].



(OS)
Kha. i.13, numbered consecutively by Hoernle(?) as fol. 1 to fol. 15. The IOL plate labels have Kha. i.13/1, Kha. i.13/2, etc., with the superscript indicating the sequence of plates, but there are no superscripts on the ms.
