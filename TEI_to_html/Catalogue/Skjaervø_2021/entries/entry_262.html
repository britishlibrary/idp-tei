Or.11344.14: KT II, p. 38 (5)

Section: 1
The same (or related) transaction as in Or.11252/23.

transliteration:
l.1: || tti vā hvrrīviṭi drrai ysārä dvī-sa haṣṭāyī haṃbā mūrä hauḍi pauḍauys-e [note a: For paḍauyse.]
mūri hauḍi drrai ysārä drrai-si tti cvai la[k]i /
l.2: u birgaṃdarajā śau thaunaki drrai ysārä mūrä u yąnaki hīye drrai ysārä drrai-sevī tti mūri nāti dasau ysāʾca śau dva na —

translation:
(1) And these mūrās Hvrrīviṭi paid in an amount of 3280. The first mūrās he paid to you (?) were 3300. Those which ... him?) ... (2) and for the people in Birgaṃdara one piece of cloth 3000 mūrās and those of Yąnaka (an amount?) of 3300. Those mūrās he received, 10,000, minus one or two.



(OS)
Kha. i.13, numbered consecutively by Hoernle(?) as fol. 1 to fol. 15. The IOL plate labels have Kha. i.13/1, Kha. i.13/2, etc., with the superscript indicating the sequence of plates, but there are no superscripts on the ms.
