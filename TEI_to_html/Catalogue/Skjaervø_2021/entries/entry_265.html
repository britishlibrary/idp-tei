translation:
(a1) The spāta Sudarrjuṃ orders thus: (2) To [Sāṃdara], the village haṃdasta Sudatta, and Suda[tta?] from Paʾ.
(3) And now the order has come from the gracious Lord that ... (4) And the ...-s of the town the Huns passed (by?).
When you hear the order, tomorrow evening (5) bring ... here.
The order went out to you previously (= a while ago). And when by that ... (6) ... you will (all) have to die, and (there will be) other beatings (i.e., punishments).
Of the month of Skarihvāra (7) [on the x-th] day the ... order (went out to you).
(b1) [To be delivered] to Sāṃdara. SIGN
