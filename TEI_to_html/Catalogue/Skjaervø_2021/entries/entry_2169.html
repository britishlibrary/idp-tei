IOL Khot S. 31
Ch.0046 (C 59): unpubl.

Section: 1
On the back of Chinese Suvarṇaprabhāsa (see IOL Khot S. 30). Ch.0040 in Enoki’s catalog is a mis-reading; the top of the final 6 is covered by conservation paper! It is to the left of and perpendicular to IOL Khot S. 43 and to the right of IOL Khot S. 32. The end of lines 3-5 were previously covered by conservation paper. IOL Khot S. 31 and 43 were at one time folded and either side was used as writing paper. PLATE 8c.
