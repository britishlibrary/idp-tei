translation:
(a1) Order from the Gracious Lord:
In Cira in the Six Villages, *accordingly the section’s (2) grain-providing men of the year (were) in all 53. For each man is ordered—according to the ṣaṃga of the Tibetans’ 7 śiṃgas—(3) 11 kūsas 9 ṣaṃgas of grain. In all it amounts to 6[30] (4) [kūsas] 7 ṣaṃgas of grain. [note a: 53*(11*10+9) = 630*10 + 7 = 6,307.] 225 kūsas 4 ṣaṃgas of lucerne. ✓
(5) [18]4 kūsas of wheat. 221 kūsas 3 ṣaṃgas of millet.
Seven men (6) from *Cira, they deliver only millet. In all, their millet amounts to 83 kūsas 3 ṣaṃgas: (7) ... Visarrjāṃ 52; Haskadarma son of Viṃgula 29; Khąṣṭarāṃ among the Jīvas(?) (8) ... x2; Vismadatta son of Hvrrīviḍta son of/from(?) Ysāḍa 18. ✓
(9) ... 34; Si Vidatta 35.
46 men, they deliver 3 pilas of grain. (10) [In all their grain] amounts to 547 kūsas 4 ṣaṃgas. [note b: 46*119 = 547*10 + 4 = 5,474.] That is 4 kūsas and 9 ṣaṃgas of lucerne.
(11) [x kūsas] of wheat. 3 kūsas of millet. 225 kūsas, 4 ṣaṃgas of lucerne.
(12) 2[x]4 kūsas of wheat. 138 kūsas of millet. ✓
(13) In Birgaṃdara: Ṣanīraka son of Mattiśka 34; Ṣanīraka 24; Vidarrjāṃ 2[x]; (14) Namaubuda, Hunaka son of Dumesalāṃ, Sahadatta, Suhadāysa, (15) Īrasaṃga son of Suhīka, Virgāṃ from *Spai, Naṃdaka son of Mattiśka, Śāṃdatta, Śude *Vaśaʾrasaṃga, (16) -datta. Sāmadatta’s son Darauka, {Ṣanīra son of Bikina}, the khau ... Sīlāṃ from *Spai, (17) ... *Mādāśa.
In Paʾ: Sudatta son of the *Sogdian(?), Kharajsajsa, Puñausa, Sude, *Sudatta, (18) In Āskura: Sīlāṃ in Puñiśāla, Altāṃ, Svarrjāṃ, Makala, Vasade, *Maṃñe, (19) Puʾysdaka.
In Baʾsa: Suhadāysa, Puñade.
In Phaṃnaa: [note c: Cf. Or.11252/14 b3.] Si Vidatta from *Spai, (20) Senila, Yseviḍta, the khau Sudatta from Kūla(?), [note d: Cf. Or.11252/39.6 khau kūlāna ṣanīraki, 34 a2 khau kūlā sudatti, Or.11344/3.3.] Kharamurraa son of Buttakāṃ.
