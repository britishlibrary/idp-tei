translation:
(a1) Now, these (are) the inspectors for the month of Mūñaṃja (that) you ordered to be agreed upon:
(2) Ṣanīra son of Suhīka, Vilocaṃ, Braṃga, Brūnade, Sūradatta, (3) Īysadatta, Vidyabuda, {Senila}, Kāśaka, Kharamurrai, Īrvadatta, (4) Vidārrjāṃ. *Checked. [note a: So, rather than name of person or place?]
This was agreed upon previously (= a while ago).

transliteration:
l.b1: biśa braṃkhaysji 20 4 mye haḍai ttā haṣḍä yuḍeṃ vaña ttā /
l.b2: dīśta vā salā na parya paʾjsä ma byąmā ṣṭi ṣai khvai paṃdāya pa[stā ?
l.b3: pīḍa kuṣṭa buri padeṃda himya || ą̄mācä mama /
l.b4: na ttiña māś[t]a ttara hīsā ||

translation:
(b1) In the bisā, [note a: Cf. Hardinge 075.3 (Or.12637/13), M.T. 0463 (Or.12637/23).] on the 24th day of (the month) of Braṃkhaysja I made a report to you:
Now to you ... (2) in the hand (?) do not order a word! I am in great doubt. Even if ... set out on the road to him(?) ... (3) you write(?) how far could they be made? The āmāca ... [came] here ... (4) ... in this month he will come there (where you are) ...
