translation:
l.a1: The following were the tax collectors who delivered sacks and aysdāṃ to Phęma: For ten men 1 sack, [1] rope.
l.a2: In ...’s year Viṃkausa delivered 3 sacks, 3 ropes, and 4 khas of aysdāṃ. Auśyaka 2 khas of aysdāṃ. Budadatta
l.a3: Iresa 1 sack, 1 rope, 2 khas of aysdāṃ. Saṃgaśūrai 1 sack, 1 rope, and 2 khas of aysdāṃ. Saṃgaka 1 sack, 1 rope ...
l.a4: Braśūrai delivered 4 khas. Māmattī 2 khas of aysdāṃ. Suhadatta 2 sacks, *3 ropes. Brapuña 1 sack.
l.b1: Māmattī: 3 sacks, 3 ropes.
l.b2: Sucandra: 2 sacks, 2 ropes.
l.b3: Budasaṃga: aysdāṃ 1 kha.
l.b4: Sulocaṃ delivered 2 khas, 2 suraṃs.
l.b5: Śūraka: aysdāṃ 3 khas.
l.c1: This is the promised amount of sacks:
l.c2: Saṃgaka: 2 sacks.
l.c3: Brapuña: 1 sack.
l.c4: Sulocaṃ: 3 sacks.
l.c5: Suhadatta: 1.
l.c6: Saṃgapuña: 1.
l.c7: This is the promised amount of aysdāṃ:
l.c8: Budadatta: a kha by pāra amount(?).
l.c9: ... Brapuña a kha.
l.c10: ... -suraṃ 3.5 khas.
l.c11: *Braśūrai 2.5 khas.
l.c12: *Saṃgaka 3.5 khas.
