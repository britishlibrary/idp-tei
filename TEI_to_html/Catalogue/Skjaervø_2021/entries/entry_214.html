Or.11252.8: KT II, p. 18 (4)

Section: 1
transliteration:
l.a1: bulą̄ni śaʾma rrjai tta parī — pharṣa sāṃdari vara haṃtsa kīraryau
l.a2: jsa mara hīsa jsār-e vāra ṣṭi cu jsāri biysīyi ʾī marai ājima thu ra spasa
l.a3: u rrūnai maṃ śtāka ṣṭi spāta yąniviṭi bvāśta ttā rrūnai keṇa past-e baḍarī vā na thu spa
l.a4: u ttīy-[e] jsāṃ ttā kapāysä kiṇa hv[i]ṃdū śäʾ va ttā girye-v-ī mūtcaʾci paḍauysye haḍai
l.b4: || pharṣa sāṃda- (upper left)
l.b5: rä hai- (middle vertical)
l.b6: śāʾñä (lower right)

translation:
(a1) Councilor Śaʾma rrJai orders thus: To the pharṣa Sāṃdara.
Come here with the workers! (2) You owe grain! Whatever grain you have obtained, bring it here today! ... (3) And I need oil. The spāta Yąniviṭi passed through (here). He ordered you about oil: Bring me (some)! ... (4) And then (he ordered) you about cotton. Hviṃdū bought it for you for the second (?).
First day of Mūtcaʾca.
To be sent to the pharṣa Sāṃdara.

transliteration:
l.b1: braṃgi | senili | naṃdaki || hvūrähvādi || są̄madatti || kharrjyāni || makali ṣanīraki
l.b2: aśnadatti [|| x s]ulo x haṃ’ sudarmä tti vā kavārma tsvāṃdi ṣanīrakä saṃgūlai ʾīrvadatti
l.b3: ʾīrasaṃgi || suhadāysi || vaśiʾraki || kāgaki

translation:
(b1) Braṃga, Senila, Naṃdaka, Hvūrähvāda, Są̄madatta, Kharrjyāna, Makala, Ṣanīraka, (2) Aśnadatta, ..., ... Sudarma.
And the following have gone to Kavārma: Ṣanīraka, Saṃgūlai, Īrvadatta, Īrasaṃga, Suhadāysa, Vaśiʾraka, Kāgaka.
Cf. Or.11344/8.
