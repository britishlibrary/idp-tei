Section: 1

transliteration:
l.1: @ tti bura hvaṃḍi cu skarihverä māśti didye haḍai spaśaña /
l.2: datti || sudarmä || puñadi || maṃgali || har(y)āsakä [||] ṣ[anīr-

translation:
(1) The following (are) the men who [went] on inspection on the third day of the month of Skarihvāra: (2) [-]datta, Sudarma, Puñade, Maṃgala, Haryāsaka, ... (3) Paṃjaka, Mulaka, Jsajsaka, Hunaka, Burmaka.



(OS)
Kha. i.13, numbered consecutively by Hoernle(?) as fol. 1 to fol. 15. The IOL plate labels have Kha. i.13/1, Kha. i.13/2, etc., with the superscript indicating the sequence of plates, but there are no superscripts on the ms.
