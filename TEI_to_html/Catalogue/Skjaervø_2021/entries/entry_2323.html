Section: 1
According to Ser., p. 176, these are 8 frgs. of a wall painting with two bands of ornament; the upper band has a series of trumpet-shaped blossoms on a maroon background which are bordered above and below by a broad white line, part of which contains a Brahmi inscription. The drawings are preserved in Hoernle’s papers (IOR/MSS EUR F 302/12).
