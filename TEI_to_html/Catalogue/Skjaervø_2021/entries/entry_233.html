(1) Budadatta, Kāśaka, Naṃdaka — 15 inspectors.
(2) The following are going on inspection on the 16th day of Braṃkhaysja: Sahadatta from Phaṃnai, (3) Ṣanīraka, Hvrrīviṭa, Visarrjāṃ, Kharamūrrai, Marṣaʾdatta, {Śāṃdatta}, Si Vidyadatta, Saloka, (4) Maṃñe, Sarkāṃ, {Mulaka}, {Makala}, Īrvadatta, {Arsäla}, {Yaudara}.
