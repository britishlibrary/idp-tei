Kha. i.13, numbered consecutively by Hoernle(?) as fol. 1 to fol. 15. The IOL plate labels have Kha. i.13/1, Kha. i.13/2, etc., with the superscript indicating the sequence of plates, but there are no superscripts on the ms.
2324
CA Khot 12
Ch.i.0022: Serindia, p. 1012

Section: 1
Inscription on silk banner. Hoernle provided a preliminary, but quite correct, reading and translation.

transliteration:
l.1: . . pasi salya rarūyi māśta 20 6 mye haḍai x thyai ahā [yi]
l.2: pasta haiṣṭe beʾysūśta brrīyi harbīśai āvama prra[ṇi]-
l.3: hna sījīde nauda .

translation:
l.1-3: In the year of the sheep, on the 26th day of the month Rarūya. Thyai gave order to give it to you, in love of Bodhi. May all his wishes and resolves (praṇidhāna) succeed! Reverence!
