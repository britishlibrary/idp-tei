l.1: I the ṣṣau Aśauka ask the haṃdasta in Gaysāta and all the concerted villagers ...
l.2: has now become better for me and not ... I have received. The dashi and the gracious one have not

l.5: come here quickly! And if you come later ... they completed ... Then in the end he can not pull at all(?).

l.7: and when the gracious lord ... Birgaṃdara
l.8: quickly *send my men to him! [if not?] he incurs guilt and *responsibility for wheat for the court (?).
