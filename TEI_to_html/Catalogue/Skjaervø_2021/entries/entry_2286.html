translation:
l.1: In the fifteenth regnal year, this much stuff was ordered for (each) half man in Gaysāta:
l.2: an amount of 606 mūrās per capita. And hemp cloth for winter clothing
l.3: 9 chās 2 tsunas; for (a piece from) a seventy-chā roll the mūrās become 616.
l.4: And hemp cloth for summer clothing was ordered 8 chās 6 tsunas; for (a piece from) a sixty-chā (roll),
l.5: the mūrās for it become 516. And cotton cloth for winter clothing was ordered
l.6: 1 chā 5 tsūnas, so for (a piece from) a fifty chā-roll the mūrās become 225. And
l.7: summer clothing, its amount was ordered (as) 170 mūrās. And for a half man, in total,
l.8: 2133 mūrās (worth of stuff) was ordered, with the exception of the amount of seventy chās for the gracious lords.
