Or.6398.1: KT V, p. 6 (12)

Section: 1
Note special form of ra looking like ṭv! Hoernle, 1901, p. 38.

transliteration:
l.1: @ skarähvęrä māśtä {śtä} hvurrä dū pamūhaja thona hauḍiʾ dva pātcä th[au]
l.2: hauḍiʾ śau cu rrusa parātä [note a: Clear tä; B.: nä.]


translation:
(1) In the month of Skarihvāra Hvurrä Dū delivered two cloths for garments. Afterward (2) he delivered one cloth, because he had sold barley.



(OS)
Kha. i.13, numbered consecutively by Hoernle(?) as fol. 1 to fol. 15. The IOL plate labels have Kha. i.13/1, Kha. i.13/2, etc., with the superscript indicating the sequence of plates, but there are no superscripts on the ms.
