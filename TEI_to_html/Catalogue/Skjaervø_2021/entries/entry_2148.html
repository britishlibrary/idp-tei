Section: 1
On the back of Chinese sutra Fo shuo guanding suiyuan wangsheng shi fang jing tu. Lines 67-97 in KT I-III, p. 147 (88). School exercise, Khotanese alternating with “Gostana Sanskrit” and model lines with copies in an unskilled hand frequently degenerating into mere scrawls (all of which were read by Bailey—probably correctly). Lines 39-65 letter/report with a date (lines 63-64) in the 14th regnal year of the Tianxing era, Year of the Pig (963).
