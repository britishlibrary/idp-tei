(a1) [N.N. orders thus.(?)] To Sude and Sudatta of Paʾ. [note a: Cf. Or.11252/2.17, 4 a2.]
Your chaskas (are too) *little. (2) Bring ... *other pack-animals! And for one man [you shall send] *without delay [note b: Cf. Or.11252/12 b2, Or.11252/19, IOL Khot Wood 29, Hedin 33b1.] barley 2 ṣaṃgas. (3) ... you (shall?) deliver to me ...
On the 26th day of Seṃjsīja he orders the order thus:
(4) 3 roads he had been able to go, you still owe *roads. [note c: Cf. Or.11252/33.2, 11344/1.27, 11344/13.6.] Now for you my ... (5) ... you shall go ... *load this seat. If the Tibetans *kill you (all) ... (6) ...
... *Buttaṃdai [note d: In Or.11252/35.4.] — Suhade Svarrnade in Āskura.

[Now] ... (3) And here in Phęma I have no tasty wine. Now, send me one wine! ... (4) If they do not have one khūra, ... those two kūsas *Puñamaya (?) ... (5) you look after ... If it comes here in this month on the 7th day ...
