Section: 1
transliteration:
l.1: @ ṣiʾ vā haṣṭāyeṃ thau[nāṃ ? paṃ]jsūsi ą̄māci sūlīna paphve thauna vā nāte dva

translation:
(1) And this is ... for 80 cloths. Fifteen the āmāca collected from the *Sogdian. And he took two cloths (2) of 14 chās. Woolen [cloths ...] *Ṣanīraka received (them). Brų̄nade received 3 cloths. Two pieces of cloth (3) according to the mūrās went out ... *thousand ... 53 *pieces of cloth, those the *Sogdians received for (= in order to make?) seven cloths.
(4) Afterward ... have come in for an amount of one thousand.



(OS)
Kha. i.13, numbered consecutively by Hoernle(?) as fol. 1 to fol. 15. The IOL plate labels have Kha. i.13/1, Kha. i.13/2, etc., with the superscript indicating the sequence of plates, but there are no superscripts on the ms.
