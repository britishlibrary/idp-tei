translation:
(r1) ... *lights, that buddha should be revered by him, (and) he will obtain birth as a man.
He whom it should please ... for birth after birth ... (r2) ... obtains ... (and) it should always be secure for him. That buddha should be revered by him ... (r3) ...
Thus ... spoke to him: Whomever it should please in his mind, he will be born in all ten directions, (r4) in Buddha-fields which (are) flawless. Or he whom far away pure, flawless buddha fields (should please), if, however briefly, (r5) he were to reach them, in just that way he must be revered by him. He whom a birth among the *Tuṣita gods pleases, where Maitreya (v1) dwells, in just that way it must be revered by him. He goes straight among the Tuṣita gods. As for those who follow evil false paths, if he has stayed away from [those], (v2) in just that way he must be revered by him, and he (shall) no more walk on false paths. He who has evil dreams at night or who (v3) ... a hundred kinds of confused, unfavorable signs, suddenly falls, or if a (v4) *ghoul [makes him miserable] (or) strength-robbing demons, (then) that Buddha must be revered by him, and they will not be able to harm him. (v5) He who (burns) in fire (or) drowns in water, who ... in ...
