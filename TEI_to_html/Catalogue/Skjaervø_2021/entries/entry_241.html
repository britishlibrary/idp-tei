translation:
(1) [The following (are)] the men who have to go on inspection on the third day of the month of Mūñaṃja: Instead of Śarkāṃ Vaśiʾraka *goes. (2) ... -datta, Budāṣṭira, Kaledra. *Checked. In Cira, Svarrnade māśa-worker, [note a: B.: “house-worker.”] (3) ... {Śarkāṃ} māśa-worker, Surade māśa-worker, Saṃgapuña, Vidarrjāṃ, (4) ... Salamai the haubarai of Sīhai, Buttaṃdai māśa-worker ... (5) *Suhadāysa māśa-worker ... Nahvąna. COPY Instead of Vidarrjāṃ Svarrnade goes.

transliteration:
l.b1: || spāta sudārrjuṃ tta parī pharṣa sāṃdarä va parau ttā hauḍeṃ si

l.b3: ā himi thyau vā puṣi ttrą̄mīrau haṃtsa gų̄hyau jsa cu bur-e tsīdä ṣapą̄ñarāṃ āṃ vā
l.b4: būka ni hīdä [note a: For hīsīdä?]
hvaṃḍi āṃ ma gva-v-āṃ na dejsārä thyau vā ṣapą̄ñara haysa

translation:
(1) The spāta Sudārrjāṃ orders thus: To the pharṣa Sāṃdara.
I sent the (following) order to you that: (2) Come right here into (the city)! We will make a report to you about the grain. (If) you do not come in here (now), (3) it will be too late (?). (So) come quickly right here together with the cattle as many of yours go.
And no būkas of *canteen workers (4) are *coming, and now the men, their *ears keep no(thing) of mine(?). Quickly send me *canteen workers! [note a: Cf. Or.11252/25.2.] COPY
