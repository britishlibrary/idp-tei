l.1101: Those names of the billions of bhadrakalpika lord buddhas
l.1102: and bodhisattvas and arhats and elders, the saṃgaka Śāṃ Khṅä Hvāṃʾ ordered (them) to be written with a view to the requirement for a long life.

l.1106: and brothers near and far. Having shared (them), may I ripen them for bodhi. Reverence!
l.1107-1109: Hail to the three jewels! etc.
