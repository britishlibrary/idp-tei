One Gostana Skt. text (Uttaratantra) with some Khot. text (see Bailey and Johnston, 1935) and two Chinese texts (C 111, 123) on back of Chinese Aparimitāyuḥ-sūtra. C 111 is a “part of a memorandum concerning supply of oil, leaven, rice, and wheat to monks” (Enoki); C 123 “16 characters written at random with the same pen as the Khotanese text” (Enoki). Bailey and Johnston, 1935, pl. II.
