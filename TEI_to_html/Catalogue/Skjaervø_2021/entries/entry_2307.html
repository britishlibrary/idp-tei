translation:
l.1: I thus make report to the Lord Minister, the ṣṣau Viṣṇadatta. — I (am)
l.2: Yseviḍta, [note b: Cf. Hedin 12 b1 phąnāji auva haṃdasti ysiviṭi.]
resident in the Six Towns. O Lord, the aḍāsvnas (?) have ordered from me royal cloths.
l.3: On the seventh day of the month of Skarihvāra of the 35th regnal year. This letter (is) for this reason that Sinila of Phaṃnai
l.4: and Ṣanīraka of Birgaṃdara received woolen cloths and hemp cloth pieces. And the amount of it (was) 1000 mūrās. Makala [received ?] cloth
l.5: and Yseviḍta.
l.6: 24 chās belong to the spāta Vidyadatta, 40 chās to Svarrjāṃ, 23 chās to Puñaśālyā, 20 chās ...
l.7: Pieces of cloth from Āskūra 20 and two here accordingly of mūrās. Sarkāṃ a woolen cloth 19 chās, (also imposed?) upon (him is?) one piece of cloth.
l.8: Suhena woolen cloth 20 chās, and (also imposed?) upon (him is?) one piece of cloth together with the mūrās. Its total 1000. And hemp pieces of cloth ...
