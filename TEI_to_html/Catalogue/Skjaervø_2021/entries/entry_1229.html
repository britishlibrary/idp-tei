l.r2: This Jātakastava, the limb of the difficult/marvelous deeds of the buddas,
l.r3: Cā Kīmä Śąnä ordered it to be written in love of bodhi. Through support upon the merits and roots of goodness (resulting) from it, may the cakravartin Śai Tcūnä have eternal rule. May my gati be a special gati,
l.r4: a special rebirth (upapatti), a special place (sthāna)! At present, may the vajra-rāja king have the enjoyment of a long life! May he have a great, endless, long life! May the karmas (and) sins that I have accumulated for kalpas

and with (his?) sons (and) daughters. That merit with (my) wife, Kīma Hva and (my) daughter
l.v3: Rūpājīvā (and my) daughter Jvālakyā, (my) brother, the traipiṭika Sīdyakavarrda, the traipiṭika Dharmajña, with all the monks, householders, (and) Vajrayāṇa-followers. May all beings obtain a prophecy toward bodhi!
