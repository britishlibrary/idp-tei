translation:
(2) ... they did ... Sarvaṃdāta ... (3) Lattärhaṃ’s wife accepts her into (her) jurisdiction. If the wife ... the husband ... (4) ... afterward in her jurisdiction. But as for her daughter, Yaśāśärī Khattunä by name, (5) the spāta raises (her). When she grows up, then Lattärhaṃ ... her ... (6) ... and he himself provides her with a husband. And whatever in future *time (7) ... she has ...-ed. Whoever may change this polished and settled case, [to] the court (8) he will pay ... mūrās and receive 30 strokes. [note a: Cf. Or 9268B b6-7.]
And then (9) [this writ takes effect when] Lattärhaṃ puts his finger on it.
These (10) [were] *witnesses. — Lattärhaṃ’s finger.
