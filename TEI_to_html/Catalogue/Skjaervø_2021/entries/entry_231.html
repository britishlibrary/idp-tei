Or.11252.25: KT II, p. 24 (4)

Section: 1
Apparently unskilled hand.

transliteration:
l.1: || spāta sudārrjāṃ tta parī pharṣa [sāṃda]-
l.2: rä vara khu parau pvaʾ agaru [note a: First two akṣaras uncertain reading.]
ṣapą̄ña ma {ha} haysa x /
l.3: 10 3 mye haḍai parau

translation:
(1) The spāta Sudārrjāṃ orders thus: [To] the pharṣa [Sāṃdara].
(2) When you hear the order send no more(?) for *canteen duty! [note a: Cf. Or.11252/35b4.]
[Of the month of X] (3) on the 13th day the order (went out to you). SIGN



(OS)
Kha. i.13, numbered consecutively by Hoernle(?) as fol. 1 to fol. 15. The IOL plate labels have Kha. i.13/1, Kha. i.13/2, etc., with the superscript indicating the sequence of plates, but there are no superscripts on the ms.
