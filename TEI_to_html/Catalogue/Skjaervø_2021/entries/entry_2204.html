translation:
l.1: Let him not now make any unpleasantness for the pharṣavata on my account! I spoke this word
l.2: before here in the city *regarding the house. Then I spoke with the gracious one.
l.3: The gracious one did not give me this house, because I heard that they intend to give this house to Prince Kheysara.
l.4: Now Puñadāysa has brought (it) to you and brought that document from the gracious one.
l.5: ... May you deign to make an effort in that matter that the men and draught animals there where Puñadāysa ... them for me
l.6: ... you yourself write the pharṣavata or give to me the documents and whatever ...
l.7: ... speak ...!
