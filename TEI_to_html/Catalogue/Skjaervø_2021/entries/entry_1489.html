translation:
l.r3-4: This upacāra should be tied onto this mantra.
l.r4-5: A circle of cow dung should be made for the bodhisattva Āryāvalokiteśvara.
l.r5-v1: Four juices mixed with milk should be placed there at fifteen harmus(?),
l.v1-2: and flower wreaths with four perfumes, with eight perfumes, and with four perfumes, (and) perfumed flowers.
l.v2-3: For three nights and days ... should not be taken,
l.v3-4: but three times a day one should wash, and standing thus this mantra should be recited for the gods.
l.v4-5: And next, for another seven days eight times daily the mantra should be recited, and thus (too) at night. [One should wear] white garments ...
