translation:
l.1058: The Mahayana sutra called Sumukha, the devotion to the Law, the knowledge protecting life, is finished (and)
l.1059: completely completed. Homage to this Sumukha-sūtra! The saṃgaka Śāṃ Khṅä Hvāṃʾ ordered (this) to be written with a view to prolonging life.
l.1060: May sufferings and untimely death go to disappearance and turning around(?)! Hail! In the year
l.1061: of the Hare, the fifth day of the ninth month, in the city of Shazhou, I finished it completely.
