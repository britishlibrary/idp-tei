IOL Khot S. 43
Ch.0049 (C 59): KT II, p. 41 (8)

Section: 1
The original Ch.no. has been scratched out and is now illegible and “629” written below. On the back of Chinese Suvarṇaprabhāsa (see IOL Khot S. 30). Edges covered by conservation paper. Two pieces that fit together perfectly (Bailey’s a and b). It is to the right of IOL Khot S. 31, which has the beginning of line 1. Cf. IOL Khot S. 33.

translation:
l.1: [The order] of the great Cao De Wang and his consort and ‘How are you,’ that: You, O great prince
l.2: Pū Yaṃ and Śī Kvaina and Ttaugä, Cā Krraśīsa, Prince Vaijalaka,
l.3: and Prince Svahaṃka!
l.4: Cā Krraśīsa and Kva Ṣanīraka
l.5: the amaga Svanī and Kīma Ttehą Śana
l.6: Yā Kī Tsena La much health!
l.7: Hvāṃ Sa(ṃ)ga and Sera Hvai may he live(?).
