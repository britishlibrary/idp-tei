Or.11252.18: KT II, p. 22 (4)

Section: 1
transliteration:
l.a1: @ spāta sudārrjāṃ tta parī au au auva-haṃda[st- vara ?
l.a2: u drrīma ttvaśdā vara ttāgutta hvāṣṭa tta parstāṃdi si hvaṃḍi vā bi[ś]i /
l.a3: ttvaśdyari khu parau pvīrau hvaṃḍi vā kītha ttvaśdyari {braṃkhaysji} kṣemye [ttā ?
l.a3a: mūñaṃji
l.a4: parau tsve

translation:
(a1) The spāta Sudārrjāṃ orders thus: [To] the village *haṃdastas in each village ... (2) and you shall transfer to Drrama(?). There (= regarding that) the Tibetan Masters have ordered thus that: Transfer here *all the men ...
(3) When you hear the order *transfer the men here to the town.
On the sixteenth of {Braṃkhaysja} Mūñaṃja (4) the order went out [to you].

(vertically)

transliteration:
l.b1: [hiyau]di āmāci ṣau viṣṇadattä haiśāʾñi

translation:
(b1) To be delivered to the hiyaude āmāca, the ṣau Viṣṇadatta. [note a: Cf. Or.11344/8, IOL Khot 177/3b.]



(OS)
Kha. i.13, numbered consecutively by Hoernle(?) as fol. 1 to fol. 15. The IOL plate labels have Kha. i.13/1, Kha. i.13/2, etc., with the superscript indicating the sequence of plates, but there are no superscripts on the ms.
