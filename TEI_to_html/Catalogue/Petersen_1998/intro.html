The Dunhuang manuscripts in the Royal Library of Copenhagen were donated by Arthur Bollerup Sørensen (1880–1932) on 29 November 1915. The Dunhuang collection of the Royal Library of Copenhagen consists of 16 manuscripts in 14 rolls. Except for one Daoist scripture, the manuscripts are Buddhist. The text of MS 2c seems to have been transmitted nowhere else but this collection. MSS 12 and 16 present texts transmitted only in the form of Dunhuang manuscripts in other collections. Both of these are of special interest as they are supplied with colophons.
