Section: R.1
Beginning mission,no title at end.
Part of juan 9 ofTaixuan zhenyi benji jing太玄真一本際經.Juan 9 of this Taoist text is also known as theTaishan dongxuan lingbao kaiyan mimi zang太上洞玄靈寶開演祕密藏, the title it bears in the Daozang.
P 3280 has been reproduced in the edition of this work made by Wu chi-yu. The present MS corresponds to lines 59-278 (皆虛 to 而去) according to Wu's numbering.
