MS 5
OA 102-MS-5

Section: R.1
Title at beginning:大般若波羅密多經卷第二百七十二.初分難信解品第卅四之九十一.
Following this: 三藏法師奉詔譯.
End missing.
On the verso of the beginning:大般若波羅密多經卷第二百七十二. 卄八. 二. 界.
Part of juan 272 of theMahāprajñāpāramitā-sũtra, T no.220. The MS corresponds to T vol.6, pp. 376c.13-377b.6 (善現 to 門清). The inscription on the verso indicatas that the MS was the second roll in the 28th wrapper in a library (not the tripitaka library) in the Sanjie 三界 Monastery. The Sanjie Monastery was established about 830-840.
