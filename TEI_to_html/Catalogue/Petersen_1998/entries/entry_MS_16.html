Juan 1 of theSi fen jie ben shu四分戒本疏, written by the ṡramana Hui 慧. Marks in red. The text of this juan has been edited and introduced by Tsukinowa Kenryu and Tsuchihashi Shuko. The copist of the present MS was the same as that of the MS (Ryukoku University catalogue no.46) used for their edition. The colophon reads: "Note written by the bhikṣu Faxian after proceeding to the Grottoes of Unsurpassable Height to copy and proofread on the 22nd day of the 10th month of a hai 亥 year." Every twelfth year contains the cyclical characterhai. TheSi fen jie ben shuhas only been transmitted in the form of Dunhuang manuscripts.
