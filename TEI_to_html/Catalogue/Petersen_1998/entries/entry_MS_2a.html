Part of theJi zhu fangdeng xuejing濟諸方等學經T no.274. The MS contains one line of text, corresponding to vol. 9, p. 374c.6 (聞如 to 比丘).
Roll 2 has been made to supply paper for calligraphy practice. Sheets from various texts have been pasted together and the latter part of the recto has been covered with doodles, including a short text. A large 兑 is found on the verso, indicating that a sheet was discarded because it contained lacunae. Among the doodles one finds three names: Zhai Huixing 翟會興 Wang Dingyuan 王定員 and Dao Gaolong 道高龍. The MSS of this roll are, with the exception of MS 2f, written in the same hand. At least two persons have written the doodles.
