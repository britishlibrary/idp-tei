Incipit ( 1r.1 ): gdung la phyag 'tshal lo/
The four folios that comprise this manuscript were originally part of a concertina. Most of the pages are not contiguous, although 4 may follow 3 . The first folio is a concertina page from the Uṣṇīṣasitātapatra-dhāraṇī . The recto corresponds to P_204: 257a.7-257b.1 and the verso to P_204: 258b.8-259a.1 .

Explicit ( 1v.4 ): sred med kyi
