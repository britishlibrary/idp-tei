[Uṣṇīṣa-sitātapatre catur-vidhi]
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/:/'u shan ka re sa ma ye swA hA/
Fragment of a ritual manual for performing the four activities according to the tradition of Uṣṇīṣasitātapatra . Another fragment, which fits into the upper right hand corner, can be found at ITJ_1236 . The rite seems to exhibit the influence of Yoga tantra ritual techniques; see for example, the blessing and protection of the five points on one's own body ( 1r.8 and 1r.28 ) and the emphasis on "one's own suchness" ( 1r.12 bdag gI de kho na).
