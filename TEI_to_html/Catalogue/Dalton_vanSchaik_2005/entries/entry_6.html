['phags pa shes rab kyi pha rol tu phyin pa'i tshul brgya lnga bcu pa]

Incipit ( Vf.1r.1 ): pa'I tshi de ni 'dI ste byanng cub sems dpa'i tshig go/
Two non-consecutive folios. Though now appearing as pothi-style folios, they clearly come from a concertina, so that 2v comes before 1v . The first folio corresponds to the canonical version at P_121: 278b.2-3 , and the second at P_121: 281b.8-282a.2 , though there are significant differences between the present item and the canonical version.
