Incipit ( V6.1 ): @/__/gnam babs kyi dar ma bam po gcig go//
This text is a historical account of Buddhism in Tibet during the imperial period. It has been transcribed, translated and discussed in Richardson_1998: pp.74-81 . The text is incomplete at the end.
