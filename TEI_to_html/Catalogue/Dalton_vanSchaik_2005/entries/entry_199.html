Incipit ( 1r.1 ): zhag bzhi pa la/
This fragment appears to have originally been part of a scroll. The ritual seems to be medical in nature. It specifies reciting a mantra 1008 times over a garland of nutmeg blossoms ( ( 1r.1 ) sna ma'i men tog), and then binding the garland to the head of the afflicted one. Reciting it to the afflicted one will cure mental confusion ( brjed byed ).

Explicit ( 1r.5 ): /sangs rgyas la phul ba'i men tog gis/bdugo/
