Section: 1
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/:/phyogs bcu'i sangs rgyas thams cad la phyag 'tshal lo/
This manuscript, a single panel from a concertina, belongs with ITJ_379_1 and PT_107 , as well as the single folio ITJ_285 . See ITJ_379_1 for a discussion of the order of the original concertina.
The text contained here begins on ITJ_379_2: verso and runs through PT_107: recto and ITJ_379_1: verso where it concludes. It is a confession prayer. For a discussion, see entry to ITJ_379_1/1 .

Explicit ( 1r.6 ): /byang shar 'tshams gyi sangs rgyas ting 'dzIn gyi glang po

Section: 2
'phrul gyi byig bshus phyi ma'i mye la bstan pa'i mdo
Incipit ( 1v.1 ): kyang chog myI shes/
This sutra begins on PT_107: verso , and a later section appears in ITJ_285: recto . It is incomplete at the end. The sutra, which appears in several versions in the Pelliot collection, is a sermon on fundamental Buddhist concepts of karma and rebirth. It does not appear in the bka' 'gyur .

Explicit ( 1v.6 ): gson gshIn lam gnyis so sor
