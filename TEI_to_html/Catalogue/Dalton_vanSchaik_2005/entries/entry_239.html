Section: 1
'phags pa rIg sngags kyI rgyal po chan po stobs po che theg pa chen po 'I mdo ste las sngags nyI tse btus ste bgyis pa
[Ārya-mahābala-nāma-mahāyāna-sūtra]
Incipit ( 2r.1 ): spyI gtsug la swa ha /_'phral ba la swa hā /
This manuscript, which is not complete, is a set of folio pages (not a concertina, as mistakenly asserted by LVP) out of their original order. Most of the folios have been placed the wrong side up, so that the modern page numbers are stamped on the verso instead of the recto.
The first text, which is fragmentary at the beginning, is part of the Mahābala-nāma-mahāyāna-sūtra . There are many copies of the complete sutra in the Dunhuang collections: see ITJ_390 for a discussion. As the explicit indicates, this text mainly comprises the mantra . It begins with 2r.1 , which corresponds to P_416: 127a.8 . After a lacuna, the text continues on 14v - 19v - 18v - 13v - 12v - 7v - 6v . At 6v.1 the text departs from the canonical version ( P_416: 128b.7 ) and skips several passages, picking up on 11v.1 , which corresponds to P_416: 130a.5 . The order of the manuscript continues as follows: 20v - 17v - 16v - 15v - 9v .
An interesting feature of this manuscript is that occasional syllables or whole lines have been erased, while others are highlighted with red pigment.

Explicit ( 9v.2 ): /'phags pa rIg sngags kyI rgyal po chan [sic] po stobs po che theg pa chen po 'I mdo ste las sngags nyI tse btus ste bgyis pa rdzogs s+ho //

Section: 2
Incipit ( 9v.3 ): @/ { dby...u...se...bskyid pa } /_/byang chub mchog gI sems nI bskyed par
The incipit and explicit of this prayer have been erased. The prayer is as the title suggests, on the development of the enlightened mind of compassion. This brief prayer runs from 9v to 8v .

Section: 3
Incipit ( 8r.1 ): @::/__/na mo rad na tra yā ya /
The remainder of the manuscript should be ordered as follows: 5v - 4v - 3v - 1v . These folios contain a series of dhāraṇī spells. The first is from the Vajravidāraṇā-dhāraṇi ( P_406: 61a.2-61b.2 ). At 3v.2 this is followed by a series of the spells from drawn from the Uṣṇīṣasitātapatre-dhāraṇī . The first can be found at P_204: 260b.4 . The second is similar to the spell that appears in P_203: 255b.7 . The third appears at P_203: 254a.6 .

Explicit ( 1r.4 ): hu'um hu'um phat /swā hā /
