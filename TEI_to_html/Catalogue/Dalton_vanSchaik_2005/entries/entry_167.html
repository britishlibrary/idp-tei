snang brgyad gyi mdo
[sangs rgyas kyi chos gsal zhing yangs pa snang brgyad ces bya ba'i mdo]
[Aṣṭa-darśana-sūtra]
Incipit ( Rf.1r.1 ): lha 'phrul dang ldan ba thams cad kyis/ mdo sde
Incomplete at the beginning and the end. There is also a gap (probably of only one folio) between Rf.6v and Rf.7r (= Vf.2r and Vf.2v ). To fill this gap, one may consult ITJ_463/1: Vf.7v.3-8r.5 (and ITJ_463/1: Vf.11r.1-11v.1 ).
This is one of six incomplete copies of the Snang brgyad found in the Stein collection. For a discussion of the content, see entry to ITJ_463/1 .

Explicit ( Vf.8r.8 ): rnal 'byor spyod pa'i mdo sde dag 'byung
