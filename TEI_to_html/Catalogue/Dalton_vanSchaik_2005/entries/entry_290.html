Incipit ( Rf.1r.1 ): 'das pa'i rang bzhin la//gong bur 'du shes ci ltar blta'//cung zad tsam yang khas blang myed//de tshe/
Compare ITJ_706/1 and ITJ_1372 . This is a page from a version of the much-studied Tibetan Chan work, PT_116/5: 128.3-133.1 , containing a number of quotations from a variety of sutras. The passages cited from the Prajñāpāramitā Sūtra and the Lankāvatāra Sūtra ( P_775 ) are also found in STMG . The other two works cited--the Sdud pa'i tshig su bcad pa'i mdo (= Prajñāpārmitā-sañcaya-gāthā --compare P_735: 6a.8-6b.1 ) and the Yang dag pa'i ye shes bsam gyis mi khyab pa'i mdo ( Tathāgataguṇajñānācintyaviṣayāvatāranirdeśa , P_852 --passage not found) are not cited in STMG . An English translation of this fragment may be found in Faber_1985: 55-58 . Further readings include: Tanaka_1992 , Ueyama_1983 .
