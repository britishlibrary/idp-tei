Section: 1
gtsug tor gzugs pa'i smon lam
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/:/srung ba mdzad du gsol/
This is a single folio, with the Tibetan number ka lnga (5). Two more folios, numbered ka gnyis (2) and ka bzhi (4) are found in PT_173 . The original whole manuscript appears to have been a collection of short prayers, perhaps all related to the deity Uṣṇīṣasitātapatrā ( Gtsug tor gdugs dkar po ). The identity of the prayer on PT_173: f.1 is unknown. PT_173: f.2 begins with the name of the current text, which concludes on the first line of the verso side.

Explicit ( 1v.1 ): gyis kyang yul rab tu zhi bar mdzad du gsol//
