Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/:/ce ti brgya...btab pa'I cho ga/
First folio of an incomplete ritual manual for building 108 caitya . The second, fourth, and fifth folios can be found at PT_308 , and the sixth and final folio can found at ITJ_522 . Note that a second text, beginning on the missing folio numbered nya, seems to belong to the same manuscript. Both texts appear to focus on the deity Hūṃkara ( Hung zhes pa ). The present text on caitya cites as its sources the tantras of Yoga , Ubhayā , and Kriyā (see PT_308: 2r.1 ). Reference is also made to the four tantric activities ( las bzhi ), in the context of preparing the clay for the building (see PT_308: 2v.6-3r.1 ).

Explicit ( 1v.7 ): dar dkar gyI bla res man dal khebs par bre/ce ti dang
