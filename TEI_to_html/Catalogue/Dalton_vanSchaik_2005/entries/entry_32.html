Section: 1
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @//he hon he hon/ge ge la ni khi la ni nya/
First item in a book of spells to be recited for various purposes. The present item, which seems to be incomplete at the beginning, includes at least eight mantras for stopping bleeding, curing illnesses, tumors and so on. The seventh mantra, for illnesses of the teeth, jaws, and throat, is followed by a brief ritual manual ( 1v.1-3 ).

Incipit ( 2r.3 ): @//://dkon cog gsum la phyag 'tshal lo//
