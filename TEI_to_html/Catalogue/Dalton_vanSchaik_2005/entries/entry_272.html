Instructions on making the sword for a statue of Mañjuśrī . The two folios directly preceding this item can be found at PT_82: 5-6 , though there the order of the two relevent folios have been reversed. Thus the Tibetan page numbers on the left margin should run: 'a-za-ya. The discussion of the statue ends on the verso of the present item, so that the next folio ( PT_82: 7r ; Tibetan number la) begins a new text on types of illness and their cures. The statue being described in the present item appears to be intended for installation in a cave ( PT_82: 6r.3 : ri’i phug na sku bzhugs pa), perhaps a reference to the cave temples near Dunhuang.
