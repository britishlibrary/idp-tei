This manuscript, a single panel from a concertina, belongs with ITJ_379_1 , ITJ_379_2 and PT_107 . See ITJ_379_1 for a discussion of the order of the original concertina.
This text begins on PT_107: verso and continues on ITJ_379_2: verso . The present manuscript contains a later section of the text on the recto (the verso is blank. The text, which appears in several versions in the Pelliot collection, is a sermon on fundamental Buddhist concepts of karma and rebirth. It does not appear in the bka' 'gyur .
