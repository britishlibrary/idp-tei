'phags ma par rna sha ba ri gzungs rigs sngags snying po dang bcas pa
Incipit ( Rf.1r.1 ): /_/tad tya tha'_/om+' mri te/o mrI te/
This concertina fragment contains the first half of the spell from the Parṇaśavari-nāma-dhāraṇī , with a few interlinear comments. It differs in some respects from the spell in the bka' 'gyur ( PT_186 ) , which is actually two spells separated by some tibetan text. Here the two are run together as a single spell.
