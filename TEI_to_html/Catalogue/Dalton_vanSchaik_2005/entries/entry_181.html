Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @//yongs su rdzogs par gyur cig /
This manuscript, incomplete at the beginning and end, contains a Mahāyāna prayer of aspiration and offering. Despite a lack of Vajrayāna terminology, the text does contain a "secret mantra " ( 4v.7 gsang sngags): oṃ sam b+ha ra bsam b+ha ra / bi ma na pa ra ma ha 'dzA b+ha hung //. The mantra is said to accomplish all of one's desires.
