'phags pa byams pa'I smon lam
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/_/'phags pa byams pa'I smon lam/_/sangs rgyas kun la phyag 'tshal lo/
A prayer to Maitreya . The colophon contains a dedication of merit by the monks from Kwa cu monastery. On this monastery, see Thomas_1955: 3-4 .
