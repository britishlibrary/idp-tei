This text exists in three other Dunhuang manuscript versions, all of which have been compared and discussed in Imaeda_1979 . All of the four copies of the text differ in some respects. The texts ITJ_420/1 (complete) and PT_37/1 (incomplete) share many similarities and may represent one version. All versions contain many irregularities in orthography.
The present fragment covers only the opening section of the text, a discourse on the inevitability of impermanence and death, and the rebirths resulting from good and bad actions, in the god realms and hell realms respectively.
