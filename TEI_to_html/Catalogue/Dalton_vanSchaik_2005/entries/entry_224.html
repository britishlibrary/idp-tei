'phags pa stobs po che zhes bya ba theg pa chen po'i mdo
[Ārya-mahābala-nāma-mahāyāna-sūtra]
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): /myI tshugs par 'gyur ro/_de nas bcom ldan 'das gyIs/
This folio corresponds to P_416: 128a.6-128b.7 of the canonical version. It is probably from the same original manuscript as ITJ_391 , which comprises folios 7-17 (according to the Tibetan numbering system.) The left side of this folio has been torn off, so the folio number is lost, but it must be one of the missing folios 1-6 of the original manuscript.
