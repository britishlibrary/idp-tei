'phags pa stobs po che zhes bya ba theg pa chen po'i mdo
[Ārya-mahābala-nāma-mahāyāna-sūtra]
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): /myI tshugs par 'gyur ro/_de nas bcom ldan 'das gyIs/
Incomplete manuscript including folios 7-17 according to the Tibetan numbering system. The translation differs in places from the canonical version. Compare P_416: 130a.1-136b.2 . An edition and translation of the text can be found in Bischoff_1956 . For more on this text, see entry to ITJ_390 .
One further folio from this text has been catalogued under a separate number ( ITJ_526 ). This folio corresponds to P_416: 128a.6-128b.7 , which makes it one of the missing folios 1-6 of the original manuscript.
