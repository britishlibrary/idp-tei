Section: 1
snang brgyad kyi mdo
[snang brgyad gyi mdo]
[sangs rgyas kyi chos gsal zhing yangs pa snang brgyad ces bya ba'i mdo]
[Aṣṭa-darśana-sūtra]
Incipit ( Rf.1r.1 ): dpa' thogs pa myed pa zhes bya ba yang/ 'khor der 'dus te 'dug go /
This is one of six incomplete copies of the Snang brgyad found in the Stein collection. For a discussion of the content, see entry to ITJ_463/1 . Only one folio is missing from the beginning, but then there is an immediate lacuna (probably of about 15 folio sides) between Rf.1r and Rf.1v (= Vf.5v and Rf.6r ). Most of the missing folios are found at ITJ_416/3 . The latter folios follow directly from the present Rf.1r , but there remains a lacuna between the end of ITJ_416/3: Vf.7r.3 and our Rf.1v . To reconstruct this lacuna, one may consult ITJ_458: 4v.3-8r.5 (=Tib. fol. nos. 5v.3-11r.5). There is also a lacuna between the end of the recto and the beginning of the verso that may be filled by ITJ_463/1: Vf.9v.3-Vf.11v.2 .

Explicit ( Vf.5v.3 ): snang brgyad kyI mdo sde bshad par bya'o// brgyad ces bya ba nI rnam
