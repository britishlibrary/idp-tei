Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/_/smon lam gdab pa /_/phyung po lngas ni rab rgyas pa/
A series of prayers for inviting in, and making offerings to, various deities including the buddhas, bodhisattvas, worldly gods and vow-bound protectors, kings of the four directions, and so forth. The title of each prayer is marked off by red strokes. For the titles and their Sanskrit reconstructions, see LVP: 376 .
