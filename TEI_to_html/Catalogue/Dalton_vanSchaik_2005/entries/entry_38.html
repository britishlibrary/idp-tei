Incipit ( 1r.1 ): /@/pad ma 'kha brgya rtsa brgyad pa la lha bkod pa'I brjed byang /
This item is divided into two sections. The first section is a mnemonic aide for how to arrange the deities on a 108-petalled maṇḍala . The second section (beginning on 1v.5 ) is a mnemonic aide for the mantra s and the mudrā s of those same deities. According to Kapstein, the maṇḍala in question derives from a Yoga tantra source, probably the Sarvadurgatipariśodhana-tantra (see Kapstein_2001 ). He also suggests that it may be related to the Na rag dong sprugs cycle of the later bka' ma teachings, attested as an icon in the temples of the Zur clan. Also related to this cycle is ITJ_584 . Another lotus mandala is found in the text contained in PT_353 and ITJ_507 .
