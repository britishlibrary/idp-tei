Section: 1
’phags pa sgo drug pa zhes bya ba'I gzungs
[Āryaṣaṇmukha-nāma-dhāraṇī]
Incipit ( Rf.1r.1 ): @/_/rgya gar skad du/a rya shan mu khI na ma dA ra NI/
This concertina manuscript is incomplete at the beginning and end. Almost all of the original manuscript can be reconstructed when ITJ_366 and PT_7 are joined to this manuscript. See the entry on ITJ_366 for a reconstruction of the original manuscript.
The first item on this manuscript is a complete copy of the Ṣaṇmukhī dhāraṇī . It differs little from the canonical version ( PT_312 ).
