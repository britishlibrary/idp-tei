Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/_/bcom ldan 'das ngag gi dpal la phyag 'tshal lo /_/nyungs 'dru gtsug du gnas pa las /
Written in same hand as ITJ_331 . A short Mahāyoga sādhana that seems to take as its starting point the sexual practice of the perfection stage ( rdzogs rim ), as is suggested by comparing the passage seen at ITJ_331/2: 4r.5-4v.4 . From this state one generates a visualized wrathful deity, and from this deity arise countless light-rays, filling all of space. As they regather, they grant a "supreme empowerment" ( 1r.4 dbang mchog). The text ends with the exhortation to "always hold to the sky-soaring accomplishment" ( 1r.5 mkha' lding bsgrub pa rtag tu 'chang).
Compare ITJ_401/6 which is a sādhana with a similar title, but otherwise little apparent relationship to this text.
