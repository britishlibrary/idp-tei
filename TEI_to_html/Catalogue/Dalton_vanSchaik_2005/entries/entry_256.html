Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @//dang sres la /myI gzugs brIs la/chu dmyIg du gdab'o/
Recto and verso probably reversed. One folio from what may be some sort of healing ritual. The text discusses how to determine the success of a rite based on which direction the person in question is facing, and then what to do to remedy a negative result. The remedy involves calling out to the person, mixing together herbs and applying them.

Explicit ( 1v.5 ): chu dmyIg byang shar 'tsham su ldas
