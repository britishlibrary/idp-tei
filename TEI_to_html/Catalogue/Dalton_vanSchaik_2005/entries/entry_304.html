Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/_/bla ma'I sa non bar gyur cIg /
A series of prayers, some of which are illegible due to damage. The first is a prayer for peace in the Tibetan kingdom and for long life and dignity for the king. This is immediately followed by a prayer for the enemies of the Vajrayāna to be obstructed, and then a prayer for all demonic beings to be subjugated through the four tantric activities ( 'phrin las rnam bzhi ). Reference is also made to the “supreme yoga” ( 1v.5 rnal ‘byor mchog).

Explicit ( 1v.5 ): yon bdag gi don du na las rnam bzhi ci brtsam ba yang gyur ba'I 'bras bu machis bar
