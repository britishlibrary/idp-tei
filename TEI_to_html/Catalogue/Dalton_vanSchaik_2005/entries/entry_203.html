Incipit ( 1r.1 ): /de ringx/_/ci 'dod sgrub oa 'khor gyi g.yang/
This single folio is a blockprint, much like those produced in China and Mongolia. It is clearly not one of the manuscripts from the Dunhuang library cave, and appears similar to the manuscripts found by Stein in Etsingol and Kharakhoto, catalogued under the shelfmark IOL Tib M. See also ITJ_765 .
