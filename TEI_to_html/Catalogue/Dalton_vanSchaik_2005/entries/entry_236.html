Incipit ( R1.1 ): @/_/'bruM byi sh+wa byi shud te
This small, complete scroll contains the mantra for the peaceful maṇḍala of the Guhyagarbha-tantra ( gsang ba snying po de kho na nyid nges pa NGB_417 ). The mantra is found in NGB_417: 172-3 . Under each part of the mantra , the name of the corresponding deity is written. The names of the deities match the forty-two deities found in the peaceful maṇḍala of the Guhyagarbha .
