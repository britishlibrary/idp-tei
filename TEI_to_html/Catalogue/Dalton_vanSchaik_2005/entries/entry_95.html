Section: 1
Incipit ( Vf.1r.1 ): chub sems dpa' mthu chen thob pa la phyag 'tshal lo/
This manuscript, in concertina form, belongs with ITJ_379_2 and PT_107 , as well as the single folio ITJ_285 . Thus reconstructed, the manuscript is still incomplete at the beginning and contains a lacuna in the middle. The order of the manuscripts is as follows:
Recto: [ ITJ_285: verso ]-[missing]-[ ITJ_379_2: verso ]-[ PT_107: recto ]-[missing]-[ ITJ_379_1: verso ].
Verso: [ ITJ_379_1: recto ]-[missing]-[ PT_107: verso ]-[ ITJ_379_2: recto ]-[missing]-[ ITJ_285: recto ].
The present item begins on ITJ_379_2: verso and runs through PT_107: recto and ITJ_379_1: verso where it concludes. It is a confession prayer, which mainly consists of homages to a series of buddhas, over 40 in number (rather than the 35 of the standard confession texts known in Tibet). There are also further homages to the dharma , and to the sangha .

Explicit ( Vf.3v.6 ): tsam ya bag tsam yang ma lus par sems dag pa nas 'thol lo bshags so//
