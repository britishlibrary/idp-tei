Section: 2
'phags pa rdo rje rnam par 'jom ba zhes bya ba 'a'i gzungs
[Ārya-vajra-vidāraṇa-nāma-dhāraṇī]
Incipit ( Vf.1v.1 ): rgya gar skad du/ a rya ba dzra bi dha ra ni//
LVP: 416 observes that this item is "incomplete at the end," but with ITJ_462/2 added to the end, it is complete. This version is almost identical to the translation (attributed to Jinamitra and Dānaśīla ) found in the canon. The few minor differences (such as the canonical edition reading 'jig par byed pa ( P_406: 60b.6 ) for 'jil bar byed pa ( Rf.2v.1 ), or adding 'byung po thams cad tshar gcod pa (compare P_406: 60b.8 to Rf.2r.4 ), and so forth) are worth noting however, for many of the earliest commentaries are missing these (later?) canonical additions. The commentary attributed to Padmasambhava is based on an edition bearing a particularly close resemblance to the present item (see, for the examples mentioned above, P_3503: 169a.2 and P_3503: 169a.8-169b.1 ). The commentary attributed to Buddhaguhya seems to fall somewhere between this and the canonical editions. (Though on these two particular examples, Buddhaguhya follows this Dunhuang version--see P_3504: 186b.3 and 3504: 186b.8 .)

Explicit ( Rf.7r.4 ): gtsang ma'i gos gyis dkri zhing dgang/
