Section: 1
[gtsug tor rnam par rgyal ba'i gzungs]
[Uṣṇīṣavijaya-dhāraṇī]
This single sheet contains the complete dhāraṇī spell from the Uṣṇīṣavijaya dhāraṇī (not the full dhāraṇī sutra). Other Dunhuang copies of the spell appear in ITJ_466/2 , ITJ_547 (a fragment), ITJ_1498 , ITJ_1771/1 , PT_72 and PT_73 . These differ in some respects from the spell found in the canonical version of the text ( P_197: 222a.7-222b.7 and P_198: 228a.6-228b.6 ). Compare also the Tibetan translations of the dhāraṇī spell in ITJ_348/3 and ITJ_322 . And for Dunhuang copies of the sutra, see PT_6 , PT_54 , and PT_368 .
