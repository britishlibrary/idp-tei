[Tathāgata-kāya-trayābhistotra]
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/:/de bzhin gshegs pa'I sku la bstod pa+' /
As LVP_350 notes, the full title of this work can be reconstructed on the basis of ITJ_360/5 . The present item is the first folio from a text the second folio of which can be found at PT_173/1 , as confirmed by comparing the break to the complete version of the same work found at PT360/5 . Note that the text probably ends on the missing third folio, while the fourth folio (see PT_173/2 ) begins a different text, a prayer to Uṣṇīṣasitātapatra titled Gtsug tor gzugs pa'i smon lam , that does not seem to appear in the canon nor in any other Dunhuang manuscripts. The present item includes several lines that do not appear in ITJ_360/5 , lines that were almost certainly added later as they do not obey the seven-syllable pattern of the other lines. Compare 1v.4 or PT_173/1: 1r.1 with ITJ_360/5: 46v.2 , and PT_173/1: 1v.1-2 with ITJ_360/5: 47v.1 . In terms of content, the praises focus on the inexpressible nature of buddha.
