Section: 2
byang chub tu spyod pa'i smon lam
[Bodhicaryā-praṇidhāna]
Incipit ( Rf.1v.1 ): ...skyabs su mchi /_/ngan song...
Four folios from a prayer that is the last text on a long concertina. Additional folios from this concertina are found at ITJ_103 and PT_53 . For a more extensive discussion of the concertina as a whole, see entries to ITJ_103 . LVP_134 mistakenly assumes that the present item is the same as the Bhadracaryā-praṇidhānarāja which immediately follows it in the original concertina. However, the present item is an incomplete copy of a different prayer. The folios should be read in the following order: [ Rf.1v-2r ]--[perhaps one folio missing]--[ PT_53D: r8 ]--[ Rf.2v-3r ]. Note that several lines from the prayer appear in Śāntideva 's Bodhicāryāvatāra .

Explicit ( Rf.3r ): /@/_/byang chub tu spyod pa'i smon lam rdzogs so/
