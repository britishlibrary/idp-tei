Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @/:/drag shul las la 'jug par bya'/
Incomplete, but appears to be part of PT_273 , which adds several folios. A brief discussion of the tantric activities, which normally number four (pacification, expansive, overpowering, and violence), but here are discussed as only three, with the activity of overpowering excluded. That said, the interlinear note to PT_273: 3r.2 does refer to the standard four activities ( las bzhi ). The present folio probably continues from the end of PT_273: 3v , as both discuss the clothing required for performing the different ritual activities. The verso of the present item goes on to discuss the preparation of the hearth for the fire offering (Tib. sbyin sreg ; San. homa ).
