Incipit ( 1r.1 ): /_/byang chub sems dpa' ta ho zag pa zad pa hwa//byang chub sems dpa' len na ker zag pa zad pa hwa/
This item is a list of the names of eight bodhisattvas. The names appear to be Tibetan transliterations of the Chinese, and the same list appears in the Dunhuang manuscripts of the Snang brgyad gyi mdo (compare, for example, ITJ_458: 13r.1 (=Tib. fol. no. 18r). The names are listed in the present item as follows:

( 1r.1 ) byang chub sems dpa’ pa ta ho zag pa zad pa hwa/ byang chub sems dpa’ len na ker zag pa zad pa hwa/ byang chub sems dpa’ ke ‘u ‘gog te ‘u zag pa zad pa hwa/ byang chub sems dpa’ su byin zhim zag pa zad pa hwa/ byang chub sems dpa’ na la yan zag pa zad pa hwa/ byang chub sems dpa’ in te dar zag pa zad pa hwa/ byang chub sems dpa’ hwa len de ‘u zag pa zad pa hwa/ byang chub sems dpa’ dmyigs pa rtog pa zag pa zad pa hwa/
Blank on verso. The second folio appears damaged (compare ITJ_305 ) and contains further scattered notes in the same hand.
