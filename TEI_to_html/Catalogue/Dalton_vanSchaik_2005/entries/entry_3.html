'phags pa shes rab kyi pha rol tu phyin pa'i tshul brgya lnga bcu pa
[Prajñāpāramitānaya-adhyardhaśatikā]
[Ārya-prajñāpāramitānayaśatapañcāśatikā]
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @//_//rgya gar skad du prad nya pa ra myI ta na ya a ru rta sha ti ka //
Complete copy of the influential tantra. The Dunhuang collections contain two distinct versions of the tantra, one that closely resembles the canonical and one that inserts a number of additional passages. The present manuscript is an example of the latter (compare for example the mantra added at 3r.10 to ITJ_97: 34v.3 and P_121: 281b.1 ) For a discussion of the two versions, see entry to ITJ_97 .
