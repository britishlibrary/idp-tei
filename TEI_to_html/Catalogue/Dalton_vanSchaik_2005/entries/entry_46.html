[Ārya-kanakavatī-nāma-dhāraṇī]
Incipit ( R1.1 ): sman thams chad dang/... stam thams chad las shin du 'da' par 'dod pas /
Incomplete at the beginning; end is present. Almost identical to the canonical edition, this item begins at P_314: 89a.7 . The dhārāṇī is taught by the Bhagavat as Vajrapāṇi , the leader of the yakṣa s, to Indra . It is recommended for kings and their ministers to use in war and other conflicts. Thus, for example, to achieve victory one should write down the spell and attach it to the top of one's banner (see lines 5-6 ).
