Three folios from the dhāraṇī . Title taken from content. This item belongs to a collection of dhāraṇī sutras, known as the Pañcarakṣā that is spread over several classmarks (see in order ITJ_399 , ITJ_397 , PT_535 , ITJ_398 , ITJ_388 , and ITJ_394 ). For more on this collection as a whole, see entry to ITJ_399 . The present text immediately follows ITJ_388 , except that two folios are missing from the beginning. Thus the first present folio corresponds to the canonical at P_181: 154b.1- . The second folio is consecutive, while the third corresponds to the canonical at P_181: 159b.3-160a.2 .
