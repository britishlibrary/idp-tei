Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @//dkon mchog gsum la phyag 'tshal lo/_/bcom ldan 'das la phyag 'tshal lo/
A Tibetan translation of the Uṣṇīṣavijaya-dhāraṇī spell (not the entire sutra). For a similar Tibetan translation of the same dhāraṇī spell, see ITJ_348/3 . Transliterations of the Sanskrit (spell only) appear in ITJ_466/2 , ITJ_547 , ITJ_1134 , ITJ_1498 , PT_72 and PT_73 . For the spell as it appears in the canonical sutras, compare P_197: 222a.7-222b.7 and P_198: 228a.6-228b.6 . And for Dunhuang copies of the sutra, see PT_6 , PT_54 , and PT_368 .
