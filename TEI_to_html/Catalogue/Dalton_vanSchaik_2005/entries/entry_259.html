Incomplete. First two folios of a treatise on "sprinkled offerings of purity" (gtsang gtor chag). The claim is made that these offerings were first taught by Śākyamuni , Avalokiteśvara , and Vajrapāṇi on the banks of the Nairañjana river. Same handwriting as ITJ_341 , ITJ_342 , ITJ_343 , ITJ_344 , all of which are about water offerings ( chu gtor ) and are in a similar style with lots of subdivisions. Here, the discussion is organized into three introductory topics and fourteen purposes.
