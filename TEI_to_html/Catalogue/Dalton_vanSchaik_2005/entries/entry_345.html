Incipit ( V1.1 ): @ srung ’i dam tshigs [...] la rdo rje slob dpon
This is a brief treatment of the samaya vows. Three vows, of body, speech and mind, are mentioned. The three vows are: [i] not ceasing the practice of yi dam , mantra and mūdra , [ii] not disseminating the secret instructions to others and [iii] not developing bad thoughts toward the vajra master or the vajra brothers and sisters. This text may be incomplete; a longer version is found in PT_269: recto. Also related is ITJ_718/1, which is a more developed treatment of the same three vows.
