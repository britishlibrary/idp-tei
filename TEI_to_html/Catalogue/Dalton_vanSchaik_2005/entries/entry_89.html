Section: 1
'phags pa rgyal mtshan gyi rtse mo'i dpung rgyan zhes bya ba'i gzungs
[Ārya-dhvaja-agrakeyūra-nāma-dhāraṇī]
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): du /A rya d+hwa ja a gra ke yU rA nA ma...
Fragmentary copy of a short dhāraṇī sutra, with beginning and end present. The Buddha teaches Indra the spell for protection from enemies and disturbances. The colophon, in agreement with the canonical colophon, says it was translated by Jinamitra , Dānaśīla and Ye she sde , and revised according to the new translation standards. The scribe is also named: mnon mtsho legs.

Explicit ( 1r.38 ): mnon mtsho legs gyi bris/
