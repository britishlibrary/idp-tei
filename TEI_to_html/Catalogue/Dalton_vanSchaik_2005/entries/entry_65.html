Section: 2
dam tshig bsrung ba
Incipit ( 1r.1 ): //@//dam tshig bsrung ba ni//dam rnam pa gsum dang/
A short work on the importance of keeping one's vows (Tib. dam tshig , Skt. samaya ). Three vows and four prohibitions (tshogs rnam bzhi) are listed.

Explicit ( 1r.4 ): 'di rnam dus thams cad du/myi nyams par bsrung ngo//

Section: 3
Incipit ( 1v.1 ): @/:/dkon mchog gsum la phyag 'tshal lo/_/bcom ldan 'das/khams gsum las khyad bar du 'phags pa la phyag 'tshal lo/
A Tibetan translation of the Uṣṇīṣavijaya dhāraṇī spell (not the entire sutra). For a similar Tibetan translation of the dhāraṇī spell, see ITJ_322 . Transliterations of the Sanskrit (spell only) appear in ITJ_466/2 , ITJ_547 , ITJ_1134 , ITJ_1498 , PT_72 , and PT_73 . For the spell in the canonical sutras, compare P_197: 222a.7-222b.7 and P_198: 228a.6-228b.6 . And for Dunhuang copies of the sutra, see PT_6 , PT_54 , and PT_368 .

Explicit ( 1v.7 ): de bzhin gshegs pa/thams cad kyi snying po byin kyis rlabs kyis byin du brlabs pa'/_/ bya phyag rgya chen po bzhi btsugs so. // legs par gsungs so //phyag rgya chen po/b+ha swa ha'//
