Section: 2
Incipit ( Vf.1r.1 ): jI lta bu { dgos } par bgyI//bcom ldan 'das kyIs bka' stsal pa//rIgs kyI bu ngo bo med pa ni/
A Mahāyāna treatise. It seems that something has changed since de la Vallée Poussin made his entry on this work (see LVP: 704 ). In particular, his fol. 13 seems to be missing, as there is no discussion of the kalyāṇamitra . Also, his folio 1 discussing the ālaya-vijñāna is now our folio Vf.7r . It is not clear why he recommends comparing this work to ITJ_706 ; no relationship is immediately obvious. Finally, "vanity" in his translation should be corrected to "emptiness."
