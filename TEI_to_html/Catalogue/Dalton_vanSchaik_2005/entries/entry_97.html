Incipit ( R1.1 ): man dzu shri/tan tra las 'byung ba/
Apparently extracted from a Mañjuśrī tantra , this is a story about a king named Tsa . In later Rnying ma sources, this is the same name used for the first human to receive the tantras (see Karmay_1981 ). Unfortunately, the item is torn in places and difficult to read. It appears that here too the king is associated with the tantras, but the story does not seem the same as the later ones involving this king.
