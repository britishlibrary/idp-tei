This copy of the Mañjuśrī-nāma-saṃgīti is incomplete at the beginning and end (although probably only two or three folios are missing in total). Like PT_99/2 it contains a prose section which follows the main verse section. The first folio begins at P_2: 2a.2 . The final folio ends at P_2: 15a.2 . For a full discussion of the Dunhuang versions of the Mañjuśrī-nāma-saṃgīti see ITJ_381 .
