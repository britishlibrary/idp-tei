Incipit ( 1r.1 ): @//_//ne s+ga ma hi li/
This short text provides a mantra for making a women give birth, as easily as the birth of a foal ( rte'u ). The same mantra can be used if one does not wish a women to give birth. The ritual also entails the application of dirt or animal dung.
