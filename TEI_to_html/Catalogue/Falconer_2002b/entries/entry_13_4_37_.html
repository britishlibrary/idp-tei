Stein Photo 13/4: Archaeological Survey of India, Frontier Circle photographs, 1911-1912
Collection of 37 half-plate printing-out paper prints of Archaeological Survey of India photographs, mostly numbered but uncaptioned, with accompanying sheet of paper titled &lsquo;Spare Frontier Photos 1912'. Captioned prints are enclosed in single quotes. Additional wrapping material has titles reading &lsquo;Half-plates not described' and &lsquo;2nd photo of Shewa Cromlech'. Some of this material is duplicated in the British Library's Archaeological Survey collections, but not all has been identified.
