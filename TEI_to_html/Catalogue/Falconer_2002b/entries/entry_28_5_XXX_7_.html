Plate XXV
Stein Photo 28/5: Illustration proofs for Wall Paintings from Ancient Shrines in Central Asia
The collection contains in addition 12 pencil sketches, listed below as numbered by Stein, followed by the actual plate number under which they appeared in the published volume:
35 gelatin silver prints and 12 sketches of various sizes, with instructions to printers regarding exact positioning and relative size. The plate numbers differ from those of the work as published in F.H. Andrews: Wall Paintings from Ancient Shrines in Central Asia (Oxford, 1948). The photographs show wall paintings from Bezeklik, with one from Balawaste (no. 35).
