Stein Photo 30: Indian and British views, 1880s-1890s
Green cloth-covered scrap album containing 25 commercially produced views of Indian architecture and English views, c.1880s-1890s. The Indian views, of architecture in Kashmir, Simla, Delhi, Jaipur and Agra, are the work of an unidentified professional photographer, and are uncaptioned apart from print 13, which is possibly one of Stein's own photographs. Most of the views of Oxford are captioned in the negative and the photographers can be identified by their signed initials.
