Stein Photo 13/1: 2nd Central Asian Expedition, 1906-1908
78 gelatin silver prints measuring 11.2x16.5 cm where not otherwise specified, contained in original envelope titled in blue pencil: &lsquo;Stein A. Photos of objects from collection of second expedition' and in red pencil &lsquo;Object Photos'. Some of these objects were photographed and reproduced in a different order and often from a different angle for Stein's Serindia (cited below as &lsquo;Ser.'). The plate references given below are not exhaustive, as not all objects could be identified. The photos are of good quality and in several cases details are much clearer than in the published versions. In a number of cases objects not published in Serindia can also be identified. Pieces published in Roderick Whitfield, The Art of Central Asia: The Stein Collection in the British Museum (3 vols, Kodansha, Tokyo, 1982-1985) are cited as ACA.
