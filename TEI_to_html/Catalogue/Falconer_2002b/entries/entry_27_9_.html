Stein Photo 27: Photograph album Belonging to A. Vambéry: views of Merv and Bukhara, c.1880s
Cloth-bound photograph album measuring 12x15.2 cm, stamped &lsquo;Photographs' on front cover, and containing 24 printing-out paper prints inserted in window mounts measuring 6.9x9.5 cm. The album is stamped &lsquo;Vambéry A. Budapest' and contains a letter from Wilhelm Hummel, probably the photographer, dated Marienstrasse, Weimar, 6 Jan 1899 attached to inside front cover. The photographs are captioned in pencil on the album page beneath each print.
