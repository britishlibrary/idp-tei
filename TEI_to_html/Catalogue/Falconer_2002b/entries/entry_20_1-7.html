Stein Photo 20/1-7: Photographs, 1923-1925
Seven cloth-bound albums with quarter-plate gelatin silver prints inserted in aperture mounts two to a page, all but a few titled with typescript captions. The albums are numbered on spine in Roman numerals I, II, IV, V, VII, VIII, XII. Captions in album XII (Stein Photo 20/7) are more sporadic.
