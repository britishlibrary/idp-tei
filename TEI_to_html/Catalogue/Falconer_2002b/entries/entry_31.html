Stein Photo 31: Miscellaneous original negatives, 1900s
A miscellaneous collection of original film and glass negatives, largely unidentified and generally of poor quality. Negatives 1-13 are &lsquo;B' series negatives, comprising B.i.1-B.i.12 and B.i.18. There are a further 18 quarter-plate film negatives of Indian architecture and 15 quarter-plate glass negatives, also of Indian subjects and including views at Elephanta, (?)Daulatabad, and the Residency at Lucknow.
