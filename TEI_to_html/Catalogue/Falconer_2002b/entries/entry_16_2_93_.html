Ganeshghati ridge from Kherigam. [R.037]
Stein Photo 16/2: Indian photographs, 1912-1916
Grey cloth-bound album, companion to Stein Photo 16/1 but title on spine illegible, containing 96 captioned quarter-plate gelatin silver prints inserted two to a page in aperture mounts and captioned in typescript. The subject matter relates mainly to Pandrethan. Some of this material is duplicated in the British Library's Stein Collection (OIOC Photo 392/50). All photos are captioned in typescript.
