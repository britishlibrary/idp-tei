Incantation
Block-printed sheet in 4 sections, each containing an identical Uighur dharani with glosses in Sanskrit in Gupta script. Ink on paper. Each leaf 26 x 11 cm. Block-printing allowed the rapid dissemination of a charm among an enormous number of people. This charm in Uighur contains an interlinear Sanskrit translation, to be read at 90' to the Uyghur. Dated 10th-11th century.
