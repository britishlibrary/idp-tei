Sutra of Golden Light
Scroll of Suvarnaprabhasasutra in Chinese. Ink on paper. Late 8th century AD. The protective idea of the mantra , advanced in the sutra, is given further significance in the colophon.

Colophon (End of text): The Buddhist disciple Lu, on behalf of her deceased ancestors of seven previous incarnations and her kinsfolk.... and because she herself has fallen into the hands of foreign barbarians [Tibetans],has reverently caused one roll of the sutra to be copied, praying that the two countries [ ChinaandTibet] may live in amity, that hostilities may ceased and that all homeless people may speedily reach their native villages.
