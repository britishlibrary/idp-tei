Upper right portion. R1 says "Enemies having appeared at [. . .], soldiers assembled and moved to'U-ten(= Khotan), then reached the enemies' rear." A phrase in r2snga-skyIn-du 'tshal-te"borrowed as advance loan" suggests that the text is a loan contract, but the rest of the text does not conform to the contract formula. Personal names and district names in probably the Khotan area are mentioned in r3.
