Three pieces of the same ms. Frags. c. and b. have been joined together; there is a small gap between frags. a. and c. The reconstructed text thus reunited is a nearly complete pothī folio of theSo-sor thar-pa'i mdo. The text corresponds to Stein Dunhuang texts,LVP_1962: 5(vol. 72) foll. 19b10–20a3, andLVP_1962: 8(vol. 12) foll. ka-22b2–5; also with minor differences toLVP_1962: 6(vol. 24) foll. 15[=pha]b2–16[=ba]a3,LVP_1962: 7(vol. 61) foll. 36.1–37.4, and toQ_1031: Che 9b5–10a4;Ui_1934: 2, 11a2–11b2. Additional glosses in smaller characters are written interlinearly. Two holes for binding are encircled in red.
