Tibetan text, written below a line of Khotanese, seems to be the beginning of a letter by a Khotanese high-ranking official, residing probably in Khotan, and addressed to the highest civil official ofShin(=Shing)-shan, who was probably a Tibetan. Text reads "A-ma-cha Yongrequests to thertse-rjeofShin-shan." The expression "thertse-rjeofShin-shan" suggests that there were certain inhabitants inMazār Tāghgoverned by artse-rjeor town prefect. Khotanese writing may be an instruction about delivery (seeBailey_1945-1963: 5:204andThomas_1951: 199).
