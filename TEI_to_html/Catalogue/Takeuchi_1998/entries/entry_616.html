a folio from concertina?, complete

A folio probably from concertina or pothī. Recto and verso are similar in style, both ruled with red lines. Recto describes physical and mental features [of a saint], such as nose, mouth (r1), tongue, throat (r2), mind, legs (r3), and hands (r4); the upper part isVairocana, and the lower part isAvalokiteśvara(r4).
