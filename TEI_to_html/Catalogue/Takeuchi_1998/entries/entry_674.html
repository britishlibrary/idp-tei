Probably a stamp likeYedharmā mantra(Texts 672 and 673). The original text seems to have been divided into two parts, and only the right part remains. R1 is no doubt the continuation of the last line of the left part. R1 mentionsLha'i dbang-po Brgya-byin"Indra." R2 containsgser-zug chen-po"great pain"; r3,nyams-su myong"experience, feel." The print probably dates a little later than the Old Tibetan period.
