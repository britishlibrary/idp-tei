frag (T, L, B)

Two frags. (E. i. 31. b. and E. i. 29.) are pieces of the same ms. and have been loosely joined together. The ms. is reunited by a slip of coarse brownish paper daubed with red paint, especially noticeable on verso. The text, originally consisting of two lines, seems to be a dispatch. R2 says "If [the paper?] does not reach [you] tomorrow because of the heat, go outside to receive it tomorrow[sic]!" This interepretation however remains tentative. Published as Text B inStein_1907.
