Five Tibetan frags. and one Chinese frag. have been placed in a sack with the requisition number Or.8212/1834. No site numbers are written on the frags. Two paper slips in the sack contain site numbers "LA.VI.ii.0221-227" and "Centre xv.," but neither seem to apply to the Tibetan frags. Three frags. have been joined together (Text 677). One frag. seems to have been written in the Khotan area (= Text 359). Text 676, the smallest piece, is the upper middle portion of probably a letter.
