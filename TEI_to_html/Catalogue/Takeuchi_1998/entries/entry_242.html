Two frags. have been joined together. The reconstructed text is still incomplete, much smudged and not clearly legible. It is likely to be a divination by dream (cf. Pelliot 55). The Tibetan text is written on the back side of a Chinese text (Chavannes_1913: 959andChavannes_1913: 960). The reconstructed Chinese text (16 ll.) is a frag. of妙法蓮華經卷第三、授記品第六(Saddharmapuṇdarīka, Chap.6,Vyākaraṇa), which corresponds toT: vol. 9: 21c, ll.2–22.
