Right half of a folio, similar in style to Texts 571, 572, 574–576. Recto may be part ofGser-'od dam-pa'i rnga-sgrabut not located in parallel Dunhuang texts. Another Buddhist text on verso mentions BuddhaRnam-par snang-mdzad(v1) and fiveTathāgatas (v4), and resembles Text 571 verso. Texts 571 and 577 probably belong to the same ms., but whether they belong with Texts 572, 574–576 cannot be confirmed.
