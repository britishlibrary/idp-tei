Three frags. have been joined together to form a single piece. The joined paper still lacks its right edge. The text is probably a report or request concerning the assignment of ranks to local officials of the district calledSka-ba bong-yul. Titles such as,gser-gyi yi-ge ched-po,dngul-gyi yi-ge ched-po, andzangs-gyi yi-ge, and names of officials are found. The text likely continues from recto to verso, and ends at v7 with the phrasedgod-pa[r] gsol"[I thus humbly] request to issue," leaving the lower part blank. Writing on verso is faint and hardly legible, except for a few personal names, includingStag-gtsug-bzhe[r]andLha-dpal-stag.
