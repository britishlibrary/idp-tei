Left portion; badly torn and writing indistinct. A title "governor (dbang-po) ofKhams" is mentioned in r2; r3 may mean "having been enthroned"; r4 seems to say "do whatever; seize the realm." Francke gives the governor's name as "Sum-bzher, the royal minister." Francke probably readblon rgyal sum bzher, namely,blonRgyal-sum-bzher; butsum-bzheris not legible from the present text. Writing style is similar to that of Text 654, but a bit larger. There seem to be four lines on verso, but very faint and hardly legible, except for a few syllables given here.
