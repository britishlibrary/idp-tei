Right half of a letter fromLegs-tshan. The form of address on verso indicates that it is addressed to LordStag rgyal-bzher; he is referred to asjo-bo lha-dpalin r1. R3 says "[I am] sending five large and smallleb'slatesorribbons' toTshes-lod," probably the wife ofStag rgyal-bzher(Thomas_1951: 381). In r4 the sender wishes to set eyes upon the auspicious face of the addressee.
