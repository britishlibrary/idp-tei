Recto is a letter fromStag-legsto blonSkyes-bzang. The right bottom corner has been cut off to 2.5 x 15.5 cm as a tally; the upper part of a few oblique lines appear above the cut off. The letter concerns the sending of medicine, an excuse for the delay, and instructions on how to take the medicine. Verso is another letter petition from the people of Lang clan (lang-myi) toGung khrI-bzher. The first three lines are partly cut off. Below the text another line is written upside down, probably the form of address of the petition. The whole text is crossed out with oblique lines in order to write another letter on recto.
