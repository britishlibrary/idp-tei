Upper? middle portion. Paper seems to have been partly burned. It was placed with seven Sanskrit frags. in a sack. The note contained in the sack designates the site numbers Khad. 029. 1. through 8., but the numbers are not written on the frags. Stein describes Khad. 029. as "This packet contains only small frs., often much crushed, of at least 8-9MSS. Writing generally of Brahmi script, but in some cases appears to be different. The frs. are so small that nothing definite seems dicoverable. Some frs. show traces of fire." (Stein_1928: 1021)
