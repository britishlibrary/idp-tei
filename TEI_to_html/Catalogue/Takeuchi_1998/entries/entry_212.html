Left half of a letter fromDu dun-skyestozhang-zhang blonKhri-bzherandnang-rje-poLha-bzang. The sender's name,Du dun-skyes, is visible on verso as part of the form of address. Place names in the Khotan area,Zhugs-ngam,'U-ten, andPe'u-tse, are mentioned. The letter seems to talk of a situation in which the sender wishes his military duty of hill stationing (ri-zug?) to be changed due to illness, and begs for generosity from the addressees.
