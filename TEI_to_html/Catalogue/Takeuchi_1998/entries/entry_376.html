Two frags. are apparently from the same ms.; M. I. i. 25. D. forms the upper left corner, and M. I. i. 25. E. may be placed to the right of it. Due to a gap in between, the two cannot be joined. Recto is a document beginning with the datestagi lo"tiger year"; the rest of the text is very faint and hardly legible, except for a few disconnected words such as,lan,'bras, andmjal. Verso is the upper right part of another document, written by a different hand. Faint and indistinct; only a few phrases such as,lan la / pang-tshab(v1),byi-ba lo'i,sla ra-ba(v2), can be made out.
