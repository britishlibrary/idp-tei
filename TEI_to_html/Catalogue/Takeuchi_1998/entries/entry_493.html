Recto corresponds to a Stein Dunhuang text,LVP_1962: 96= Ch. 9. I. 45. (vol. 42) fol. 2b6–10; also with minor differences toLVP_1962: 97(vol. 38) foll. 25b1–28b1,LVP_1962: 102(vol. 63) foll. 16a1-18a4, and toQ_121, Ta 280a3-280b2. Verso corresponds toLVP_1962: 96(vol. 42) fol. 3b3–8; and with minor differences toLVP_1962: 97(vol. 38) foll. 38a1–40b3 and toQ_121: Ta 281b5–282a3. Cf. Text 651.
