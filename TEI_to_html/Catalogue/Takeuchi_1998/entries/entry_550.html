Texts 550–553 are pieces from the same roll bearingAparimitāyur-nāma-sūtraon recto. Texts 550 and 553 form the beginning first column (Text 553 fills the missing left end in Text 550). The Sanskrit title (r1 in Text 550) seems to be missing due to paper damage. Text 551 and Text 552 each forms the second and the third column. Each column seems to have originally consisted of 18 lines. The reunited text from Texts 550 and 553 is given here. Text 550 verso has only two syllables of a personal name; probably a scribal exercise, written by the same hand as Text 551 verso.
