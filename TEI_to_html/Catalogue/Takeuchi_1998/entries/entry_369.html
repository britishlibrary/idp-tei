Recto is the upper right corner of a contract for loaning barley. R1–r3 mention "when [we] counted the provisions, there was great shortage of barley; so it was decided to loan the barley." R7 gives the date "middle spring month of the ox year." On verso are six lines of calligraphic writing, with short phrases regardingngo"side,prospect" written between the lines. The phrases, such assnying-dga'-ba'i ngo,ngo-bzang,grog-kyi ngo,skug-bla,bla-phyugs, suggest that verso is a kind of divination, but may be scribbling.
