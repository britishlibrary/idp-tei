Nine small frags. previously placed in a sack with a slip recording site numbers Mr-tagh [= M.Tagh.] 0408–0428. No site number appears on each frag. Four pieces (two each) have been joined to form two frags., thus reducing the overall number to seven. Since M.Tagh. 0410–0428. are used to designate other mss. or materials, these frags. are here designated as M.Tagh. 0408 a–f. Each frag. is described under a different text number.
