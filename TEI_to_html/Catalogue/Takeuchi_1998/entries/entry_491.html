pothī, frag (T, L, R)

Two frags., once glued together to form recto and verso of a large pothī with two holes for binding, have now been detached and placed on a large sheet. They are mistakenly located together with the Dunhuang mss. in volume 72. Four or five lines seem to be missing in the lower part of recto (= the upper frag. in Plates) and the upper part of verso (= the lower frag.). The back sides of the frags. seem to be blank. Lines are ruled. The title'Gyod tshangs dang smon lamis found in r1 and v2, but the text does not agree with the Dunhuang mss. bearing the same or similar title:LVP_1962: 208.2,LVP_1962: 209,LVP_1962: 210,LVP_1962: 247,LVP_1962: 452.2;PT_17,PT_18,PT_24,PT_175-177.
