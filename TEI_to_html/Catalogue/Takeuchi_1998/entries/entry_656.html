Middle part of a pothī folio. A comparison withStein_1921: Pl. CLXXshows that the left edge is missing and writing worn in the present ms. Francke mentions that the page number 53 is given on the left side, but this no longer visible. The recto text given here is reconstructed from the Serindia plate, while the verso is from the present text. V4 mentions "a stupa and offering of butter lamps."Sha-ri-bu(i.e.,Śāriputra) is found in r2. V4 mentions the offering of butter-lamp stupas.
