frag (T, L)

Recto is a contract for loaning barley and wheat (cf.Takeuchi_1995: Text 24). Verso is an instruction on the collection of repayment. The bottom edge of the paper is torn off. Thus, the lower part of recto (lines below r4) and the upper part of verso (above v1) are missing. A round vermilion seal is faintly visible on the right end of r2, and also to the right of v4. According to Francke, this ms. was rolled round a small stick.
