Two frags. bear the same site number. According to Francke, they were previously pasted to one another, but their relative position is unknown. Possibly written by the same hand, they are not likely to form a single piece. Smaller frag. (text a) speaks of the banning by mantra of eating evil foods, such as arrow-pierced foods (mda's-pa?), blood (khrag), marrowbones (rkang), flesh (sha), fat (tshil), impurities (myi-gtsang-ba), etc. Larger frag. (text b) contains combinations of small circles, suggesting divination with dice, but the text, written in verse, is illegible in parts.
