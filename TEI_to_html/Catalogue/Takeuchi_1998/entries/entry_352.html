Contains traces of three or four characters, such aslaandga; very faint and illegible. Since it has been placed with other paper documents, its material has been considered to be paper; but it is actually a very thin (less than 1 mm) piece of wood. The ms. should therefore be re-registered in the forthcoming catalogue of wooden slips.
