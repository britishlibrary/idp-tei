Two Buddhist verses (r1–r7, r8–r17), written byGnam ched-pho(r18). Published inStein_1907by Barnett with the help of Francke, where the verses are considered to have been taken from theTheg-mchog-mdzod, a 14 c. encyclopaedic work byKlong-chen rab-'byams. However, this ms. is apparently older than the latter from both palaeographic and linguistic aspects. The identified passages, r1–r3 of the first verse andTheg-mchog-mdzod43a2 (wrongly given as 44 in Das: 653), seem to have been taken independently fromNāgārjuna'sMūlamadhyamaka-kārikā(§17, verse 33).
