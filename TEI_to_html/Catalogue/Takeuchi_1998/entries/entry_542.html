r 10 + seals, v 14 + 1(inv) + seals

Contains two different documents, one on each side. Upper edge has been torn off. The beginning few lines of the verso text are missing, but the recto text starts below the missing part and is complete. Evidently recto was written after verso. Both texts are apparently not drafts but original documents. Recto is a contract for selling yaks (seeTakeuchi_1995: Text 42). Verso is a petition from a courier namedLda-ka wa-lbatortse rjeand other officials inLittle Nob.Lda-ka's hand-letter-measure is written inverted below v13. Four round vermillion seals of witnessees (zhang-lonofficials) appear near the bottom edge. Cf. text 518.
