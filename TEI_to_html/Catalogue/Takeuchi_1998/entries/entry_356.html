Two small frags., each containing only a few syllables. The larger one has the beginning of probably a letter draft on recto (ra1). There seem to be a few Tibetan characters on verso, but too faint to ascertain. The smaller piece is probably part of another letter (rb1–3); unidentified scribbling on verso. Since no site numbers are written on them, the site number for each frag. cannot be specified, except that they belong to between Har. 072. 12. and Har. 072. 19., as designated by the attached slip.
