Middle portion of probably a roll. Two pages or columns separated by two vertical lines remain partly on recto; two lines on the left page (ra1–ra2) and six lines on the right page (rb1–rb6) are written in an upright sutra style. Text resembles and possibly belongs with Texts 610 and 611 recto, which partly corresponds to theLaṅkāvatāra-sūtra. Verso also consists of two pages, but in a more cursive style.chos-gyI mtshan-nyid(vb5) andsku gsung thugs gyi rang-bzhin(vb10) are mentioned.
