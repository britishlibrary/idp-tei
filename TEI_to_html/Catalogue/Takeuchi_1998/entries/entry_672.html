Two pieces of the same blockprint known asYedharmā mantraorDharmakāya (śarīra) kārikā(Takeuchi_1990a: 210–211). Probably found from the layers of débris embedded in sand near the stupa base ofKichik-hassārnear the south-east corner of the Turfan basin (Stein_1921: 1166). Many prints of the same mantra are found in the German Collection (Taube_1980: Text 49), the Otani Collection ("Otani": Texts 6027–6070), and the Malov Collection. Cf. Text 673.
