Recto is the left half of a sale contract. The objects for sale are a saber and something else unidentified. The seller's name isLi mar-pon, a Khotanese belonging to the parish ofZhum-ba. The buyer, whose name is missing due to paper damage, belongs to theYang-rtsangregiment.Thomas_1951gives the wrong site number M.Tagh. 0500. Verso is the right half of a military document. Many personal names and place names in the Khotan area are listed. Cf. Texts 247 and 309.
