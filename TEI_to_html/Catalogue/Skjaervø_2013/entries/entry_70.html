translation:
Thus I have heard, as (=when, how) the Lord Buddha formerly went in (his) career as far as the land of Vaiśālī; he stayed(?) in the Mrakāṭikā grove. 1.
(This) grove was very nice, pleasant, fragrant, very peaceful. There were nice dwellings of various kinds, just as was worthy of the Buddha. Springs, lakes, lotus ponds, pools, fountains, reservoirs (with) fresh water, cool, pure, tasty, quick(?), fragrant, lotuses, utpalas, kumudas, sūmana, caṃbā[ṭala ...].
