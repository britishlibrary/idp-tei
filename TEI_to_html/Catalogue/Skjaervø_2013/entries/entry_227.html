(1) The spāta Sudārrjāṃ orders thus: To the President of the Year [the pharṣa Sāṃdara].
(2) Now, an order has come to me from the gracious Lord *regarding the *fulfillment. One man [must deliver] a cloth [x chās] (3) long, 2 chās wide. When you hear the order, within ten days gather those cloths, four ... (4) request ... for me. If you have the full 23 chās, then one (man) [must deliver?] *per capita ... (5) six, so that* all the grain can be delivered within this month.
On the 12[th day] of Ttuṃjāra the order went out [to you]. SIGN
