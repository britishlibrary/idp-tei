translation:
[69] (143) And the Linggong had deigned give us a winter coat each. And we had nothing else, so (144) we gave them four coats. Then Ana the Younger said to us: (145) “That was too little! Put (some) underwear (on top of the pile)!”
[70] We said to him: “First (146) show us the order!” And so he showed (us) the order. And the order to us was thus: “In whatever manner (by whatever expedient) they can go (147) they shall go!”
[71] And he did not give them the coats. And he said to them thus: “How is it that you did not go to China?” And then (148) the Linggong *in the meantime had deigned give us one *soft coat. And he said to us thus: “Give me that garment!” (149) And we did not give (it) to him. And he said to us thus: “Now I’ll just throw you out!”
[72] And then we said: (150) “We are going!” We went hither to China. And (first) we said: “Send the information to them—we are going” (151) And they sent the information.
[73] And there the Linggong deigned utter: “If the three monks have left (and) in the seventh month (152) no word has come at all to us, those (with you) shall no more be sent!”
[74] And when they (we?) learned: “the Linggong has not deigned (153) send (any more), and the Linggong has heard about these coats and has become angry,” and in the third month (154) he gave it to us/them. And then {he said to us} he *chastized [note a: See Kumamoto, Studies II, p. 137.] many of us/them here.
[75] And then there was here a reverend one from the Gūmattīra (monastery), (155) a certain Dharmaidrākarapūña. And he settled his wife there. And at present whatever things there were worth even a single copper mūrā (156) that he took for her(?): one roll of cloth, one pair of trousers that he had, and a *coat of 20 chās, and (157) one phaurtaka coat, one *goat fur coat(?) worth one roll of cloth. And then (158) he made a *sworn statement of it for you in the (Golden) Land: “one thick blanket.”
[76] And then here (there was) a certain Ganzhou’ese [note a: See Emmerick, 1985b, pp. 307-8.] Sīca Yavanaindraja (159). And him he led away to you. He made a *sworn statement to the (Golden) Land: (160) “fifteen blankets.”
