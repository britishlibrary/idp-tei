translation:
(1) In the 20th year, on the tenth day of the month of Kaja. This promissory note [note a: Cf. Hedin 4.2 KT IV, p. 23 ṣiʾ pāra-vastū pīḍakä ttye pracaina [cu].] (2) (is) for this reason that Hvā Cai Sai had the (following) mūrās *outstanding. These were the ones (3) who requested the mūrās. He had promised Hatkaṃ 125 mūrās. He had promised the spāta (4) Sīḍaka 125. He had promised Pheṃdūka 125 mūrās, Alttāṃ (5) 125, Budarśaṃ 125, Mayadatta 125, Rruhadatta (6) he had promised [...]. (7) [If ...] can not deliver the cloths, then he [shall pay] interest (8) ... 100 mūrās.
Alttāṃ’s finger (mark), Pheṃdūka’s finger (mark), Hatkaṃ’s finger, Budarśaṃ’s finger, Rruhadatta’s finger, Jsajsaka’s finger.
