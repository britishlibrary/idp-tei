translation:
(1) ... comes ... Now I have heard ... not only this (?) ... (2) ... [gave an order]: “Send me some sesame oil here at once!” I am really weak in winter, and I do not yet find strength. ... (3) I now have (some) warm, cooked ones. You shall buy as much as six ... nice oil for me ... (4) ... and you shall see to it that you ask for a little there so much (?). On the 10th day of Haṃdyaja [the order went out] to you.
