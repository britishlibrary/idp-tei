(4) If it pleases (one) to do homage to these *teachers [BSkt. gaḍin?], then one should go in reverence to the lord buddhas and (5) the bodhisattvas, and to Śakra, the sons of gods, (6) the asuras, and the other great mighty kings. Together with the noble ones of pure morals (śīla) (8) there ... the one dwelling according to the method, ask him(?). If (9) now today, he were to ask those gaḍanas, I ask (10) according to (my) own wish, according as it may be here in my mind, not solely (11) for the sake of my life, if I ask that, however great (12) it may be, deign to teach me in detail(?).
