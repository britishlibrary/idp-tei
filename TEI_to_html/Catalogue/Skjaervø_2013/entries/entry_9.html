Or.6396.1: KT V, p. 4 (8)

Section: 1
Vouchers issued by the spāta Sīḍaka dated in the 20th year (of Väśaʾ Vāhaṃ = 782). Hoernle, 1901, p. 39.

translation:
(1) Year 20, 20 days of the month Rrāhaja.
This voucher (was) for this reason that the spāta (2) Sīḍaka has collected the sesame of the merchant Suhadatta. (3) And the ttucus (?) took the mūrās instead of .... And so this Aśäñekūli from Cira received (it). (4) He received *in person the sesame by ṣaṃga: twelve kūsas three ṣaṃgas. (5) And Gvīlāṃ Tcyāṃ’s debt he took from Svasū (in) sesame: (6) 17 ṣaṃgas—and 4 pieces of cloth.
And this writ (7) will take effect when Aśäñekūli puts (his) finger on it. — (8) Aśäñai Kūli’s finger.

transliteration:
l.9: @ kṣā[ṃ]ṇ[ä] 20 mye salye hārū saleṃgari spāta sīḍakina
l.10: kāṃha thauna nātä drrairatcahauʾsi rrāhaji māśtä 20 6 mye
l.11: haḍai tī ri ṣiʾ kṣau prąmāṃ hämi khu hā saleṃgari akṣari niśä [note a: B.: for viśtä, but there is no -t-; cf. Ch.0048.4 (IOL Khot S. 6), Or.8212/162 line 138.]
SIGNUM

translation:
(9) In the regnal year, the 20th year, the merchant Saleṃgara received from the spāta Sīḍaka (10) 43 hemp cloths, on the 26th day of the month of Rrāhaja.
(11) And this writ will take effect when Saleṃgara [note a: Also Hedin 53b1, SI P 95.5, 9, 96.4. $$] puts (his) letter on it.
