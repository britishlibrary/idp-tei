translation:
(a1) The reverend *Śikṣabhadra(?) and (his/my) boy *Śalyāsa I, the dvaipiṭika Javendraśikṣa(?), ask how well it is (for them and whether they are) healthy. I here with (2) mother reckon it a blessing. But for us it is not so well and healthy. When you were here and went away you said: (3) “I will come back here,” but so far you have not come back to us. Think about this: When you went away the care for the boy was not so good as ... (4) So then, come back to us. Perhaps we can settle the matter. You bring the boy to us (5) to (this) land. Afterward I shall *put him in the *census register(?).
Of the month of Mūñaṃja the thirteenth (6) day.
(b1) To be delivered to the reverend *Śikṣabhadra.
