Hoernle 7: KT II, p. 66 (30); SD II/XXVIII (2); SD, TV, p. 54

Section: 1
Doc. of purchase (gärya-vāḍa) dated in year 20 of Väśaʾ Vāhaṃ (= 782), 3rd year of the ṣṣau Vädyadatta. Hoernle, 1897, pl. v, no. viii. In KT V, p. 5 (10) and SD, TV, p. 54, this doc. is referred to as “Or.6397.1” and “Or 6397,1 = H 7.”

translation:
(1) Year 20, 13 days of the month of Cvātaja, of this king of Khotan, Viśaʾ Vāhaṃ.
In the third (year) of the ṣṣau (2) Vidyadatta, [note a: Also dated in a year of the ṣṣau Vidyadatta are SI P 103.26 and perhaps SI P 93.3.] (this) document of purchase (was) for this reason that Bugura bought three kūśeṃ (?) of earth from Maṃdūsa [note b: Cf. Or.6401/2.3, Or.6402B/2.1.] unmeasured (3) in *Gaṃtsakūḍai for a period of four autumns at the price of 1000 mūrās. The following are *co-terminous of that earth: (4) From the *water (it is) Puñavärṣa’s (land). From the ... (is) Sanekula’s man Kaḍaka’s (land). (5) ... if Bugura has not sown in his plot—if then Maṃdūsa within the four-autumn period (6) buys it back, he shall buy (it) back. Older (then that) it can not be bought back.
And this writ shall take effect (7) when Maṃdūsa puts his mark on it.
These were the witnesses: Witness Arsalaṃ. Witness the parramai (8) Buttąnaka. Witness *Bauḍa. Maṃdūsa’s finger (mark).
