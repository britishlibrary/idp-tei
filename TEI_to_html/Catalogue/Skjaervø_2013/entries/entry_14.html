(1) Year 5, 1x days of the month of Sijsīṃjsa, in the year of the ṣṣau Śāṃdatta. [note a: Cf. SI P 99.3, dated in year 4, the year of the ṣṣau Śāṃdatta. A Śāṃdatta is mentioned repeatedly in Or.11252 and Or.11344.]
The spāta (2) Malārrjuṃ [note b: A Malārrjāṃ is mentioned in IOL Khot W 1-2 and Ṣṣanīra son of Malārrjuṃ(?) in Or.9268A.] of Gaysāta has delivered kūras for the amount of the debt, twenty thousanders, eight hundred (= 20,800). In that count (3) were *5,800 ... together with .... and together with Saṃganaṃda ... (4) ... he ... owed cloths worth 150 and two chās of woolen (cloths). ... (5) ... the debt ...
