translation:
l.r25–28: A man is born in the Year of the Hare. He will be altogether happy. He will be altogether blessed with money, cattle, horses, and all [blessings?]. And he will not have to go far away. And on him there will be a lot of itching. And when he sows harvest it will not be good for him. And he will be evil in tongue. And he will be evil in mind. He will die of the grasp (of an evil planet).
