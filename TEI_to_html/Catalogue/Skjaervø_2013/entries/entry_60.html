translation:
(1) In the year of the ṣṣau Ṣacū. The cloths of the first paṃda: (2) And he delivered 2 cloths of nine chās.
Khattīnai had (3) 2 cloths and 200 mūrās and an ox, five cloths.
And (4) Puñargaṃ delivered 6 cloths of 11 chās.
(5) Pheṃdūka delivered 11 cloths of 11 chās and (6) ... one sheep, 250 mūrā and 4 she-goats and there were four (7) ... sheep *aged 4 ...
The voucher ... 1000 ...
(8) On the 29th day of Muñaṃja, Pheṃdūka (9) delivered 15 cloths.
Khattīnai delivered [x] cloths.
(10) In the month of Cvātaja, Khattīnai [delivered] ... cloth(s?) (11) ... 2000 *mūrās ...
Pheṃdūka [delivered] ... cloth(s?) ...
