l.1: @ skarähvęrä māśtä {śtä} hvurrä dū pamūhaja thona hauḍiʾ dva pātcä th[au]

(1) In the month of Skarihvāra Hvurrä Dū delivered two cloths for garments. Afterward (2) he delivered one cloth, because he had sold barley.
