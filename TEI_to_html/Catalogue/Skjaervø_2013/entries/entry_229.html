Or.11252.23: KT II, p. 24 (4)

Section: 1
The same (or related) transaction as in Or.11344/14.

transliteration:
l.1: tti vā drrai ysāri dvī-sa-haṣṭāyī haṃbā mūri puḍa
l.2: upadatti haṃtsa yąnaki hīyau mūryau jsa mūri puḍi tcaʾhauʾ ysāri 40
l.3: yaudari u mūri puḍi paṃ-se 20
l.4: cira hvrrīviṭi dasau ysāʾca sa caʾhausä
l.5: makali mūri vāra dvī ysā hauda-se tcaʾhausä

translation:
(1) The following have promised me mūrās in the amount (of) 3,280:
(2) Upadatta together with Yąnaka’s mūrās promised 4,004 mūrās.
(3) Yaudara U promised 520 mūrās.
(4) In Cira, Hvrrīviṭi (promised) 10,140. (5) Makala owes 2,740 mūrās.
