Or.11252.26: KT II, p. 24 (4)

Section: 1
Ductus like the following.

transliteration:
l.1: tti vā rarūyi māśtä spaśaña tsvādi kharajsajsi || suhadāysi || saṃgade || virgāṃ || /
l.2: saloki [note a: The -o is attached to the top of the l- here and in the next text.]
|| sūradatti || kūcalai || sudatti paʾji

(space)

translation:
(1) The following went on inspection in the month of Rarūya: Kharajsajsa, Suhadāysa, Saṃgade, Virgāṃ, (2) Saloka, Sūradatta, Kucalai, Sudatta from Paʾ.



(OS)
