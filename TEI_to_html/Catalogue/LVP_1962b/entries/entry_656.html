Ch. 03. 22, 03. 31, 03. 44, 51. I. 34, Pothī; 27.2 c. x 7.5 c.: foll. 9; ll. 4: dbu-can.
A sort of Catechism (compare 698-9), Vijñāna-vādin: incomplete and defective. A number of paragraphs, some introduced by questions, dealing inter alia with Pratītya-samutpāda, the four Māras, the four Truths, the ten good acts, the ten evil acts, the saṃskṛtas, the four asaṃskṛtas, the five states (gati) and the acts leading thereto.
