'This son of mine, who has made Suvarṇanāga Suvarṇaprabhāsa his Kalyāṇamitra in time past, may be born in all his births in my house and obtain with [me] the prediction (luṅ) of supreme Enlightenment. All creatures who, being without refuge and protector, are suffering many sufferings a long time, may they, in the future, find in me refuge and protector and always enjoy happiness. Owing to the merit of this Suvarṇa-prabhāsa prayer....'
