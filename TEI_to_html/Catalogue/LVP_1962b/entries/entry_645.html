Title from the colophon. A small prose treatise on the Pāramitās.
Begins: pha rol tu phyin pa drug la//sbyin ba'i pha rol tu phyin pa dang/....
'Dāna-pāramitā, Śīla-pāramitā,... There are three Dāna-pāramitās: dharma-dāna, āmisa-dāna, śaraṇa-dāna. There are three dharma-dānas....'
