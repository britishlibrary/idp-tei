chu gtor kyi gleng gzhi dang phan yon/
(Jala-bali-nidāna-anuśaṃsā)
Ch. 73. III. 11, frag, Pothī; 17.5 c. x 6.2 c.: foll. 2, numbered ka 8 and 9; ll. 4, fol. 9 verso blank: dbu-can.
Nemiṃdhara questions Bhagavat on the causes of the illness of his father. Bhagavat explains the previous birth of his father, and sends him to Mañjuśrī.
Ends abruptly. See 343 and 377.
