A note on the twelve Pravacanāṅgas. A series of questions: Why Sūtra?, Geya, Gāthā.... The answers are given in a smaller hand. In the section which deals with the Vaipulyas reference is made to the Mahāyāna-sūtra and also to parts of the scriptures of the Little Vehicle, namely to the Dīrghāgama (Ḥdi-rga-a-ga-ma).
