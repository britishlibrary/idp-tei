Section: 1
kun tu chad pa' sa/
[Avakrama? Vichinna-pāśa?]
Ch. 73. VII, frag. A. 11, Pothī; 44.5 c. x 9.7 c.: fol. 1; ll. 5: dbu-can.
No title; colophon, title only, without rdzogs-so; paḥ-sa more likely than paḥs, although there is no dot before s.
Contains the end of a metrical text on the Ṣaḍ-aṅga-vidyā (oṃ mani padme hūm), mother of the Buddhas, Pratyeka-buddhas, Śrāvakas, devas, and brāhmaṇas.
Begins:
rten skyong gi 'khord rnams kyis/
/rjes su bstan pa ma thobs ste/
/gang phyird 'di la sangs rgyas dang/
/rang sangs rgyas dag 'byung 'gyurd zhing/
/'di las nyan thos rnams kyang skye/
/lha rnams dag kyang skye bar 'gyur/
/rig byed yan lag drug mthar phyind/
/bram ze rnams kyang skye bar 'gyur/
Ends:
'di la sngags gyi dam pa' ni/
/pha rol gnod pa ston pa'r byed/
/rigs sngags lam ni 'da' ba'i 'khor/
kun tu chad pa'_s/
