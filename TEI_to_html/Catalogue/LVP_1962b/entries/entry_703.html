Ch. 51. I. 53, Concertina; 27.5 c. x 7.5 c.: foll. 2, slightly damaged; ll. 4 and 5: dbu-can.
Recto. Fragment of a treatise. Absence of Manaskāra is the right way of the 'meditation of the nature of a Buddha' (Buddha-dharmānusmṛti). If one proceeds by the method of attention, that will be an obstacle to Nirvāṇa. Quotations to this effect from the Prajñā-pāramitā, the Ghana-vyūha (Sdug-po-bkod-pa), the Laṅkāvatāra.
