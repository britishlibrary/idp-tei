'Some people say: When the first thought [of Bodhi] has been produced, one must not meditate on the avikalpaka. But it is said in the Prajñā-pāramitā-sūtra: "When the first thought has been produced, one must keep in mind the sarvākārajñāta", and in the Samyag-jñānācintya-guṇa-sūtra(?) "As beings have been educated, so they see; as they have been educated, so they hear."'
The same quotation from the Acintya-jñāna-sūtra, 707, 1. Compare the Tathāgata-guṇa-jñānācintya-viṣayāvatāra-nirdeśa, Bkaḥ-ḥgyur, Mdo, Csoma_1836: XV. 167-228; Beckh_1914: XVII. 143-96. Compare also 704.
