Ch. 73. III. 39, Pothī; 26 c. x 6 c.: fol. 1, numbered verso, by a second hand, 2; ll. 4: dbu-can.
Fragment of a treatise. Mādhyamika aphorisms: there is neither Samādhi, nor Prajñā, nor Jñāna.... Quotation from the Buddha-kośa (verso, l. 1). The Tathāgata tells Śāriputra that all Dharmas are insubstantial.
Buddha-kośa = Saṅs-rgyas-kyi-mdzod = Saṅs-rgyas-kyi-mdzod-kyi-chos-kyi-yi-ge, Bkaḥ-ḥgyur, Mdo, Csoma_1836: VIII. 235-333, Buddha-dharma-kośa-kāra; Beckh_1914: X. 191-266, translated from the Chinese.
