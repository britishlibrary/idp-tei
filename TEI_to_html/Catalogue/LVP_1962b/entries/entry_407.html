rin po che sna brgyad kyi mchod pa/
(Ratnāṣṭaka-pūjā)
Ch. 73. III. 6, Pothī; 33 c. x 9 c.: foll. 2; ll. 8: dbu-med.
Title in the colophon.
Begins:
oM sngar 'das rigs kyi mgon po bder gshegs dang/
/ma byon rgyal ba rgya mtsho sras bskyed yab/
/rin cen sna brgyad mngon du gyur [...]'dis/
sku gsung thugs la mnyes par thim 'gyur cig/
The last line as a refrain in 9 stanzas. Then, after a blank, fol. 1b, l. 8:
'phyogs bcu dus sum rgyal ba mchod pa dang/
/rnal 'byor dbang phyug thugs dam skong pa dang/
'gro ba mtha' dag thugs rjes bzung ba'i phyir/
bdag nyid klu dbang rin cen brgyad las spyod/
/byang chub sems dpa' brgyad kyi thugs dam dang/
/rnaM shes brgyad kyi yul dang/...
Ends with a stanza beginning:
/rin cen sna brgyad mchod pa sbyar ba 'dis/
nam mkhA las ni char bzhin 'bab 'gyur nas/...
