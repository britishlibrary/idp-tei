Section: 1
Ch. 73. XV. 15, Pothī; 29 c. x 6 c.: foll. 4, numbered (letter) 1-4; ll. 7, 8, and 9: dbu-med.
A small treatise, probably Tibetan (neither title nor colophon). On the Durlabha (things difficult to obtain). Stanzas with a commentary.
The first stanza can be read:
sangs rgyas shes bya ba'i sgra 'di yang /
/'jig rten dag na rab du dkon /
/myi rnyed rnyed par shin du dkon /
/rab du byung ba shin [du]rkon /
Compare Śikṣā-samuccaya, p. 2.
