Buruman (Yeshil-kol) panorama from south slope (Section II). View south-east, flat shore of lake mouth.

'To the eyes of the non-geologist the formation of the Buruman ridge closing the western end of the lake...seemed to bear a close resemblance to the newly formed barrage which has created the Sarez lake...'
