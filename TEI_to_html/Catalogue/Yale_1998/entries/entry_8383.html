Mask for corpse 面罩 (复面)
Date: 653 (epitaph 墓誌)
Photographs of original: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 14: 4
Described in: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 15
Comments: Suggested reconstruction. All three corpses wear masks. For male corpse, covers from eyebrows to chin including cheeks, openings for eyes. 死者臉部都有复面。 想象復原圖。男尸， 蓋眉至嘴， 眼部開洞。 Bands on four corners for tying 四角有帶子以束系。
