Section: 1
Mural painting of scenes from daily life 莊園生活情景
Date: Northern Liang 北涼 (401-439)
Described in: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館 1978: 7
Material description: mural painting 壁畫
Comments: 6 sections, left to right 六個畫面， 從左到右。 1. Servant with hand in pottery bowl behind three footed stove 仆人手伸向陶盆內前面是三腳爐灶。 2. Bow with arrow in quiver 一副弓箭， 箭在箭襄。 3. Seated male tomb occupant and one female both wearing long wide sleeved robes 一男一女各穿闊袖長袍， 應是主人。 男席地而坐。 Bow and arrow in quiver hang from wall, table with brush and pottery bowl牆上掛有弓箭， 箭在襄內。 一張小桌， 上有一支筆杆一個陶盂。 4. Person wearing short sleeves, long pants tucked in boots, high hat, probably minority, leading horse followed by camel 一人牽馬， 人穿短袖長褲， 褲腿塞在靴統內， 頭帶高頂帽，為一少數民族形象。 馬后有一匹駱駝。 5. Upper half has 3 fruit trees, lower half has one ox pulling a cart 上半部有三棵果樹， 下半部一牛拉著車。 6. Five black lines forming field 五條黑線表示田地。 Sun and half moon frame scenes 1, 2, 3 太陽半圓形的月亮在第1， 2， 3 畫面的上部。
