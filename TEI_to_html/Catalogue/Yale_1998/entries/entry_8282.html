"Substitute person" wooden peg 代人 木牌
Date: Qushi Gaochang period， 502--640， 麴 氏 高 昌 時 期
Described in: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館 1978: 2
Material description: wood 木
Physical description: l. 長 9.5, w. 寬 2.8, thickness 厚 0.5 cm.
Comments: Red ink inscription "substitute person" 朱書代人二字。 Diamond shaped 菱形. Similar objects have been found with characters: "substitute person"; symbolic of wooden figurines. 以往發現有 代人 字樣， 象征性的木俑。 See 見 75TKM97:3,4,5; 75TKM91: 6,7,8,9; 75TKM90:1,2,5,6,8,10,12,13,14,15.
