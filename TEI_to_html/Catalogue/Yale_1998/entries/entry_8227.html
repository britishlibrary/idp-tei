Clay lump of dough 泥團
Date: Qushi period at Gaochang, 502-640 麴氏高昌
Described in: Xinjiang shoujie kaogu zhuanye renyuan xunlian ban 新疆首屆考古專業人員訓練班 1989: 8
Material description: Clay 泥
Physical description: Largest 最大 l. 長 10, w. 寬 5, thickness 厚 0.5 cm.
Comments: Total 16 共十六塊. Larger one has appearance of pastry/noodles 最大有 象面食品, smaller one shaped round and flat, molded with facial features 最小者偏圓形， 刻人的五官。
