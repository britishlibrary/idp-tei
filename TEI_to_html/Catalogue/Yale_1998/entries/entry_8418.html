Clay figuines 泥俑
Date: late 4th-late 7th c. 4世紀末到7世紀末
Photographs of original: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 14: 2, 17: 7
Described in: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 21
Material description: Clay 泥
Comments: One painted: white with red, blue clothing, facial features and hair painted in ink. Hole in back of neck, used to attach stick with small yellow and red flag. 彩繪一件﹕ 白底上繪紅， 藍彩衣， 五官和髮用墨筆勾畫。 後頸有一小洞， 是插旗桿用的。 發現紅， 黃色絹質小旗三面。 Three flags found.
