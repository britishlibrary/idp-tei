"Jin" brocade with bird, beast, and tree motifs 鳥獸樹木紋錦
Date: Qushi Gaochang period (502--640) 麴 氏 高 昌 時 期
Photographs of original: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 4: 23
Described in: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 16
Material description: "jin" brocade 錦
Physical description: 21x9cm
Comments: Yellow base with green and ochre designs. 黃地綠赭色花紋。 Five registers of design 花紋五組: 1.tree 樹木; 2. beast with human face and two wings 人面雙翼獸; addorsed flying birds above 上有兩鳥對飛; 3. addorsed long-neck standing bird and tree 長頸鳥向樹對立; 4. addorsed deers and trees 對鹿雙樹; 5. addorsed birds 對鳥。
