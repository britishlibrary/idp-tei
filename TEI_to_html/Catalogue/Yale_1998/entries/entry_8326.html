Astronomical map 天文圖
Date: Tang, mid 7-mid 8th c. 盛唐至中唐
Photographs of original: Xinjiang bowuguan 新疆博物館 1973a: 18
Described in: Xinjiang bowuguan 新疆博物館 1973a: 19
Material description: mural painting 壁畫
Comments: Located on ceiling and upper four walls of main chamber. Includes: twenty-eight constellations, sun with gold bird in northeast, moon with cassia tree and jade rabbit holding pastal, and crescent moon in southwest, and milkway in center. 主室頂部及四壁上部繪28宿， 東北壁有太陽， 內有金鳥。 西南壁有月亮， 內有桂樹和持杵玉兔， 旁有殘月。 之中有銀河。
