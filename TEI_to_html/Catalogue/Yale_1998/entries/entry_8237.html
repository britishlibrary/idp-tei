Section: 1
Head of Buddha 佛像頭
Date: Qushi period at Gaochang, 502-640 麴氏高昌
Described in: Xinjiang shoujie kaogu zhuanye renyuan xunlian ban 新疆首屆考古專業人員訓練班 1989:11
Material description: Clay 泥質
Physical description: Remaining h. 殘高 25, w. 寬 28 cm.
Comments: Excavated in Jiaohe temple. 交河寺院發現。 Open eyes, bare chested, hands in meditation mudra, seated, robe painted red and green, facial features and hair painted black, painted mandorla with 2 Buddhas flanking umbrella shaped cloud .長目， 袒胸， 雙手定印握腹前， 盤腿而坐，衣著紅色綠色， 黑髮髻， 黑線勾勒眉目，彩繪頭部圓光， 畫面有兩佛， 之間有蓮花似的傘狀云。

Originally compiled by: Elizabeth Owen 歐麗文 and Katherine Lee李佩璞
