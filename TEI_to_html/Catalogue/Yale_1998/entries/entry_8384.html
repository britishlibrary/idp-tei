Mask for corpse 面罩 (复面)
Date: Qushi Gaochang period (502--640) 麴 氏 高 昌 時 期
Photographs of original: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 3: 11
Described in: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 15
Material description: "Juan" silk, cotton 絹， 綿
Physical description: Fragment 殘 l. 長 28, w. 寬 24 cm.
Comments: Suggested reconstruction. For female corpse I, covers from eyebrows to chin including cheeks, openings for eyes. 想象復原圖。 女尸， 蓋眉至嘴， 眼部開洞。 Bands on four corners for tying 四角有帶子以束系。 Purple "juan" silk with silver border decorated with 3 beads, 2 made of bone placed around eyes, 1 made of amber placed by tip of nose. 紫絹銀邊， 上綴穿珠三顆， 兩顆骨質眼部， 一顆瑪瑙質鼻尖。
