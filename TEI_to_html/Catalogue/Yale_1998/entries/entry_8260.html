Mural painting of scenes from daily life of tomb occupant 墓主生前的莊園生活情景
Date: Northern Liang 北涼 (401-439)
Described in: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館 1978: 7
Material description: mural painting 壁畫
Comments: 5 segments 五個畫面. Left to right : 從左到右. 1. grain thrasher 舂臼 and alms bowl 缽, 2. field outlined in black in upper half 黑線描的田地在上半部， curved tree branch in lower half 彎曲的樹枝在下半部, 3. seated male tomb occupant wearing long, wide-sleeved robe and holding fan 席地而坐的墓主， 穿闊袖長袍手持團扇, Behind stands one female attendant with two bun hairstyle and holding long stick 墓主身後站立一女侍 頭梳雙髻， 手持一長棍。 4. Upper left, short table with pottery basin and ladle, 2 vessels on top 左上部 為矮几， 上置陶盆， 柄勺，另置二個器皿. Lower half 下半部 has one servant behind a stove with pottery basin with ladle 有一仆人前有三腳爐灶上有一陶盆， 柄勺。 Upper right corner 右上角有一個推磨。
