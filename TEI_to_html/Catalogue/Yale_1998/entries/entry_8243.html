Section: 1
Wooden plank 木板
Date: Qushi period at Gaochang, 502-640 麴氏高昌
Described in: Xinjiang shoujie kaogu zhuanye renyuan xunlian ban 新疆首屆考古專業人員訓練班 1989:11
Material description: Wood 木
Physical description: L. 長 8, w. 寬 3。4cm.
Comments: Excavated in Jiaohe temple. 交河寺院發現 It has 13 holes, diameter 0.8cm each. 上有13個圓坑， 直徑0.8cm. On the reverse side are two more, incised with wave design. Probably used as a brush handle. 背面有兩個坑， 有刀刻水波紋， 疑為毛刷板。
