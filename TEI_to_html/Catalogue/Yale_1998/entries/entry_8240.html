"Kaiyuan" copper coins開元銅錢
Date: Qushi period at Gaochang, 502-640 麴氏高昌
Described in: Xinjiang shoujie kaogu zhuanye renyuan xunlian ban 新疆首屆考古專業人員訓練班 1989:11
Material description: Copper 銅
Physical description: Diam. 直徑 5-6 cm.
Comments: Excavated in Jiaohe temple. 交河寺院發現 Five total, 3 complete, 2 fragmentary. One has"yuan feng" characters inscribed.一共5枚， 3枚完整 ,2枚殘。 一枚有 元丰 字樣。
