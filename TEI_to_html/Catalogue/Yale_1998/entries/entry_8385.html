Clothing on female corpse I 女尸衣服
Date: 653 (epitaph 墓誌)
Described in: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 14
Material description: "Juan" silk, hemp, "jin" brocade 絹， 麻布，錦
Comments: First layer is hemp cloth, second layer of plain silk wrapping includes head. She wears long overcoat to knees and skirt, both made of purple "juan" silk. Addorsed horse design exposed. 內裹麻布， 外以素絹連頭包裹， 上身穿齊膝外套， 下系裙， 均用茄紫色絹縫制， 胸上露出的部分縫 <對馬>>織錦.
