Temple plan 寺院平面圖
Date: Qushi period at Gaochang, 502-640 麴氏高昌
Photographs of original: Xinjiang shoujie kaogu zhuanye renyuan xunlian ban 新疆首屆考古專業人員訓練班 1989:11
Described in: Xinjiang shoujie kaogu zhuanye renyuan xunlian ban 新疆首屆考古專業人員訓練班 1989:10-11
Physical description: Main hall 正殿 l. 長 7.75, w. 寬 7.2 m.
Comments: Located on west cliff of Jiaohe, pounded earth walls, total 25 rooms, main hall in south center with platforms to the north and south and bench in center. Ash pit under north platform probably for incense burning. Row of rooms surrounding main hall. In rubble 200 plus objects excavated: Buddha head, copper coins, pottery, wooden tool, cotton textile. 位于交河古城西面崖邊高坡上， 由二十五間房組成， 正殿在寺院中央偏南, 南北各置台階， 中央有須彌坐，北面台下有一灰坑可能為燒香火之用。寺院底禰捅壑 . 寺院周圍黃土� 牆。 堆積 中發現文物200餘件﹕ 佛像頭， 銅錢， 陶器， 木器， 棉織品。
