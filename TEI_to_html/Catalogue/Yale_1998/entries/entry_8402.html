"Jin" brocade with addorsed horse motif 對馬紋錦
Date: 653 (epitaph 墓誌)
Photographs of original: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 2: 1
Described in: Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 16-17
Material description: "Jin" brocade 錦
Physical description: Roundel diam. 圓圈長短徑 8.5-9 cm.
Comments: Face cover and chest decoration of Female Corpse 1 為I女尸腹面及胸飾。 One has design of addorsed winged horses galloping with character "5" ("wu") above. 一有昂頸相對的帶翼雙馬， 前蹄騰起， 馬頭以上有 <<五>>字。 The other has addorsed winged horses with raised heads and horses head lowered eating grass. 另一件有昂頭對馬， 還有似在吃草的底頭對馬。 See clothing for female corpse I in 59TAM302 見 59TAM302 女尸 I 衣服 (Xinjiang Bowuguan 新疆博物館1960: 14).
ID number: 59TAM302
