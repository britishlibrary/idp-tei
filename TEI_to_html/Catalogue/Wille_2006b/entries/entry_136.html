Section: R
[Samādhirājasūtra?]
The text of ru-v2 corresponds to the opening verses 19-29 of the SRS (ed. Matsunami); the verses 15-50 are extant only in one Nepalese manuscript. Regarding the gap between recto w and x verse 23 must have been omitted in the text of the fragment in hand which has also a different verse numbering, verse 10 corresponds to verse 24. The text from verso 3 onwards could not be found in the text of the Samādhirājasūtra known so far.
