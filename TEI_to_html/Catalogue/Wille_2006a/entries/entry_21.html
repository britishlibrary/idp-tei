Section: R
[Śūraṃgamasamādhisūtra]
Written in the early southern Turkestan Brāhmī, partly rubbed off. In the Tibetan translation the text of the fragment corresponds to Peking Kanjur, section Mdo, vol. thu (70), foll. 314a3-315a2 = TT 800, vol. 32, p. 86.2.3-4.2; Derge[Taipei] Kanjur, section Mdo sde, vol. da (11), foll. 288a3-289a2 = No. 132, vol. 11, p. 534.575.3-577.2. In the Chinese translation of Kumārajīva the corresponding passage is found in T 642, 15.638bc (translation Śgs §§ 95-99). The fragments Crosby 262/263, Stein Kha. i.96 an i.303 contain the same passage.
