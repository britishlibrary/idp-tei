Section: R
[Saddharmapuṇḍarīkasūtra]
Written in the southern Turkestan Brāhmī (main type), the foliation number 33 is on the recto. There are altogether ten fragments of one folio. The two fragments Stein Kha. ix.36 [ed. Saddhp(C), pp. 281f., I fol.78 = microfilm IOL 17A], here given again in italics, belong to the same folio. The text of the verses corresponds closely to the socalled Kashgar manuscript. Parallels: Ka 32a5-33a6; KN 24.13-25.12; Wa 16.14-17.13.
