A treatise; no title, no colophon.
Begins: @//bcom ldan 'das dpal rdo rje sems dpa' la phyag 'tshal lo//_//lung las //lus dang ngag sems rdo rje la//rdo rje sems dpa' rab tu bsgom//lus dang ngag sems rdo rje yis//rdo rje sems dpa' lta bur 'gyur//ji ltar bsgom zhe na dpa' bo dmyig dang ldan pa yis//rdo rje sku mchog thob 'dod na//....
'Homage to Bhagavat Vajrasattva. It is said in the Āgama that a Vajrassattva meditates on the Vajra of Body-Voice-Thought. How does he meditate?...'
Ends: mdor na rdo rje sems dpa'i dngos grub nyid kyang thob bo zhes gsungs so//rdzogs s+ho//
'He becomes like Vajrasattva...he realizes the nature of Vajrasattva.'
