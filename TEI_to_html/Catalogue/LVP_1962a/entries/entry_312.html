Section: 1
Ch. 0011 (fol. 1), XXVII. i and n, Pothī; 43 c. x 9.7 c.: foll. 4, 3 numbered verso 1, 2, 3-4, and one unnumbered (Ch. XXVII. n); ll. 8 and 6: dbu-can.
