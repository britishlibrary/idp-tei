['phags pa shes rab kyi pha rol tu phyin pa sdud pa tshigs su bcad pa/]
(Ārya-prajñā-pāramitā-sañcaya-gāthā)
Ch. 2. C, Pothī; 47.5 c. x 8 c.: foll. 4, numbered ka 3, 10, kha 100, ga 13 (= 3, 10, 200, 213); ll. 6: dbu-can.
Foll. 3 and 10. Begins:
snying rje chen po skyed kyang sems can 'du shes myed /
/'di ni shes rab pha rol phyin mchog spyod pa yin /
'To produce the great companion and to be free from the notion of beings, that is the excellent practice of the Prajñā-pāramitā.'
= 110, fol. 5b, l. 4.
