Ch. 9. I, frag. 40, Pothī; 44 c. x 8.5 c.: fol. 1; ll. 5: dbu-can.
Bhagavat tells Maitreya (Ajita, Ma-pham-po) how Bhagavat Candrasūyapradīpa (Gñi-zla-sgron-ma) teaches the Dharma-paryāya 'Mahānirdeśa': miracles follow, earthquakes, rays from the body of the Buddha.
