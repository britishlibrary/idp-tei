*/sa bon las ni myu gu me tog 'bras bu 'byung/

= 110, fol. 31b, l. 3:
/sa bon las ni myu gu me tog 'bras bu 'byung/
/de yang 'gags la shing yang myed pa de myin ltar/
Reference to the first thought of Bodhi (Bodhi-citta), seed of Buddhahood.
The translation in this MS. Differs from 110, which is better.
