A commentary on the Bhadracarī-praṇidhāna-rāja.
Not the same as 146.
Fol. 1a, l. 7, quotations of the Gaṇḍa-vyūha and the Maitreya-vimokṣa, and Śikṣā-samuccaya, p. 9).
Fol. 10b, l. 6: 'It has been said by Bhagavat that all Dharmas are like a mirage....'
Fol. 10b, l. 2, on four Acintyas: the great strength of Dhyāna, of Karman, of the nāgas, of the Buddhas. Deals with the topics that are 'beyond human reason' and which must be admitted though faith:
