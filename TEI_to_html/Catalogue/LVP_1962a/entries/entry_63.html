II. Three stanzas on the advantages of the two Gāthās: contempt of the Buddha and of the Law, laziness, to be satisfied with mean things, love, egoistic conduct, discontent, uncertainty—vices that are an obstacle to beings—the excellent Vehicle has been taught as an antidote to them. All faults are abandoned...
