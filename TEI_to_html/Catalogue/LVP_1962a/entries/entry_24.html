Ch. 03. 35, Pothī; 31.5 c. x 8 c.: foll. 2, numbered verso ka 2 and 4; ll. 55: dbu-can.
Prātimokṣa topics.
A treatise. A kind of 'Nidāna-pudgala-samgraha' (see Bstan-ḥgyur, Mdo-ḥgrel, XC. 5, Cordier_1909_1915: p. 412); looks like extracts from Commentaries on the Prātimokṣa-sūtra (compare 17). The preface makes reference to the Vinaya-vibhaṅga and to the Vinayavastu, and states that the present work gives nidāna or each point (śīla-vastu) and every specification thereof. Incomplete.
