Section: 1
Ch. 73. VII, frag A. 7, Pothī; 46.5 c. x 11.7 c.: foll. 2: dbu-can and dbu-med.
Vinaya extracts.
The first folio contains (recto) 3 lines in large dbu-can script.
Ends: dal bus song ste zan chad par bya'o snyam nas de dal bus 'gro'o//
'Thinking "going slowly, one must eat", he goes slowly.'
Texts follow, in small dbu-med, first folio recto 15 lines, verso 28 lines; second folio recto 32. These texts are abstracts from several books. The first: Vinaya-vastu, 12th Khaṇḍa, extract from the Poṣadha-vastu (gso sbyong gyi dngos po las); the second: extract from Pravrajyā-vastu.... Second folio verso, Chinese.
