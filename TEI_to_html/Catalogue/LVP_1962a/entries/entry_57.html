Ch. 73. XIII. 3, Scroll; 65.5 c. x 29.7 c.: ll. 47: dbu-can.
Two copies of a fragment of a Mahāyāna-sūtra, the first complete, the second (another hand), incomplete.
An address to Kāśyapa, probably Kāśyapa-parivarta. But Kāśyapa is one of the chief characters of the Ratnarāśi-sūtra (see Śikṣā-samuccaya, pp. 136, 138).
