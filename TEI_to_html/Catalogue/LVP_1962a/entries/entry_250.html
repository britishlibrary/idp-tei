(Pāpa-deśanā)
Ch. 73. VI. 5 and 73. IV. 5a (foll. 62 and 65), Pothī; 38.5 c. x 8.5 c. (foll. 62-65 slightly different size): foll. 8, numbered 41-44, 59, 62-63, 65; ll. 4 and 5: dbu-can.
Foll. 41-44 contain a part of the Vandanā (the Bodhisattvas Chos-kyi-sde-snod, Mñam-bar-gzigs, Myi-mñam-gzigs, Tiṅ-ṅe-ḥdzin-dbaṅ-phyug, Chos-kyi-dbaṅ-phyug-rgyal-po...; ending with the Bodhisattvas Mtshan-ñid-du-chud, Ṅes-paḥi-mtshan-ñid).
Fol. 59 contains a part of the Śaraṇa-gamana, beginning with the universal Saṃgha, and the Tathāgatas of the East (Akṣobhya, Ḥkhrul-myi-mṅah-ba), &c. Foll. 62-65 contain part of the Pāpa-deśanā.
