Homage to all Buddhas, Bodhisattvas and Ārya[ ].—With a desire to explain this Prātimokṣa-sūtra, one has just to state by whom and where the three Baskets of the Śrāvakas were collected, to which part of the Scripture belongs the Prātimokṣa....
There follows the history of the Councils (ma-mo = chos mṅon-paḥi-sde-snod); the genealogy of the twenty-two schools, amongst them the Mūlasarvāstivāda...; the localization of the Prātimokṣa-sūtra, why it is Vinaya, why it is Sūtra.
There is a gap after fol. 4 (which contains the beginning of the commentary on the prefatory stanzas). There are 17 foll. dealing with the Pārājikas, 5 foll. dealing with the Naiḥsargikas, 4 with the Śaikṣadharmas.
Incomplete and defective.
