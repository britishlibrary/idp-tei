[rin po che'i tog gi gzungs/]
(Ratna-ketu-dhāraṇī)
Ch. 9. I, frag. 73, Pothī; 45.5 c. x 9.3 c.: fol. 1; ll. 5: dbu-can.
A fragment.
...rin po che'i tog gi gzungs 'di thos ma thag tu...
bud med kyi dbang po myi snang zhing/skyes pa'i dbang po skye bar 'gyurd...
'Just after hearing this Ratna-ketu-dhāraṇī, the female sex ceased to appear and the male sex was produced.'
