[shes rab kyi pha rol tu phyin pa/]
(Prajñā-pāramitā)
Ch. 9. I. frag. 45, Pothī; 38 c. x 8.5 c.: fol. 1; ll. 13: dbu-med.
Bhagavat teaches Śāriputra; the Prajñā and the Prajñā-caryā (śes-rab-kyi-spyod-pa) of a Bodhisattva.
Begins: sha ri bu de la shes rab zhes bya ba ni /'di lta ste/dge bi chos thams cad rab du rtogs pa gang yin ba de ni mngon sum mthong bi shes rab yin te/chos thams cad rjes su rtogs pi phyir ro/
Observe pi=pa'i, bi=ba'i
Ends: ...myi skye bi chos kyi bzod pa yong su rdzogs pi don gang yin ba de ni pha rol tu phyin pi don ces bya 'o/phyir myi ldog pi sa mthar yongs su rdzogs pa gang yin pa de ni pha rol du phyin pi don ces bya 'o/sangs rgyas
