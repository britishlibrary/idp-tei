'phags pa las legs nyes kyi rgyu dang 'bras bu bstan pa zhes bya ba'i mdo/
Ch. 73. VII. 2, Pothī; 58 c. x 8 c.: foll. 7, numbered kha 59-65; ll. 5: dbu-can and dbu-med (fol. 65).
'The Sūtra of the causes and results of good and bad action'.
Defective at the end, although the text, fol. 65b, l. 5, does not continue to the end, leaving a blank space of more then half a line.
Bkaḥ-ḥgyur, Mdo, Beckh_1914: XXXII. 237-51; Csoma_1836: XXX. 303-20. The title omits las and adds mahāyāna-sūtra. Translated, according to the colophon, by Chos-grub, from Indian and Chinese originals (rgya-gar-daṅ-rgya-ḥi-dpe-las).
Ends: 'People who in this life are attached to many women after death fall into the hell of the iron-mill.'
See 335, 2.
