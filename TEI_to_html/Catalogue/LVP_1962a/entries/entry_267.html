Fragment 11, Concertina; 24.4 c. x 8.5 c.: fol. 1; ll. 6: dbu-can.
Bhagavat answers some questions of the Bodhisattva Gaganagañja and explains that, in the days of yore, he remained in hell for twelve mahā-kalpas, having committed many sinful actions and 'destroyed' the three Jewels.
