Vajracchedikasūtra
A large sheet of paper, probably of the Tang period, 18 1/2 in. wide and 9 5/8 in. high. On recto, a fragment of theVajracchedika Prajñā-pāramitā sūtra, in Chinese, in writing of about 700 A.D. On verso, Uighur phrases in a large hand, apparently exercises.
