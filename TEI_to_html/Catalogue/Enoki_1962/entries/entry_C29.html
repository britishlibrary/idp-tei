[Miao-fa lien-hua ching, fasc. 4.]
(Sad-dharma-puṇḍarīka-sūtra)
Ch. 73. XIV. 16b (572); 30 columns (some incomplete).
A part of the Hua-ch'êng-yü p'in. On the 30th column a passage of gāthā is translated as ching wu wan yu chü, of wich wu-wan is a variant hitherto unknown for san-po or wu-po of the existing printed edition.
