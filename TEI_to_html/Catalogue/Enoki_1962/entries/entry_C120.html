Section: R.1
Ch. 73. III. 3, Scroll, 52 c. x 29 c.: 23 columns; semi-cursive hand, not easy to decipher: verso, T.t.d. II, p. 71.
A draft or copy of a prayer read at a Buddhist ceremony held by officials in order to pray for the recovery of a Shang-shu, their head, from illness. Only the middle portion remains, the beginning and ending part being lost. At the end of the last column eight characters ... ('On the birthday of the general he implanted the virtue') are inscribed by a different hand.
