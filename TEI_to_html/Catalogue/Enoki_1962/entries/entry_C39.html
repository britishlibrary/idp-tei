Miao-fa lien-hua ching, fasc. 7.
(Sad-dharma-puṇḍarīka-sūtra)
Ch. 00267, Kh. scroll; 203 c. x 25 c.: 180 columns (including 1 column of colophon).
Contains the Miao-chuang-yen-wang pên-shih p'in (chap. 27) and the whole of the P'u-hsien-p'u-sa ch'üan-fa p'in (chap. 28) with the following colophon: 'In the second month of the second year of T'iao-lu (A.D. 680), Chang Tsê, disciple (of Buddha), copied (this sūtra) for (the happiness in the other world of his) dead wife (who came from the family) So'.
