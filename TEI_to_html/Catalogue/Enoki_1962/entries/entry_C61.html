Section: R.1
Ta-ch'êng ju-lêng-ch'ieh ching, fasc. 3.
(Laṅkāvatāra-sūtra)
Ch. 00269, Kh. scroll; 930 c. x 25 c.: 520 columns.
Contains the whole of the Chi I-ch'ieh-fa p'in (chap. 2, div. 3). At the end of the MS. a miniature portrait of a priest with the inscription '(Portrait of a) (P'u?)-sa at Sha-chou of the Empire Great T'ang' and 'Chin-kuang-ming tsui-shêng-wang ching' (Suvarṇa-prabhāsottama-rāja-sūtra.) These are written by a hand quite different from that of the sūtra. But, this may tell that the sūtra was copied during or before the T'ang era.
