Section: V.1
[Lun-yü chi-chieh]
Ch. 73. VIII, frag. 6, two fragments of scroll; 27 c. and 24 c. x 27 c. (original width): 17+10 columns (six complete, lower halves of the rest missing): Tibetan verso writing exercises.
A fragment of the first chapter of the Lun-yü chi-chieh by Ho yen. Many MS. remains of the same book are preserved in the British Museum (Giles, Descriptive Catalogue, nos. 7088-7105) and the Bibliothèque Nationale.
