Section: V.1
Tao(shê)-wên
A prayer for the welfare of members belonging to the same shê or to a club and village.


Section: V.2
Lin-k'uang-wên
A lamentation read at a funeral ceremony in front of a tomb. These two seem to be used as a model of writing. As for prayers or addresses of similar nature see Naba (Toshisada) in Shirin, XXIV, pp. 550-1, 543-4, 744 ff.
For a description of the MS., and the text recto, see C130.
