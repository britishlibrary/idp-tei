Section: R.1
[T'ang-lü]
'Penal code of T'ang'
Ch. 0045, Kh. scroll; 40 c. x 10 c.: 19 columns (including 6 of annotations, upper halves missing).
A fragment of a copy of the T'ang-lü contains a part of the Pu-wang-lü corresponding to the T'ang-lü in the T'ang-lü su-i, fasc. 28, pp. 18a-21a (ed. Suŭ-pu tsung-k'an san-pien). Some variants of texts. The Bibliothèque Nationale in Paris possesses two fragments of the T'ang-lü. One (P. 3252) is of 18 columns, of which 13 are complete, containing some articles of the Hu-hun-lü and the Chiu-k'u-lü. The other (P. 3608) is of 153 columns of the Chih-chih-lü (50 articles) and the Hu-hun-lü (33 articles). Cf. Wang (Chung-min), Pa-li Tun-huang ts'an-chüan hsü-lu, Pt. 1, 1936, fasc. 2, p. 7a-7b; Pt. 2, 1941, fasc. 2, p. 11a-11b, and Tun-huang ku-chieh hsü-lu (Peking 1958, pp. 140 ff.).
