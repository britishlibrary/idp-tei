IOL Khot S 5

Section: V.3
Ch. 0047, Kh. scroll; 128 c. x 31 c.: 16 characters written at random with the same pen as the Khotanese text.
A photograph is published by H. W. Bailey and E. H. Johnston (B.S.O.S. VIII, p. 77).
The character Liu is repeated ten times and the following characters are inscribed, ...; perhaps playing with words: .... tomorrow, ... the sky is bright (dawn); ... field, ... farmers.
For the other Chinese texts written on this MS. see C63 and 111.
