Section: R.1
Fo-shuo yao-shih ching
Ch. LVIII. 007, Kh. scroll; 252 c. x 26 c.: 134 columns.
The content is identical with the twelfth section of the sūtra named Kuan-ting ching or Fo-shuo ta-kuan-ting shên-chou ching as is recorded by Nanjio or with the sūtra named Fo-shuo kuan-ting pa-ch'u kuo-tsui shêng-ssŭ tê-tu ching as in the Taisho, no. 1331. The end of this fragment is reproduced in Serindia, IV, pl. CXLV. As for sūtras named Yao-shih ching cf. Bussho Kaisetsu Daijiten, ed. by Ono (Genmyo), XI, pp. 65-66.
