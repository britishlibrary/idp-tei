[Ta-chih-tu lun, fasc. 91.]
(Mahā-prajñā-pāramitā-śāstra)
Ch. 81. X (737 part); 143 columns (1-31 and 36-147 of the whole MS.).
Contains a part of the Shih-chao-ming p'in (chap. 81).
In preparing the paper for Tibetan text verso four columns of another sūtra (C81) were inserted upside down between the 31st and 35th column of this text: hence the lacunae. For two additional scrolls used for this purpose see C49 and 80.
