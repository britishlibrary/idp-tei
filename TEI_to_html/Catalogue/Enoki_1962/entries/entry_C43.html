Ta-fang-kuang-fo hua-yen ching, fasc. 64.
(Buddhāvataṃsaka-sūtra)
Unnumbered Kh. scroll (B); 765 c. x 25 c.: 443 columns.
Contains the Ju-fa-chieh p'in (chap. 39, div. 5). In the British Museum there are several dated copies of the same sūtra: S. 2067 (A.D. 513), S. 2724 (A.D. 522), and S. 2527, 6650, 4520, 1529, 5762 (A.D. 579). Cf. Giles, B.S.O.S. VII, pp. 816-817, 819, 834, and Yabuki (Keiki), Meisa Yoin Kaisetsu pt. 1, p. 268.
