Section: 1
Ch. 9. I, frag. 55 (631); interlinear gloss to Tibetan text and 4 columns.
Nineteen Chinese terms of the P'u-sa-ti or Bodhisattva-bhūmi given to corresponding Tibetan names. The Chinese terms are taken from the Yü-chia-shih-ti-lun or Yogacaryā-bhūmi translated by Hsüan-chuang. Chinese names for four Yogacaryā-bhūmi are written in vermillion, verso, fol. 3.
