Ta-ch'êng tao-kan ching sui-t'ing shou-ching-chi
(Commentary on the Śālistambha-sūtra)
Ch. 77. X. 6 recto (672): 15 columns (some incomplete).
A commentary edited by Chos-grub (Fa-ch'êng) on the Tao-kan-ching (Nanjio, no. 280; Taisho, XVI, no. 712) and on the Ta-ch'êng tao-kan-ching, both of which are Chinese translations of the Śālistambha-sūtra. Several fragmentary copies of the same commentary are preserved in the British Museum (Giles, Descriptive Catalogue, nos. 4653-5), The Bibliothèque Nationale, and the National Library in Peking (see Yabuki, Meisa Yoin Kaisetsu, Pt. 1, pp. 62-64).
For the text verso of this MS. see C102.
