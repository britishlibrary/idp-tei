Or.8210/S.515

Section: V.2
The second of two similar documents on this ms. Transcribed in DSJW: DSJW4.44 it is a copy of a communique authorizing the appointment of Vinaya Master Shenxiu (not to be confused with the Chan patriarch of the same name) as Acting Dharma Master at Kaiyuan Monastery. G. dates this ms. to the tenth century.
