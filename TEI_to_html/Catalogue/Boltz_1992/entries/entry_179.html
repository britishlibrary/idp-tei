Or.8210/S.395

Section: R.1
"A dialogue between Kongzi (Confucius) and the boy Xiang Tuo. The colophon is dated 943. LMS: LMS notes that this dialogue is otherwise not preserved in Tang-Song sources but may be found in the late Heian "Konjaku monogatari". There is no evidence that the story was recorded prior to the Tang. LMS: LMS suggests it inspired Pi Rixiu 's (c.834-c.883) 'Discourse on the non-existent Xiang Tuo'. The transcription in DBJ: 1.231-43 is drawn from this ms., P.3883, P.3833, P.3255, P.3754, P.3882, Or.8210/S.1392, Or.8210/S.2941, Or.8210/S.5529, Or.8210/S.5530, and Or.8210/S.5674. Giles_1940: 341-2 introduces one episode; Soymie_1954 provides a critical edition with translation, as well as studies of versions in Tibetan, Mongolian, Thai and Japanese; and Waley_1960: 89-96 supplies a summary translation."
