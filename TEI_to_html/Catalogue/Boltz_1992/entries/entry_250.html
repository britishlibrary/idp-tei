{CHI} Tai shang dong xuan ling bao wu liang du ren shang pin miao jing
The title appearing at the end of this ms. shows it to be a fragment of scripture central to the Lingbao revelations codified by Ge Chaofu c. 390. The text corresponds to HY1: HY 1 Ling bao wu liang du ren shang pin miao jing 1.1b5-18a0. Ofuchi_1978: 55-6 collates this ms. with its match in P.2606.
