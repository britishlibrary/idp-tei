Or.8210/S.476(A)

Section: V.1
"[Note: this entry also refers to Or.8210/S.476: Or.8210/S.476(B)] The opening line, pertaining to the count of sutra wrappers, is dated 29th of third month. The lists that follow mention the monasteries named on the recto along with the three nunneries of Dacheng, Puguang and Lingxiu. Additional ledgers of sutra bestowal are listed on Or.8210/S.375: V.1. G. dates this ms. to the tenth century."
