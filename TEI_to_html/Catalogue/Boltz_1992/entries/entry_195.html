Section: R.2
Essential summary of the rituals for settling the mind and embarking on the Way according to the four immeasurables of Mahayana. These instructions prescribe meditation beginning at the fifth watch (3-5am). One is instructed on how to evoke visions by concentratin on various seed syllables. Among them is a sermon by Amitabha and the suffering of those condemned to purgatory.
