"Partially transcribed in LMS: LMS and SSKB: SSKB4.118-9. Pachow_1965: 29-30 provides a full transcription with the observation that, in addition to Xuanzang, other monks serving as translators during the Tang were known by the title 'Sanzang' ("Tripitaka) and so the provenance of these poems remains to be determined. Two of the five heptasyllabic quatrains are incomplete and, like the preceding verses ascribed to Li Cunxu, they all bear marginal corrections. The entire sequence appears to have been produced as a student exercise. Title of the poems are: 1. 'Da Tang Sanzang ti xi tian ++"
