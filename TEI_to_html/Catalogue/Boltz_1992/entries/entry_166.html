Section: R.1
"Notice issued by the Department of Examination of Jungtu Monastery : G., SSKB: 2.22 and DSJW: 4.130, with transriptions, all suggest that the year "mouzi" may correspond to 928. The notice was issued by the Chief Sangha Controller and calls for the dispatch of clergy from all monasteries to recite suutra, saastra, or vinaya. Failure to comply is reported to invite punishments ranging from fifteen strokes of the staff to a fine of fermented beverages."
