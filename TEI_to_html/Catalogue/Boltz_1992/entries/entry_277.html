Section: R.1
Published with emendations by FanFengyuan_1976. Meng Shen compiled a text by this title c.701-4. The 89 entries in his text were supplemented shortly after by Zhang Ding. Each of the 26 entries in this fragment is set off in red. Giles_1940: 333 lists and translates the entries. TanZhen_1987 collates them with citations from the 'Shi liao ben cao' in a Song Materia Medica re-edited in 1249. Additional studies are cited in WangZhongmin_1979: 154-7.
