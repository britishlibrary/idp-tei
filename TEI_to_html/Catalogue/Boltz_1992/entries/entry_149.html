Section: R.1
"Historical romance dealing with Wu Zixu, minister of Wu, about 500 BC. Johnson_1980 examines the sources of this bianwenand provides a translation. The text transcribed in DBJ: 1.1-31 is drawn from this ms., P.3213, P.2794, and Or.8210/S.6631. Mair_1983: 123-65 provides a full translation of the Wu Zixu story based on all four mss. It is also partially translated in Waley_1960: 25-52. Additional studies are cited in SSKB: SSKB4:21-22."
