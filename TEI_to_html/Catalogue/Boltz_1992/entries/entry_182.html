"Account of a pilgrimage to Wutai shanJiaoDemieville_1971: 193 transcribe and date the text to the Five Dynasties. ChenTsulung_1984: 74-78 collates this and other transcriptions. As LMS: LMS notes, this ms. is comprised of two, apparently inverted, segments.The second traces a journey departing from the Northern capital (ie. Taiyuan) on the 21st day of the 5th month and arriving at the south face of Mt. Wutai on the 25th day. The author describes Foguang Monastery where he spent the 27th. The opening segment of this ms. centres on Da'an Monastery with the presiding lecturers named as follows: Dengyi in Maitreya Hall, Jilun in Manjusri Hall, Daoshu in Menlou Hall, and Chongde in Bhaisajya-raja Hall. The Sutra Repositry Hall is said to have held a Tripitaka totalling 5,600 pieces. P.3973 and P.4648 have similar accounts."
