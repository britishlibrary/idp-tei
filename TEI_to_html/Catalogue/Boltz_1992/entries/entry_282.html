Or.8210/S.766

Section: V.2
"contract of a certain resident of Pingkang township for hiring a labourer, dated the 25th of the seventh month of a renwu year. This contract concerns the hiring of Luo Changying, son of Luo Bunu of Chixin township. Monthly payments of wheat and millet, as well as one suit of spring clothing, undergarments and leather shoes are promised in exchange of one year's labour. TTD: TTD suggests the text is a writing exercise and equates the year renwu to 982. See also S.1897/R.1."
