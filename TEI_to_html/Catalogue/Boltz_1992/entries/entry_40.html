Or.8210/S.343

Section: V.5
"Account of the Sage narrated by the majestic sovereign of the great Tang. This text, composed by the Heir Apparent Li Zhi (the future Tang Gaozong), is recorded in QuanTangwen: Quan Tang wen 15.5a-6b, as well as in other Dunhuang mss. and various works of the Tripitaka. It notes the imperial command of 645 authorizing Xuanzang 's supervision of a translation of the Tripitaka. The preface his father Tang Taizong contributed to the Tripitakacommonly precedes it (see Or.8210/S.343: 343 V/7)."
