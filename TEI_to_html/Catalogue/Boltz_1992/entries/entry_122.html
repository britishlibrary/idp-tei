Section: R.1
"Contract of monk Guangjing for the purchase of an axle for exchange of lining cloth, dated 13th of second month of Dazhong 5 (851). This contract, transcribed in DSJW: 43 and TTD: 81(140)-82(139), documents Guangjing 's negotiations with a fellow member of the sangha, Shenjie. Three other monks, Longshan, Zhijiao and Zhiheng, signed as witnesses to his promise to supply one-hundred chi of cloth by the tenth month or risk penalties. If he did not paid by the tweflth month then twenty chi of cloth shall be added to the price payable. If either party reneged on the contract then they would forfeit one bale of cloth. There are three small red seal impressions."
