Section: R.1
Scripture of the Transcendent Lord Zuo of the Grand Ultimate in Request of Instruction
"This text is not included in the MingDaoistCanon: Ming Daoist Canon of 1445 but the title is registered in the index to the Lingbao scriptures compiled by Lu Xiujing (406-477), which is preserved in P.2861 and P.2256. It was issued in the name of Zuo Ci, putative mentor of Ge Xuan (164-244), to whom the Lingbao corpus is ascribed."
