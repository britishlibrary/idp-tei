Section: V.6
Zibao
(Treasury of Words)
"This phoentic glossary, also known as the "Zibao" (Treasury of Words), is ascribed to a Mr Zheng. P.2058 contains a preface transcribed by ZhouZumo_1988: 52-3, which reveals the compiler's debt to the lexicographic data in the "Zitong", "Yupian" and "Qieyun". Additional fragments of the text include Or.8210/S.6189, Or.8210/S.6204, P.2717, and P.3906."
