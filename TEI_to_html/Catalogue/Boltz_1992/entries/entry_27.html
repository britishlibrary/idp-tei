"This contract, transcribed in DZ: 138, DSJW: 95 and TTD: 3:102(119), documents Cao 's negotiations with a monk of an unidentified monastery. The guarantor for repayment by the seventh month is named as Duan Xingzi. The columns in which the names of two witnesses were to be inscribed remain blank. SSKB: 141 dates this document to the first half of the ninth century."
