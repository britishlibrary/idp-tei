Or.8210/S.559(A)

Section: R.1
"The labels are coded according to the ""Qian zi wen""[Thousand Character Classic] and include seal impressions of the prince of Guazhou and Shazhou (also found on Or.8210/S.3967). The titles are of juan 11, 12, 14-25, 27, 29, 32, 36, and 37. From ch. 11-20, the title is followed by the library mark (?) 'ju ye'; from ch. 21-29, by 'chan suo'; from ch. 32-37, by 'ji si' (or 'si ji')."
