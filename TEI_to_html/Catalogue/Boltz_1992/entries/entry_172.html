Section: R.1
"The last line reveals that this text was copied out on the 27th of the fourth month of the year "gengchen". As LMS: LMS observes, the somewhat incongruent title by which this text is known does not appear in the ms., but is found on a full copy of the text in Or.8210.S.1625. It tells the story of how Zhixing 's ringing of a bell evoked a divine response. The narrative is ascribed to the "Yi yuan" in TaipingGuangji: 112. Comparable accounts on the merit derived from ringing a bell are collected under a heading on the subject in FayuanZhulin: 99 (T2122: T2122, vol.53.1016c-12-1017b24)."
