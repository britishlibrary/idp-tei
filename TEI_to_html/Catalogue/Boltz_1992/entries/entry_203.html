Or.8210/S.525

Section: R.1
"The text and its history are discussed in Giles_1921. It includes 9 complete stories and the start of a 10th. One of the four episodes is translated in Waley_1960: 174-88 and included in Giles_1944: 25-6. Giles dates the ms. to ca. 900. Transcribed in LuoZhenyu_1924. The transcription in DBJ: 865-900 is based on a copy in the Nakamura collection, in collation with this ms., S.6022 and P.2656. WangGuoliang_1986 notes that P.2656 merely cites the 'Sou shen ji'. He tabultes the five mss. he determines to be fragments of the text (Nakamura.902, Or.8210/S.525, Or.8210/S.3877, Or.8210/S.6022, and P.5545). ZhangXihou_1982 provides a more detailed analysis, comparing the mss. to received versions of the text. Additional studies are cited in SSKB: 42-3."
