"TTD: TTD2 pp.41-4, 50(225)-59(216) provides a summary and transcription for this ms. which was compiled in prepartion for a Census Register. The date 749 recoded in the transciption in DSJW: DSJW1 pp.187-207 may be corrected to read 769, inaccordance with Ikeda_1979: pp.215-33. The ms. bears the impressions of two seals denoting the authority of Dunhuang County and Shazhou prefecture respectively. The contrast between the red seal markings and text is shown in the colour photograph of this mss in YZ: YZ1. Numerous studies of the text examine its significance as a record of the equal land allocation system."
