Or.8210/S.374

Section: R.1
"This communication, transcribed in LMS and DSJW: 3.106, expresses gratitude for the wheat supplied by the wife of an anonymous official to relieve famine in the border regions in the first and second months of the year. It also serves to document the allocation of the wheat. Other signatories include Yuchi Fode, Ma Xingzi and Zhu Asun."
