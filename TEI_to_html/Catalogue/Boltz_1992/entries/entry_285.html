"Teraoka_1960, Teraoka_1961: 1961 and Teraoka_1966: 1966 reproduces this and 13 other Dunhuang fragments of 'Zhuangzi' or 'Nan hua zhen jing', with commentary by Guo Xiang (d.312). collates them with printed editions as well as with citations in the 'Jing dian shi wen' (583), and traces their history. He concludes that most of the fragments including this ms, were rendered by the same hand sometime after 742 when the title 'Nan hua zhen jing' was authorized by imperial decree."
