Or.8210/S.692

Section: R.1
"An Yousheng, a scholar at Jinguangming Monastery, produced this copy of Wei Zhuang 's (d. 910) heptasyllabic narrative on Huang Chao 's seizure of Chang'an in 881, completing his transcription in 919 (see colophon). The translation in Giles_1925 is based on a rescension derived from collating this ms. with Or.8210/S.5476: Or.8210/S.5476, Or.8210/S.5477, P.2700, and P.3381. Giles notes that the copying is full of mistakes."
