Or.8210/S.462

Section: R.1
"Account regarding the just recompense of the Suvarnaprabhasa [uttamraja] sutra. This narrative tells how Zhang Judao and others of Wenzhou benefitted from copying the sutra. The incomplete ms. here corresponds to the final two-thirds of the account recoded at the close of Tanwuchan 's (385-433/436) translation (T663: T663/16.358b28-359b1), which has a preface by Zongyi dated Yuanfeng 4 (1081). The first part of the narrative is recorded in Or.8210/S.4487. Full and partial copies of the text are found in P.2099 and P.2203 respectively."
