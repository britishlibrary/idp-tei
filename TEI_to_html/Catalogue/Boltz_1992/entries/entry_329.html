Section: R.1
Scripture on the Spirit Spells of the Cavernous Abyss
"no. 6, 'Taking oath against distaff species'. The text outlines steps to be taken for overcoming afflictions thought to have been provoked by female spectres known as 'xiang', and other malevolent forces. Additional fragments of the text are listed under Or.8210/S.318: R.1. Ofuchi_1978: 269-73 collates this text with HY335: juan 6 of HY 335 'Taishang dong yuan shen zhou jing'. "
