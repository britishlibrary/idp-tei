Section: R.1
"This ms. appears to have been produced by the same hand and to be a continuation of P.3216. Saato_1963 collates the two fragments with a complete ms. copy of the same text, "Fa zhao he shang nian fo zan" held by Ryukoku University Library. He concludes that this anthology of encomia was complied in the first half of the ninth century by sramana Hongsu of Zhangjing Monastery in Chang'an, former residence of Fazhao (fl. 766-74). This ms. includes the latter half of seven 'Encomia of Amitabha ': 5. Encomium on the ultimate bliss of Sukhaavatii (starting with the eighth "géthé"); 6. Proceeding to Sukhaavatii ; and 7. Once the dharma boat has left; along with complete texts of 'Encomium on proceeding in mass towards ultimate bliss' and 'Encomium on Mount Wutai ', all of which are transcribed in Pachow_1965: 93-5, 99-100, 118-19. See also P.2483 and P.3645 for copies of the later encomium."
