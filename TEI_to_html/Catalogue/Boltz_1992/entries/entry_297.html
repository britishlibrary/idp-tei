Section: R.1
"A Daoist scripture. This text is not included in the MingDaoistCanon: Ming Daoist Canon of 1445, but the title appearing at the end of this fragment with the addition of Dongxuan lingbaois cited in the catalogue of missing scriptures, HY1419: HY 1419 "Dao zang que jing mu lu". Van der Loon also notes that a 1144 catalogue of the imperial library cites a "Tian zun jin jie miao jing" as missing. G. dates this ms. to the seventh century. Ofuchi observes that it was inscribed with a wooden stylus. "
