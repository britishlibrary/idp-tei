Section: V.3
"contract of Cao so-and-so of Pingkang township for borrowing silk with interest, dated 22nd of fifth month of a jiashen year. Cao Yanyan is named as the borrower and Zhang Wanzi of Longle township as the lender. Cao 's son Jicheng is identified as guarantor in the text of the contract but it was his elder brother, Yanchang, who actually signed in that capacity. Other signatories include the borrower and a witness named He'a Funu. DSJW: DSJW and TTD: TTD suggest the jiashen year corresponds to 924 or 984."
