Section: V.1
"Roster of graded forced labour for Dunhuang County, Shazhou [for the Dali period, 766-779 ]. Varying interpretations of this document take into account a more detailed register of the Tianbao period (742-755) in P.2803, which lists seven identical names. The brevity and lack of official seal impressions suggest a draft. The colophon attached to the end of the ms., inscribed at Lingtu Monastery on 30th of the fourth month of a xu year, is thought to be associated with the texts recorded on the verso and to date to the first half of the ninth century."
