"Wuzhen took up the post of Chief Sangha Controller of Hexi in 870. Both the preface, with he wrote in a state of semi-paralysis at the age of 70-odd, and the two heptasyllabic quatrains which follow are transcribed in LMS: LMS and SSKB: SSKB4:122. DGZ: DGZ3:1338-47 provides a transcription of the preface and the full sequence of ten verses by collating this ms. with P.2847 and P.3821. "
