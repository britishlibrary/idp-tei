Section: R.1
"The anonymous preface states that this sequence of poems was composed to represent the events chronicled in the thirteen histories (i.e., from "Shiji" to "Suishu") as the passage of one's life from birth to the age of a hundred. Only thirty-five complete verses and one couplet of altogether one hundred and ten heptasyllabic "lushi" are preserved in this fragment, going from years one to 28. A full transcription of all extant verse is given by Pachow_1965: 6-13 and, in collation with a Ming work, in LiuXiuye_1988."
