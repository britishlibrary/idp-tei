Or.8210/S.613

Section: V.1
"This early register has been the subject of numerous studies (see refs.). Two types of records of uncertain relation are distinguished. One lists household members, taxes owed, and land alloted. The other classifies and totals this data. The ms. consists of 17 pages pasted together and contains numerous seal impressions (see Or.8210/S.113: R.1)."
