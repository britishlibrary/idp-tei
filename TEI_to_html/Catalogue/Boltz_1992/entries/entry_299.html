"Poems by Gao Shi. This fragment includes the latter half of 'Gu da Liang hang' (Song of the ancient great Liang), and a nearly complete copy of the well-known 'Yan ge hang' (Song of Yan) by Gao Shi (716-765). The latter is accompanied by a preface in Gao 's collected works, dating its composition to Kaiyuan 26 (738). Additional fragments of his poems include P.2552, P.2567, and P.3862. "
