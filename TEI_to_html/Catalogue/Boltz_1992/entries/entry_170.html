Or.8210/S.375

Section: R-V.1
"Enumeration of the fascicles of illustrated sutras bestowed on the ninth of the third month of the jisi year. Giles suggests that the year "jisi" may correspond to 969. The section on the verso is transcribed in SSKB: SSKB2.39. The closing entry identifies the donor as a monk named Zhang of [San]jie Monastery. see Or.8210/S.476: V, Or.8210/S.1364, Or.8210/S.2712, Or.8210/S.3071: V, Or.8210/S.4914, and Or.8210/S.5676."
