Or.8210/S.474

Section: V.1
"Voucher accounting for the exchange of millet [wheat and beans] in payment to the overseers of the procession of the image, dated 13th day of 3rd month, wuyin year. Transcribed in DZ: DZ1.362-3 and DSJW: DSJW3.344. G. dates this ms. to tenth century. SSKB: SSKB2.121, 152 equates the wuyin year to 918, the date established by Chikusa_1961b: 121. The terms of payment ot two Elders named Zhao and Meng (?) were authorized by Chief Sangha Controller Fayan and the Vinaya Masters Shaojin and Gongren. Ogawa_1965 examines the significance of this text as a document on financing procession of a Buddha -image. See also Or.8210/S.4812."
