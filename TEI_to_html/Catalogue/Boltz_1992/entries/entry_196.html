Or.8210/S.526

Section: R.1
"Letter to a certain monk by Madame Yin of Wuwei prefecture. Transcribed in LMS: LMS and DSJW: DSJW5:38, where it is ascribed to the time Guiyi Commandery fell under the rule of the Cao family, that is, ca. 910-1035. The monk is urged to return to Wuwei. Written when Suzhou and Anzhou were in turmoil. Tributes to Madame Yin 's son Li Cunhui (d. 980) are preserved in Or.8210/S.289: Or.8210/S.289/V.1."
