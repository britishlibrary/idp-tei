Or.8210/S.389

Section: R.1
"Transcribed in DSJW: 4:487-89. This ms. provides a brief chronicle of relations with and between Uighurs and Turks. It concludes with a tally of the 257 residents of Ganzhou who were forced to relocate in Suzhou following Turkish occupation of Uighur territory. SSKB: 1.74 notes related fragments in Or.8210/S.2589, Or.8210/S.6333 and P.2772. RongXinjiang_1990 suggests that this document was composed shortly after the communique of 884 recorded in Or.8210/S.2589."
