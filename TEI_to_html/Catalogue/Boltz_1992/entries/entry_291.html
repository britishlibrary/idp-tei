Or.8210/S.779

Section: V.1
"The transcription of this text in T2862: T2862 is designated as an account (ji) rather than a prayer (wen). This title is in any case derived from a fragmentary inscriptionat the end of the ms. which lacks either designation. The text itself, reportedly drawn from the Suvarnaprabhasa sutra (see Or.8210/S.462: R.), recounts how Vaisravana came to offer protection to the rulers, as well as all inhabitants, of Jambudvipa. Vaisravana 's role as a national guardian is popularly traced to the Tang emperor Xuanzong 's victory in 742 over foreign tribes through an application of the rituals of Vaisravana recommended by Amoghavajra. Hong Bian held the post of Chief Sangha Controller of Hexi from Dazhong 5 to 7 (851-853)."
