"The title inscribed at the close of this fragment conflicts with the conventional word order identifying the scripture as part of the DXLB corpus. The corresponding text in the Ming Daoist Canon of 1445, HY369, Tai shang dong xuan ling bao san yuan wu lian sheng shi miao jing, lacks this segment. Ofuchi collates this ms. with the matching unit in P.2865."
