Or.8210/S.289

Section: R.1
"'Bao ci mu shi en de': Giles_1943: 163-4 lists and translates the ten virtues. DGZ: 748-66 provides a critical edition of the ten verses, based on a collation of this ms., Or.8210/S.4438, Or.8210/S.5564, Or.8210/S.5591, Or.8210/S.5601, Or.8210/S.5687, Or.8210/S.6274, P.2843, and P.2411. ZhengAcai_1984: 637-81 collates and discusses versions of the text in this ms., Or.8210/S.5573, Or.8210/S.5638 and Or.8210/S.6270, in addition to those Stein mss. cited above. Studies of the fragment in Beijingzhou: Bejing zhou 87 are cited in SSKB: 105-6."
