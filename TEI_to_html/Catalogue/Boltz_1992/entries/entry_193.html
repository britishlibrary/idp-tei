Section: R.1
"bulletin issued by Baoen and other local monasteries G. dates this ms. to the tenth century. Transcribed in SSKB: SSKB2.21-2 and DSJW: DSJW4.128-9. The document includes two impressions of the seal of the Chief Sangha Controller of Hexi. The notice calls on the persons named to take on assigned roles which include serving as receptionist in the font pavilion, overseeing supplies, looking after the bathing quarters and toilets in the north and south halls, and reciting sutra. The monasteries indicated by one-character abbreviations are: [Bao]en ; Lian[tai] ; [Ling]tu ; Qian[yuan] ; [Da]yun ; Long[xing] ; Kai[yuan] ; Yong[an] ; Jin[guangming] ; [Jing]tu' and [San]jie."
