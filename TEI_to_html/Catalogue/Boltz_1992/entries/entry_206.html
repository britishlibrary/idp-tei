Section: R.1
" Guiwen composed these on a journey to India by imperial decree to collect sutra. He speaks of arriving in Lingzhou on 23rd of the fourth month, whereupon he purchased two camels in exchange for items in his wardrobe. One letter indicates that he travelled in the company of Dequan, a fellow monk from Kaiyuan Monastery. One communique is reversed. Transcibed in LMS: LMS and DSJW: DSJW5:9-14."
