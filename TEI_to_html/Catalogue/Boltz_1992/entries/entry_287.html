Or.8210/S.778

Section: R.1
"An entry for this work is found in the bibliography of 'Song shi '. Demieville_1982 transcribes and translates this fragment along with Or.8210/S.1399, Or.8210/S.5474, and Or.8210/S.5796, all of which include prefaces. This is one work associated with the brahmacarin Wang. Guo Zaiyi collates the transciptions in this and other modern editions. Demieville_1982: Demieville (p. 77) discerns a connection between this text and four inscriptions on the verso naming Deng Qingchang, scholar of Dayun Monastery, one of which bears the date of fifth of eleventh month of a 'renxu' year (902 or 962?). Name and affiliation are recorded at the close of the transcription of this ms. in T2863: T2863."
