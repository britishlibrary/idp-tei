Section: R.1
"This is a fragment (juan 10) of the encyclopaedic anthology compiled on the order of Zhou Wudi (r. 561-578). Juan 10 is among those missing from HY1130: HY 1130 "Wu shang bi yao" in the MingDaoistCanon: Ming Daoist Canon of 1445. Ofuchi_1978: 337-8 concludes that this is by the same hand as the table of contents to the encyclopaedia in P.2861 and that the text corresponds to the segment of j.10 listed in this table of contents under the heading 'Di yu' (Purgatories). The colophon at the end of the table of contents, dated the 8th of the 2nd month of Kaiyuan 6 (718) is matched in this ms."
