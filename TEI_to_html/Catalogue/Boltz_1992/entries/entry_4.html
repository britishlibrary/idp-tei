Protocols for the Registers of Salvation of Trancendents and Numina
"The title is recorded midway in the ms. (plate 3). The text itself, as Ofuchi_1978: 361 observes, appears to correspond to the written protocols of Zhengyi (i.e. Celestial Master) parishes with which the renowned Shangqing codifier Tao Hongjing (486-536) was familiar."
