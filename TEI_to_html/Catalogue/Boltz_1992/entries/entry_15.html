"Entries date from Xianheng 5 (674) to Kaiyuan 1 (713) and concern various types of households, filial conduct, foreigners, and servants. Giles_1940: 30 translates an entry regarding the prohibition against the sale of men or women under the pretense of being hired as labourers. Transcribed in DSJW: 570-78 and, with summary, in TTD: 43-44, 36(93)-37(92)."
