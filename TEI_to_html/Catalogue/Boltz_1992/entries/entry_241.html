Or.8210/S.617

Section: R.1
"Lexicon for Everyday Situations. This fragment contains entries under the following headings: agriculture; sericulture and weaving; needlework; textiles; precious treasures; incense; colours; weights and measures; the marketplace; fruit; vegetables; meat; drink; social gatherings; domestic animals; wild animals; birds; insects; fish and turtles; trees; bamboo; plants; boats; wheeled transport; fire; water; and diseases (beginning only). Phonetic and/or semantic glosses are provided for each entry. ZhouZimo_1988: 49-51 compares this ms. to another version of the text in P.2609. Additional fragments include Or.8210/S.3227, P.3776, and P.5001. G. dates this ms. to the 7th c. "
