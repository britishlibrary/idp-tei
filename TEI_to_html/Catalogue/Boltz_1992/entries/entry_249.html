Annotated Interpretation of the Calendrical Statutes
"The preface lists the eight scholars, headed by Li Linfu (d. 752), ordered to compile the Annotated Interpretations. The attributions correspond to those in the entry on "Yu kan ding Liji yue ling" (Imperial Publication of the Calendrcial Statutues of the Book of Rites) in the bibliography of the "Xin Tangshu" (New Tang History). WangZhongmin_1979: 49-50 collates the variants between the preface and its counterpart engraved on stone in Kaicheng 2 (836)."
