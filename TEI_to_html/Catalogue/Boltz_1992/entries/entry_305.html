Scripture of Instructions from the Celestial Worthy on Deliverance from Suffering
"One juan. The title appearing at the close of this ms. reveals it to be a fragment of a late Lingbao codification. Ofuchi_1978: 113-4 collates the text with corresponding segments in P.4559 and HY375: HY 375 "Taishang dongxuan lingbao tian xun shuo ji ku jing". The passage on "huahu" (converting barbarians) is missing from the latter. Additional fragments of the text include Or.8210/S.5921 and P.2385."
