Chunqiu hou guo yu
"This chronicle of Kong Yan (268-320) is alternatively listed in bibliographies as the "Chunqiu hou guo yu" (Discourses on States in Succession to the Spring and Autumn Period). This ms. section covers the years 212-207 B.C.Wang Zhongmin notes the evidence for the three-juan division of the text, as denoted by the title appearing at the end of this ms. Zhang Liangshu provides a transcription in collation with citations of the text in the "Shiji", "Zhanguoce" and various anthologies. Additional fragments include P.2589, P.2702, P.2872, P.3616, P.5010, P.5034, and P.5523. "
