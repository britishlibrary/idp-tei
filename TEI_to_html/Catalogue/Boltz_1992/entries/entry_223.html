Section: R.1
"The two verses on cloth and silver by Li Qiao (644-713) in this fragment are included in the "Li Qiao za yong" preserved in the "Itsuzon sosho". Zhang Tingfang contributed a preface to the anthology in 747 in which he takes credit for the commentary. An additional fragment of the text is found in P.3738. Kanda_1962 compares a selection of verses in the two mss. with versions in the "Itsuzon_sosho" and" QuanTangshi"."
