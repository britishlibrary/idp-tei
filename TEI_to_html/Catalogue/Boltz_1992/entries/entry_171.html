Section: R.1
"Letter from a Minister of State to Vinaya Master Deng, dated 24th of first month of unspecified year. Transcribed in LMS and DSJW: 50. The caption in the latter names the Minister of State as Cao. The letter notes the bestowal of two portions of fine paper by the Minister and his wife Qingji for the copying of suutra and saastra. G. dates the ms. to the tenth century. A Vinaya Master Deng also figures in P.3005. Picture of bird at end in place of official's name."
