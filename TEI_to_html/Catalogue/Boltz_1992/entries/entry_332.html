Section: R.1
"Instructions on determining auspicious and inauspicious days for specific activities are provided in a preface by the compiler Zhai Fengda, Acting Erudite of the Provincial Academy with the rank of Court Gentleman for Promoted Service. His son, Zhai Wenjin, was copyist. Zhai and his son both compiled other almanacs. There is a remarkable continuity between such formulations and contemporary Chinese almanacs. Giles_1943: 154-5 summarizes the contents. Fujieda_1973: 411-24, citing Yabuuchi_1964, discusses this and fragments of other dated almanacs. The format and contents of almanacs in the Stein and Pelliot collections are analyzed and compared in ShiPingting_1987."
