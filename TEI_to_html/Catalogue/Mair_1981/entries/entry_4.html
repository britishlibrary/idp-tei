“General Exhortation of the Twelve Hours to the Four Orders (i.e., monks, nuns, male and female devotees) to Cultivate Themselves in Accordance with the Doctrine” 十二時普勸四衆依教修行 (head title). Much used, ordinary coarse, fibrous, beige paper. Fairly good hand; a few changes and corrections; some highlighting in red. Colophon (same hand): “Copied by the student, Hsüe An-chün, on the seventeenth day of the fifth month of the year 924. Especially retained for recitation by the faithful disciple, Li Chi-shun. Encourage to goodness.” 同光貳年甲申歲[#1](=[#2])[#3](→[#4])之月蓂彫二葉學子薛安俊書／／信心弟子李吉順專持念誦 勸善. Title on v° (in a much cruder hand): “The Twelve Hours of the Great Teacher, Chi-yen,” one scroll 智嚴大師十二時一卷. The copyist of this scroll is the same person as the copyist of Or.8210/S.2614.
