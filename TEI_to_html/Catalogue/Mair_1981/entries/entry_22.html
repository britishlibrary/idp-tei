Sūtra lecture text on the Sūtra of the Kindnesses of Parents 父母恩重經 (lacks title). Section title (at end): “To lead on the Laity,” the sixth 誘俗第六. T672–694. Begins at very edge of sheet, lacks head. Colophon: “Copied on the seventh day of the eight month of 927 by I-chüeh (?)” 天成二年八月七日一[#11](=覺?)書. Has identifying formula for chiang-ching-wen. Highlighting and punctuation in red ink. Unusually thin, whitish paper. Rapid, good hand; few corrections.
