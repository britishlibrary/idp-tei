Or.8210/S.4504

Section: v1
Prayer. Lists of districts and monasteries in the Tun-huang area. Two “Circulars for Mobile Guards” 行人轉帖. A specific time limit of three days is set in the second for recipients of the circular to report to a place “outside the southern gate” 南門外. They must bring with them bows and arrows, spears and shields. The last two persons to arrive will be flogged seven times. Those who miss altogether will be officially punished. Then a list of names. Practice characters. Thousand Character Primer. Prayers. Gāthā. Contracts. Various poor hands.


Section: r1
Vinaya text with commentary. Beige paper; fairly good hand.
