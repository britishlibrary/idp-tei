Or.8210/S.1441

Section: v1
Notes on the life of Buddha and prayers. “Vimalakīrti Seat-Settling Text” 維磨(→摩)押座文 (head title). Used in collation of T829–831. Has identifying ya-tso-wen sūtra title quotation formula. Followed by quaint story about efficacy of belief in Avalokiteśvara 觀音 and other Buddhist homilies. Prayers. Also the famous Assorted Cantos from the Cloudy Folksong Collection, altogether thirty 云(=雲)謡集雜曲子共三十首 (only 18 on the ms). More prayers, writing on v° is in mediocre to moderately good hands; unruled; covers about half of scroll. Near head of v°, twentieth-century notation consisting of commercial numerals in red and “Miscellaneous Records of Governance and Human Affairs” 雜錄政教人事各部.


Section: r1
Encyclopedic comendium of non-Buddhist text on miscellaneous subjects, mostly illustrating different human qualities. Beige paper; lightly ruled. In two good, regular hands; few corrections; red punctuation.
