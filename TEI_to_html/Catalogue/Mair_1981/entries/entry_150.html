Section: 1
“The Story of the Crown Prince of the House of Liu during the Former Han,” 前漢劉家太子傳 (head title). “Marvels of the Crown Prince of the Liu Family,” one fascicle 劉家太子變一卷 (end title). T160¬–163. Semivernacular prose; lacks identifying pien-wen formula. Followed immediately in same hand by “Poetic Recitation on Chi Pu” 季布詩詠 (head title). T844–845. The piece is actually about Chang Liang. Then later “The Story of the Crown Prince of the House of Liu during the Former Han” 前漢劉家太子傳 (different hand, begins abortively to copy the text again). This surely is the correct title (cf. Or.8210/S.5547). The end title above must be considered an aberration. Poor quality beige paper with reddish tint; faintly ruled. Nondescript, mediocre hand; uncorrected. Several eulogies also on this scroll, r° and v°.
