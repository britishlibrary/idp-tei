Section: r1
“Incantation for a New Son-in-Law” 呪願新女婿 (end title; ms torn at beginning and end). Abridged version not used in collation of T273–277. Other short pieces of secular literature, both poetry and prose, mostly about marriage and including “The Five Watches” 五更轉 (head title) and “Rhapsody on the Hot Springs” 溫泉賦一首 by the Advanced Scholar, Liu Hsia 進士劉瑕. Ordinary beige paper folded to form columns. Punctuated near end of ms; very few additions.
