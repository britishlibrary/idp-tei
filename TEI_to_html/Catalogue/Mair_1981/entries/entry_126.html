Transformation text on Chang Huai-shen 張淮深 (lacks title). T121–127. Has identifying pien-wen formula. First portion of scroll is ordinary light beige paper; last thirteen columns are very poor quality, rather light beige paper. Red punctuation; fairly messy ms. Fairly good hand; numerous corrections. At T127.1, changes to a different fairly good hand; not a part of the pien-wen; corrections.
