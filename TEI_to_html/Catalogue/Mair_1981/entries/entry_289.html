Section: r1
Vernacular prose story of “Catch-Tiger” Han 韓擒虎, a general of the founder of the Sui dynasty, Wen-ti (lacks title). T196–206. Coarse, beige paper. Mediocre calligraphy; many changes; very messy ms. Divided episodically by the passage of time with such formulas as 前後不經旬日. Last sentence: “The illustrated text has come to an end and we have left nothing out of our copy.” 書本既終並無抄略. No drawings on this ms.


Section: v1
Gāthās, vows, odes, hymns, airs, etc. Better calligraphy than r°, some very fine.
