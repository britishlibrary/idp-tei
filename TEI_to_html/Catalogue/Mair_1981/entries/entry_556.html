Section: v1
Patch of brownish paper to serve as backing. Has “Writings of Brahmacārin Wang” 梵志書王梵 and other practice characters. Signed: “Paper in the hand of a girl of the Ts’ao family.” 曹家一娘子手[#199](=紙). Inside the patch, which is falling off, may be found approximately twenty lines of a copy of the Family Teachings of the Grand Duke, one fascicle 太公家教一卷 (actual title at beginning) in a poor hand.


Section: r1
Portion of the Vajracchedikāprajñāpāramitā-sūtra. Golden paper, patched and restored. Very good hand. First sheet is in a different, fairly good hand on whitish paper.
