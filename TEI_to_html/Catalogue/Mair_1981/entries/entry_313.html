Poems of Brahmacārin Wang, one scroll 王梵志詩一卷 (head and end titles). Coarse, beige paper. Fairly good hand; partial punctuation in red. Title repeated at end several times for practice in clumsier hands.

“Rhapsody on Getting Rid of Three Injurious Things” 去三害賦. Very faint. Crudely written lay society circulars. Practice writing; doodles. Included is the truncated title “Questions of Hsiang-tzu,” one scroll 順(=項)子問書一卷.
