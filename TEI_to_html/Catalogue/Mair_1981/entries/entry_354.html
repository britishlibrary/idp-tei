Inscription and preface. Poem. Gāthās. Prayer. Vows. Eulogy. “The Marvels of Shun as a Boy,” one scroll 舜子變一卷 (head title). Used in collation of T129–130.10. Lacks identifying pien-wen formula; is entirely in vernacular prose. Then questions and answers on Buddhist doctrine. Story of the Buddha. Prayer. Poems. Vow. Other miscellaneous texts and documents. Beige paper; various poor to mediocre hands. This scroll composed of separate sheets with separate texts on them pasted together. Generally very messy; corrections, additions, erasures. The copying of the story about Shun stops abruptly before coming to the end of the sheet it is on.
