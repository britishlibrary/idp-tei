Section: r1
Important Instructions for Beginning learners, one scroll 開蒙要訓一卷. Brittle, yellow paper; good hand. Colophon (same hand): “Recited by the student Sung Wen-hsien and copied by An Wen-te on the twenty-third day of the third month of 851.” 大中五年辛未三月廿三日學生宋文獻誦 安文徳寫. Heading for lay society circular in different hand. Title repeated in another hand. Many lay society circulars as small patches that were originally separate slips of paper.


Section: v1
The year 908 天復八年 written twice; lay society heading; a few practice characters; name. All crudely written. Crude drawing of an ox at a position corresponding to that part of r° which talks of horses, cows, etc.
