Pelliot chinois 3109

Section: 1
r° and v° “Various Difficult Characters” 諸雜難字壹本 (head title). A very neat (except around margins) copybook. Head colophon: “Inscribed in the year 983.” 太平興國八年記. End colophon: “Inscription of an abbreviated booklet of miscellaneous difficult characters on the twenty-fifth day of the fifth month in the year 990.” 庚寅年五月廿五日略雜難字壹[#22]記. Consists of narrow sheets connected in such a way that the booklet can be folded accordian fashion. Ruled; fairly good hand.
