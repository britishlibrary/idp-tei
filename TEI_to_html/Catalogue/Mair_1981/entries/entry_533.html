Sūtra-lecture text on the Sūtra of the Feast for Souls in Purgatory (lacks title). Not included in T. Begins with an untitled seat-settling text as an introduction:

This morning my only intention is to preach to you,
The filial deeds of Maudgalyāyana…;
I broadly encourage all of you faithful disciples.
That you surely must be filial to your {fathers} and {mothers}
The title of the sūtra is right now at the cantor’s side,
Since he has not even started, I’ll {tell him to begin}
Will the whole assembly respectfully join your palms,
As I invite the cantor to sing the doctrine of souls in purgatory.

今朝只為擬宣傳
目連孝行(?){ }{ }{ }
普勸信心諸弟子
大湏(=須)孝順阿{耶}{孃}
經題合(=恰)在都講邊
未舉都來[#181](=而?) 效{(→教)}{開?}
大[#182](=衆)虔[#183](=恭)合掌着
孟蘭盆法(sic)教唱將來

Translation note: 都來 is T’ang colloquial and means “altogether,” “after all,” “completely,” or “reckoned, considered.”
The lecturer then discusses the various titles of the sūtra and the history of its repeated translation into Chinese. “The one I shall lecture on today” he continues, “Is the first adaptation of the {Sūtra of the Feast} for Souls in Purgatory.” 今日講者即苐一譯[#184](=改) (also occurs above in the text as 譯 [#185](=改))[#186]蘭{盆}{經} . Has identifying formula for chiang-ching-wen. Soft, fibrous, whitish paper; mediocre hand.
