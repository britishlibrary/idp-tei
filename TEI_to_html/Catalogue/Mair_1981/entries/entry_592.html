Section: 1
Two mss recounting the story of the king of a country called “Happiness” 歡喜國, one T772.2–777.16.7 (originally same ms as Pelliot chinois 3375v°, on the basis of which it belongs to the type of story known as nidāna 緣 [“underlying causes”]) and the other 772.5–780.15. Prosimetric vernacular narrative. No consistent, identifying pre-verse formula; several verses preceded by the refrain “Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva. Buddhaputra” 觀世音菩薩 仏子. These and the performance notations (吟, 側, 斷, 吟斷, 斷側) are written in red on the second copy.
