“Words of a Waiting Maid” 下女夫詞一本 (head title). The title on v° is the same but, being written in conjunction with red commercial numerals, dates from this century. Used in collation of T273.1–276.3.21(狀). Whitish paper. Blockish, ill-formed hand; few changes, erasures.
