Collected Poems of the Brahmacārin Wang, with introduction 王梵志詩集並序 (head title). Coarse, drab paper; moderately good hand. Repeated several times on patches on v°: “The lay student, Teng Ch’ing-ch’ang, of Mahāmegha Monastery.” 大雲寺學仕郎鄧慶長. Possibly in same hand, but with finer strokes. “On the fifth day of the eleventh month of the jen-hsü year (902?), Teng Ch’ing-ch’ang….” 壬戌年十一月五日鄧慶長.
