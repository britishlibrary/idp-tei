Titles: “A Record of a Pair of Kindnesses,” third [fascicle] 䨥(=雙)㤙(=恩)記第三 (head). “A Record of a Pair of Kindnesses,” seventh [fascicle] 雙㤙記弟七 (1. 238). The Buddha’s Sūtra of the Recompense for Kindness, seventh [fascicle] 佛報恩経弟七 (1. 460). Sūtra of Recompense for Kindness, eleventh [fascicle] 報㤙経弟十一 (1. 461). The Buddha’s Sūtra of the Recompense for Kindness, eleventh [fascicle] 佛報恩経弟十一 (end). A variant type of sūtra lecture text. At least partially based on the Sūtra of Recompense for Kindness 報恩經 (see 11. 129–130). Not in T. Has identifying chiang-ching-wen formula but it does not always occur regularly at the end of verse passages. Also has greater variety of verse forms than typical chiang-ching-wen. Soft, white paper; irregularly ruled. In three (?) mediocre hands: first is thick, halting, blockish; second (starting at 1. 238 where there is a seam between two sheets of paper) is somewhat neater; third is quick, spidery, attenuated. Full of errors; very few corrections and additions.
