Section: r1
Sūtra lecture text on the Lotus Sūtra (lacks title). T488-500. Fairly good, drab paper. Fairly good hand. Very few corrections and additions. Had identifying chiang-ching-wen formula.


Section: v1
A series of exhortations by a preacher to his congregation, largely heptasyllabic verse, to take to heart the teachings of the sūtras (especially the Inconstancy Sūtra 无常經) and to come often to the “lecture courtyard” 講院 to hear him elucidate them. T656–670. Slightly better than average hand. Lacks chiang-ching-wen identifying formula.
