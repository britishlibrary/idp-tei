Photostat from Library of Congress of ms formerly in possession of Hu Shih; original now somewhere in China but can not be located “Transformation Text on the Subjugation of Demons” one scroll 降魔變文一卷 (actual title at end). T361.6.36(讚善)–389.10. Drab (?) paper of fairly good quality; lightly ruled; slight staining. Moderately good hand; punctuation marks; corrections and additions. Notation at end in same hand but written much larger: “If anyone who reads this sees a part which is incorrect, I pray that he will correct it forthwith.” 或見不是[#226](=處)有人讀者即与政著. Has identifying pre-verse formula pien-wen. The written counterpart of the famous illustrated transformation storytelling scroll in Paris (Pelliot chinois 4524).
