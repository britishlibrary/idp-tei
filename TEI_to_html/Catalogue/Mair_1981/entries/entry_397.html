Or.8210/S.5813

Section: 1
Lay society circular stipulating that contributions are to be doubled of they are not brought on time. Lists names of officers (names of members cut off) with the character 知 beside to indicate receipt of the circular. Whitish paper originally used as a patch on Or.8210/S.2607 (consisting of lyric meters on r° and an inventory of goods on v°). Bold, clear hand.
