Section: v1
Text for a religious lecture; not a chiang-ching-wen. One section is labled “Ceremony of ūrdhva-janma (rebirth in the upper regions)” 上生礼 and begins: “Invite the Buddha and call the assembly to order as usual.” 請佛作梵如常. Ends: “We are finished with this place” 此[#204](=處)了也. Written very hastily but in a good hand. Red highlighting; lines blacked out; interlinear corrections and additions. Small notations indicating various refrains and ceremonial formulas. V° also includes several other texts in the same hand.


Section: r1
Portion of Mahāprajñāpāramitā-sūtra in a good hand.
