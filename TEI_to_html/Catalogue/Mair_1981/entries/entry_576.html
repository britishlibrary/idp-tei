Section: 1
An exhortation to subscribe to the vinaya 勸戒文. Specifically religious text with evidence of oral composition and performance. Messy ms in a mediocre hand; many corrections. Colophon: “Inscription of the receipt and retention of this joint composition by the scribe, Ma Yeh(?)-yüan and the monk, bhikṣu Pao-hui of the Requital for Kindness Monastery, who recited it on the seventh day of the seventh month in the chia-shen year (924?).” 書手馬[#208](=業?)員共同作甲申年七月七日報恩寺僧比丘保會誦持受(→受持)記. Before this another colophon, ending “received and retained” 受持, that has been blacked out.
