“Transformation Text on the Subjugation of Demons,” one scroll 降魔變文一卷 (head title). Fragment of a picture of a man beating a drum (usually incorrectly described as a warrior with a sword) on a separate piece of paper glued to the beginning edge. T361.1–361.6.35 (有佛). Drab paper; lightly ruled; moderately good hand. Part of same ms as that formerly belonging to Hu Shih (photostat in the Library of Congress). The picture may have been pilfered from a storyteller’s illustrated transformation tableau scroll, bearing as it does a certain resemblance to the celebrated Pelliot chinois 4524 (q.v.).
