Pelliot chinois 5019

Section: 1
Fragment of a crude drawing depicting the building of the Great Wall under the First Emperor of the Ch’in dynasty. On v° is a verse description of the scene. Probably a transformation tableau scroll of the Meng Chiang-nü narrative comparable to Pelliot chinois 4524 for the Śāriputra story, though of inferior quality. Coarse, fibrous, dark beige paper; mediocre hand.
