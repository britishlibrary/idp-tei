Pelliot chinois

Section: v1
Inscription of the copying of the “Nidāna on the King of the Country Called ‘Happiness’” 歡喜國王緣一本寫記 (at end). T777.16b–780.15. Angular, good hand; very few corrections. Followed immediately in the same hand by a text with this colophon: “Inscription of the copying of the ‘Nidāna on Prince Sujāti [well-born]’” 湏(=須)闍提太子因緣一[#55](=通)寫記. More corrections. Then another Buddhist narrative in classical. Colophon (in a different hand using red ink, upside-down after the seventh line from the end): “Written on the sixth day of the month of the i-mao year (955?) by Chieh-ching, a monk of the Monastery of the Three Realms” 乙卯年七月六日三界寺僧戒淨寫耳.


Section: r1
Models for Letters, one scroll 書儀一卷. Messy ms; ruled; red punctuation. Mediocre hand.
