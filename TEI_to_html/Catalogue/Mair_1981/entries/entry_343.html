Section: r1
Ten poems by “Cumulous Debater” 雲辯; frequent mention of lecturing on sūtras. Also gāthās by the same individual and his will 與緣人遺書. “Cumulous Debater,” who was one of the best known sūtra lecturers in the whole of tenth-century China, died on July 24, 951. Light beige paper; mediocre hand.


Section: v1
List of donations for a funeral.
