“Sub-Commentary on the Four-Fold Vinaya,” first scroll 四分戒本疏卷第一 (head title). “Related by the śramaṇa Hui” 沙門惠述 (in red ink). Colophon: “From the twenty-third day of the tenth month of the year 915 and beginning at the Monastery of Recompense for Kindness, the Preceptor-Ācārya Li lectured on this sub-commentary. Inscription this fifteenth day of the eleventh month of copying while listening along.” 乙亥年十月廿三日起首於報恩寺李教授闍梨講說此疏隨聽隨{寫}十一月十五{日}記 (in red ink). The carelessly written colophon may not be by the same person who copied the sub-commentary or, if by the same person, may not have been written at the same time he copied the sub-commentary. Thick, beige paper; mediocre hand.
