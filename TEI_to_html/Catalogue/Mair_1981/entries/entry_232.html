Accounts of strange happenings written in simple classical (lacks title). Not used in collation of T865–889 but cf. the Luo Chen-yü ms recorded on T884.1(slightly different at this point)–889.1. Good hand; very few corrections. Note at end (in a rapid, very fancy and flourishing hand which I take to be that of a teacher): “Whose writing (book) is this?” 此是阿誰文書?
