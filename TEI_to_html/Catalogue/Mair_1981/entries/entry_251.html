Section: v1
Account of Prince Siddhārtha’s attainment of the way (torn off at beginning). Used in the collation of T285.7.15(悉)–296.12 (lacking final title). Includes inserted ya-tso-wen at 286.9–287.13. Fairly good hand; occasionally rushed; unruled. Colophon (same hand): “Written and inscribed on the nineteenth day of the eighth month of 934 by Hung-fu, a monk of the Lotus Throne Monastery.” 長興伍年甲午歲八月十九日蓮臺寺僧洪福寫記諸耳. “Retained for reading and recitation by the monk, Hui-ting. His acquaintances are not to take it away.” 僧惠定 (these three characters blacked over) 池(→持)念讀誦知人不取. Lacks identifying pre-verse formula for pien-wen; once has identifying sūtra-quotation formula and several times has performance notation “to be intoned” 吟. Practice characters interspersed.
