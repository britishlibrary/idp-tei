Section: 1
Contracts for sale of silk. Account note of an oil press. Memorandum. Lay society circulars. One, commemorating the death of the mother of “Silly boy” Chang 張憨兒, lists fines to be imposed for noncompliance and the names of members with marks indicating receipt of the circular. The other is dated “second month of the ting-hai year (927?)” 丁亥年二月. Very coarse, drab paper; all carelessly written.
