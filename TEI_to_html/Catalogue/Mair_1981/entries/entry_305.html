“Rhapsody on Han P’eng,” one scroll 韓朋賦一卷 (end title). Head title is [韓]朋賦一首. Used in collation of T137–141. Slightly torn at beginning and abbreviated in middle (before 139.5). Vernacular prose narrative. Below title: “The Chaste Wife Ho (?) …, a domestic Bodhisattva.” 貞妻[#131][#132]賢土(→士). Colophon (same hand): “Copied on the eighth day of the third month in the kuei-ssu year (933?) by P’ei Ch’ing-t’ung.” 癸巳年三月七八(sic)日裴[#133](=慶)[#134](=通)書了. Red ink over it. Coarse, whitish paper; badly discolored. Unpracticed hand; messy ms; red punctuation. Many errors; some erasures, otherwise largely uncorrected.
