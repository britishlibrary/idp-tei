Poems of Brahmacārin Wang, one fascicle 王梵志詩一卷 (head and end titles). “Discussion of Tea and Wine,” one fascicle, with preface 茶酒論一卷并序 (head title; end title same but without 并序). Colophon: “Inscribed ad copied in his own hand on the fourteenth day of the first month in the year 972 by Yen Hai-ch’en, fellow of the Academy of Knowledge and Arts” 開寶參年壬申歲正月十四日知術院弟子[#17](=閻)海眞自手書記.
