Section: v1
Fragment of a seat-settling text (lacks title). T843. Has identifying ya-tso-wen formula. Records of father and son building a cave-shrine and performing other meritorious deeds. Poor to mediocre hands; corrections.


Section: r1
Miscellaneous topical Buddhist prayers and a eulogy on the Western Paradise. Crisp, whitish paper; calligraphy ranges from mediocre to fairly good.
