“Vimalakīrti-sūtra Seat-Settling Text” [維]摩[經押座]文 (actual title). Used in collation of T829–831. “Trikāya Seat-Settling Text” 三身押座文 (actual title). T827–828. After the sūtra title quotation formula (T828.1), a space and then the following verse:

This morning the Master of the Dharma has spoken the truth,
Those who are sitting below in the audience, don’t dilly-dally;
Eachof you Reciting the Buddha’s name hurry back to your house,
If you get back home late, your old lady will scold you.

Section: v1
“Seat-Settling Text” 押座文 (actual title). T842. Poem about the Buddha’s asceticism in the mountains. All the ya-tso-wen are on separate sheets of drab paper that have been glued together after they were written. Only the second Vimalakīrti ya-tso-wen is distinguished by its calligraphy; the others are in various poor to mediocre hands. All end with the identifying formula for ya-tso-wen.
