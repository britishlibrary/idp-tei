Section: 1
“A Tale about Hui-yüan of Lu Mountain” 㠠(→廬)山遠公話 (actual head title). T167–193. Stops copying in mid-sentence and in middle of paper. Colophon: “Inscription of copying by Chang Ch’ang-chi in the year 972.” 開寶伍[#112](=年)張長繼書記. Coarse, whitish paper. Rather assured, fairly good hand; very few corrections and additions. Both paper and calligraphy are inferior after 193.1.16(at 進旨). The entire scroll is composed of various sized sheets and erratic calligraphy.
