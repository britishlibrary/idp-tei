Family Teaching of the Grand Duke, one scroll 太公家教一卷 (end title). Very poor hand; additions. Colophon: “On the fourteenth [day] of the tenth month in the jen-shen year (972?), the lay student, Chang Ying-hsin, copied the second part about women.” 壬申年十月十四學仕郎張盈信紀書[#92](=笑→第)二女[#93]. Additional colophon (poor hand): “Scripture for learning characters. Now there are three hundred words and I understand each of them. An eldest son who is at home.” 斈字経今三再(=百)言語壹勿不[#94](=解)富次(＝當九→就[basis of the translation] or 久 [“long”]?)家中大郎.
