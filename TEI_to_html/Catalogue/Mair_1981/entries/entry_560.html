Section: 1
Semivernacular prosimetric narrative recounting of the eight major events in the Buddha’s life. First sheet of paper (begins T329.2) is rough, beige, and lightly ruled. Writing thereon is mediocre; many corrections; red ornaments and punctuation; generally messy. Second (begins 330.7) and succeeding sheets (to 342.7) are of still poorer, woody quality paper though the writing is slightly better and rapid; fewer corrections. Verse sections preceded by the abbreviated formula 云〻or by the full formula “at that time, what did he say?” 當爾之時道何言語 or some variation thereof. Note that the title, “Transformation on the Eight Manifestations” 八相變, which is at the head of v°, is different from any of the hands used in copying the main text. The only other writing on v° is two practice characters.
