Pelliot chinois 2515

Section: 1
Family Teachings of the Debater, first scroll, with introduction 辯才家教卷上 并序 (head title). Family Teachings of the Debater, one scroll 辯才家教一卷 (end title). Questions are posed thus: “The students asked the debater….” 學士問辯才曰. Colophon: “Inscribed by the exoteric bhikṣu, the monk Yüan-ch’eng whose lay name is Wang Pao-chüan, on the twenty-fifth day of the fourth month in the chia-tzu year (964?).” 甲子年四月廿五日顯比丘僧願成俗姓王保全記. Fine paper; lightly and irregularly ruled. Fairly good hand, becoming careless in the latter part of the ms. A few additions and corrections.



(VM)
