Section: 1
“Circular of the Saṇgha Administrator to the Company of Monks” 僧政法律徒衆轉帖. Stipulations similar to those of a lay society circular except that here the meeting is to be held in a temple. Followed by the names of monks to whom the circular was sent with finger-marks indicating their receipt of it. Coarse, whitish paper; very poor hand.
