Pelliot chinois 2439

Section: v1
Beginning of a lay society circular. Scribbles of Hsü Tsai-hsing 徐再興. Many practice characters, most in a very poor hand. Part of the “Rhapsody on Yen-tzu” 晏子賦一首 (so titled at beginning). All as practice writing of Hsü Tsai-hsing. Clearly the efforts of a beginner.


Section: r1
Sarvāstivādavinayavihāṣa(?) 薩婆多毗尼毗婆沙. One line skipped but added later. Rather dark, ochre paper; ruled. Very good hand.
