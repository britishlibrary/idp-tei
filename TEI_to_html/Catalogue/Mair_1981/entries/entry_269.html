Or.8210/S.1386

Section: r1
Classic of Filial Piety, one scroll 孝經一卷 (end title). Drab paper. Bold, distinctive, fairly good hand; red punctuation. Colophon (same hand): “Copied and inscribed on the twelfth day of the twelfth month in the year 942/943 by Kao Ch’ing-tzu, lay student of the Eternal Peace Monastery” 維天福柒年壬寅歲十二月十二日永安寺學仕郎高清子書記了.


Section: v1
Several lay society circulars, mostly crudely written, one announcing an autumn meeting at the chairman’s home 主人家. Names. Scribblings, some apparently in same hand as r°. The year 944(?) 甲辰年 appears twice among the scribbled characters.
