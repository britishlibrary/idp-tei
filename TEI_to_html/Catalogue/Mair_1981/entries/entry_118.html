“Lyric Text of Chi Pu Cursing at the Battlefront in the Third Year of the Great Han [204 B.I.E.]” (end title; torn at beginning and end; same ms as Pelliot chinois 2747). T70.4-71. 大漢三[#58](=年; this note on ms in different hand)季布駡陣詞文一卷. Not ruled. Very thick, mediocre hand; few corrections. “Copy of (sic) Hymns on the Classic of Filial Piety by Yang P’u-ch’uan in Eighteen Stanzas pentasyllabic. Another name is Man-shan.” 楊浦川詠孝經書 (marked 卜 on the ms to show that it is an error) 壹拾捌章五言 一名滿山 (head title). “Hymns on the Classic of Filial Piety by Yang Man-shan in Eighteen Stanzas” 楊滿山詠孝經一十八章 (end title). Colophon (same hand): “Inscribed on the twenty-second day of the seventh month of the year 942 by Chang Fu-ying, lay student in the Monastery of the Three Realms.” 維大晉天福七年壬寅歲七月廿二日三界寺斈士郎張富[#59](=盈？)記. Additional colophon and poetic notation (same hand): “On the thirtieth day of the tenth month in the wu-ch’en year (968?), a lay student of the Monastery of the Three Realms.

My plan is to write two scrolls of copied text,
In my heart, I never have any doubt;
Naturally, you need to be a sincere person,
All the more you need a teacher-ācārya.”

戊辰年十月卅日三界寺斈士
