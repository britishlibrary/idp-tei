Section: v1
Long, prosimetric introduction to a popular lecture on the Amitābha-sūtra 仏說阿彌陁經 (lacks title). T460–477. Clearly intended for oral delivery since it contains many such expressions as the following: “say ‘Buddhaputra’” 稱佛子, “sing” 唱, “listen” 聽, and so forth. Lacks identifying ya-tso-wen and chiang-ching-wen formulas but refers to sūtra lecture 講經 at 473.12. After this line comes a discussion of the title of the sūtra, as is normal in a chiang-ching-wen. Before it is a prolix introduction that displays some features of certain ya-tso-wen (such as the questions “Can you? Are you willing?” 能不能 願不願 addressed to the congregation) but which contains far more personal detail than any other known ya-tso-wen. Rapid, moderately good hand.


Section: r1
Commentary on hymns concerning vinaya. Beige paper; fairly good hand.
