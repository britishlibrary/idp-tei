Section: r1
Classic of Filial Piety, one scroll 孝經一卷 (end title). Discolored beige paper. Moderately good hand; few corrections and additions. Colophon (same hand): “After being copied on the fourth day of the fifth month of 936 (?) by the śrāmaṇera (novice) of Numinous Chart [Monastery, given over to his] junior, the disciple Liang Tzu-sung.” 丙申年五月四日靈圖沙彌徳榮寫過後輩弟子梁子[#99](=松). Additional colophon (different, clumsier hand): “Already … on the fifteenth day of the second month of 940 (?) by the lay student of Numinous Chart Monastery, Li Erh(?)-chang. Liang Tzu-sung.” 庚子年二月十五日靈圖寺學郎李爯(=爾?)昌已 梁子松. The last three characters are on a separate line at the bottom of the scroll.

The lay student, big brother Chang Fu-ch’ien,
All of a sudden goes rushing off to the Classic of Filial Piety;
His Family Teachings of the Grand Duke is not at all defective,
He’s a clever fellow, truly an eccentric.
