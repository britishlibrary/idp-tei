Or.8210/S.4037

Section: v1
Brief reflections on moral issues; written in large characters. Lay society circular, in a poor hand, announcing a meeting at the home of the president, “Buddha-Slave” Fan; contributions of flour, oil, and grain are requested; fines for noncompliance. Anecdotes and texts relating to the Diamond Sūtra. Notes, anecdotes.


Section: r1
Zen composition. “Text of the Transfer of Merit (pariṇāmanā) at the Conclusion of a Sūtra Reading” 轉經後廻向文. Other noncanonical Zen texts. Soft, whitish paper. Mediocre, semicursive hand.
