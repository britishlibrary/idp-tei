Pelliot chinois 2808

Section: 1
“Stanzas on All Sorts of Behavior,” one scroll 百行章一卷 (end title). Mediocre hand. Colophon (in different, clumsier hand): “Inscription of copying on the twenty-fourth day of the fourth month in the year 923 by Yin I-chin from Ch’ing-ho, lay student at the Pure Land Monastery.” 維大梁貞明玖(=九)年癸未歲四月廿四日淨土寺學士郎清河陰義進書記之. There were counties named Ch’ing-ho in Chihli, Kiangsu, and Anhwei.
