Pelliot chinois 3707

Section: 1
“Family Feelings Society Circular” 親情社轉帖 (actual title). Announcing a meeting at an araṇya (hermitage, monastery) 蘭[#69](→若) to commemorate the death of a mother. Contributions for the memorial service include grain and wine. List of officers, many surnamed K’ung 孔. Dated the twenty-fourth day of the fourth month of the wu-wu year (958?) 戊午年四月二十四日.
