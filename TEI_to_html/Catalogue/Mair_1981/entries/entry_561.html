Section: 1
Lay society circular announcing the yearly spring meeting. On coarse, beige, fibrous paper in an extremely crude hand. The characters are very ill-formed and the message is not wholly intelligible because of miswritings and poorly constructed sentences. Obviously the product of someone who is just learning to write. Followed, after nine spaces, by row upon row of ill-formed practice characters based on the beginning of the Kṣitigarbha-bodhisattva-sūtra 地藏菩薩經.
