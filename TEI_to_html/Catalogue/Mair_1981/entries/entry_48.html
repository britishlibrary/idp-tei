Pelliot chinois 2715

Section: v1
Crudely written lay society circular. Practice characters.


Section: r1
Classic of Filial Piety, one scroll 孝經一卷. Good hand; red punctuation; very few corrections and additions, colophon (same hand but much larger): “Copying completed on the fourteenth day of the second month of the ting-hai year (927?). Verified to have no omissions or mistakes whatsoever. In transmitting this to later students, I request that they not hesitate to inscribe it.” 丁亥年二月十四日寫畢點勘一無脫錯傳之後學請不疑慮記也. Followed by a nonsensical comment in an inferior hand.
