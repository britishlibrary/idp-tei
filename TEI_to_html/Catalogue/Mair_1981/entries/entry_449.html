Sūtra lecture text on an undetermined sūtra. Not included in T. Has indentifying chiang-ching-wen formula. Uses the expression “this sung sūtra passage” 此唱經文 to introduce the prose discussion after citation of the sūtra. Prose sections closed with the mark of abbreviation (〻). Long, scroll form. Messy ms. Good hand on r°, becomes sloppy on v°. Stops near end of v° before being completed. Some marginal notations. Few evidences of immediacy.
