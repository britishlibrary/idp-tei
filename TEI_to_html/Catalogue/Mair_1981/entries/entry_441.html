Section: 1
“The Best of the Vimalakīrtinirdeśa-sūtra” 維摩砕(=碎)金一卷 (end title; beginning torn). Part of a variant type of sūtra lecture text on the Vimalakīrtinirdeśa-sūtra not included in T. Has chiang-ching-wen identifying formula (though not consistently); notations for singing (上下吟); refrain (仏子). Colophon: “Inscribed by the sūtra lecturing śramaṇa, K’uangyin, of the Lung-hsing Monastery in Ling-chou [Ninghsia]. I was constrained by Yüan-tsung (?) to come and was tied up many days in completing the writing of text books. Because of this, the saṇgha do not wish to respect the Buddhist sects of the northern regions as patriarchal (?) ….” 靈州龍興寺講經沙門匡胤記 被原宗堅來尤泥累日寫盡文書緣是僧家不欲奉阻(→祖?)朔方[#168](=釋)[#169](=家?)派. Stiff, thin paper of poor quality. Very messy ms. Mediocre hand, very hurried in parts; numerous miswritten characters and corrections.
