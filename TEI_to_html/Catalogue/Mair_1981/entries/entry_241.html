Vernacular, prosimetric story of Wu Tzu-hsü 伍子胥. Main ms for the composite text on T3.6(今)–28. Sheet(s) detached at beginning; scroll torn at end. Yellow paper with columns marked by folding. Mediocre hand; few corrections and additions. On v°, twentieth-century notation giving commercial numerals for 759 in red and the following identification: “Account of the Various Kingdoms” 列國傳. Lacks identifying formula for pien-wen; has higher proportion of prose to verse than typical pien-wen.
