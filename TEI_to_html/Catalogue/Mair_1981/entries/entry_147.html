Pelliot chinois 3627

Section: 1
“One Layout of the Transformation on Wang Ling in Which, during the Eighth Year of the Han Dynasty, Ch’u Is Destroyed and Han Gains the Ascendancy” 漢八年楚滅漢興王陵變一鋪 (end title, in same hand as text). The word “layout” in the title refers to a picture scroll used by transformation storytellers as a visual aid. T36.8.16(帝)–47.1. Ends abruptly with “enfeoffed ten thousand households” 封万戶, then starts to write the title 漢{X}{X} but crosses it out before writing it again (as given above). Has identifying pien-wen formula. Booklet of ordinary, light beige paper; faintly ruled. Fairly good hand; few corrections. Colophon (on a separate line and in a different hand): “Inscription of copying by the Bibliographic Secretary, Yen Wu-ch’eng, on the sixteenth day of the eighth month of the year 939.” 天福四年八月十六日孔目官閻物成寫記. Followed immediately by miscellaneous local documents in different, mostly poor hands and from different times.
