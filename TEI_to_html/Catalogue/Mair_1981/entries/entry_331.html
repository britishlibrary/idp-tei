Section: r1
Poem about the hours of the day. “The Chatterbox,” one scroll 齖䶗書壹卷 (end title). Used in collation of T858.12.21(色)–861. Drab, discolored paper; badly damaged, torn at both ends. Irregularly ruled. Very poor hand. Corrections, erasures; very messy ms. This is the prototype for the famous vernacular tale. “Fast Talking Li Ts’ui-lien” 快嘴李翠蓮. “Madame Ts’ui Trains a Girl” 崔氏夫人訓文女(→女文) (cf. Pelliot chinois 3780v°).
