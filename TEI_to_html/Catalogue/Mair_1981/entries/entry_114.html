Pelliot chinois 3369

Section: r1
Classic of Filial Piety, one scroll 孝經一卷. Folded for columns; torn. Fairly good hand. First colophon (different, very poor hand): “Inscription of copying a copyscroll by the student, So Shih-te, on the twenty-first day of the tenth month in the year 876.” 乾符三年十月二十一日學生索什徳書卷書記之也. Second colophon (in a still worse hand): “So Shih-te, lay student at Sha-chou, on the eighth day of the fifth month in the year 888 (?).” 咸通十五年五月八日沙州學郎索什徳. Other (mostly incoherent) scribbles and doodles. Beginning of a lay society circular.
