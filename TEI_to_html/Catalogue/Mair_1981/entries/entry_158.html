Section: r1
Classic of Filial Piety, one scroll, with preface 孝經一卷 并序 (head title). Classic of Filial Piety, one scroll (end title). Poor quality paper. Mediocre to fairly good hand; red punctuation; few corrections. Dated the twentieth of the twelfth month of the chi-hai year (939?) 己亥年十二月廿{日}.


Section: v1
Many lay society circulars. Practice characters, including a group labled “Miscellaneous Characters” 雜字一本. Very poor hands. Scribbles include: “A cow is a powerful Bodhisattva” 牛是大力菩薩. “Brahmacārin Wang” 王梵之(→志). Inscription at very edge (same hand as r°): “Ch’en X-x, lay student of the Numinous Chart Monastery.” 靈圖寺斈郎陳(torn off) .
