Pelliot chinois 2680

Section: v1
Accounts. “Preaching Text of the Śrāvaka” 聲聞唱道文 (head title). This is a text for a sermon; has notations such as “repeat three times” 三說 and directs the Teacher of the Commandments 戒師 to mount his high platform 高座 and intone in a “Sanskritic voice” 梵音. Not a chiang-ching-wen. Other miscellaneous texts. Very poor to mediocre hands.


Section: r1
Biographies of eminent monks. Practice writing. Official document. Other miscellaneous texts. Mediocre to fairly good hands.
