Section: r1
Text about consolation for those who are lost. Semi-cursive, mediocre hand; ms a bit messy. Colophon (same hand): “Inscribed in his own hand by Chai Ch’ing-tzu, lay student of the Great Cloud [Monastery], on the first day of the seventh month of the year 960.” 維大周顯徳七年歲次庚甲(→申)七月一日大雲斈郎[#79](=祭)[#80](=清)子自手記.


Section: v1
Religious poetry by “the bhadanta (i.e., one of great virtue), ‘Round Mirror,’ commissioned by the Emperor to oversee the inner bodhimaṇdala (i.e., religious meeting ground) of the Thousand Blessings Monastery on the street to the right.” 右街千福寺 內道場應制大德圓鑒.
