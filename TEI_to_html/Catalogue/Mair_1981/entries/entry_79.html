Pelliot chinois 3051

Section: r1
Nidāna on ‘Intends-to-Create-Merit’ who is reborn in heaven for having given her devotion to a stūpa (lacks title; torn at beginning). T767.3(three damaged lines of ms precede this)–769.7. First part (in a different hand) of the composite text given in T is from Or.8210/S.3491 (q.v.). Fairly good, standard hand; corrections, additions, erasures. Colophon (same hand): “Inscribed and copied in his own hand by the Zen monk of Three Realms Monastery, Fa-pao, on the twenty-third day of the fourth month of the year 953.” 維大周廣順叁年癸[#20]歲肆月二十日三界寺禪僧法保自手寫記. The name of the author, Pao-hsüan 保宣, another monk, may be found in T769.5.
