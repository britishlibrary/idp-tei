Pelliot chinois 3692

Section: r1
“Letters between Li Ling and Su Wu” 李陵与蘇武書一首. Torn. Stylish, good hand. Colophon (same hand): “Copied and inscribed on the twenty-fifth day of the second month in the jen-wu year (922?) by So Fu-t’ung, lay student at the Golden Light Monastery.” 壬午年二月二十五日金光明寺學郎索富通記之耳.


Section: v1
Lay society circulars, one announcing a meeting before the gate of the Numinous Chart Monastery 靈圖寺. A fine of wine will be imposed on those who come late. Dated in the eleventh month of the same year as r°. Doodles; practice characters; the Thousand Character Primer 千字文 written for practice. Upside-down and amidst lay society circulars: “Copyscroll of So Phoo-t’ung, lay student of the Golden Light Monastery.” 金光明寺學士郎索富通書卷.
