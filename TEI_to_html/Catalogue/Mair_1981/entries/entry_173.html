Section: r1
“Sūtra Lecture Text for the Imperial Birthday of the Year 933 in the Hall of Restoration” 長興四年中興殿應聖節講經文 (head title). T411–424. This is the only extant chiang-ching-wen which actually bears a title designating itself as such. Has identifying chiang-ching-wen formula. Fairly good drab paper; not exactly same quality in each of three sheets that make up scroll. Rapid, fairly good hand; some additions. At end, in a different hand, “Copied from the Prajñā-sūtra of the Benevolent King.” 仁王般若經抄. This text does make a half-hearted effort to explicate the title and a portion of the sūtra. But it consists mostly of lavish paeans to the reigning monarch.
