Pelliot chinois 3093

Section: r1
Exegetical text about the rebirth of Maitreya in the Tuṣita heaven (lacks title). T646–655. There are 34 additional characters at the beginning of the ms that should have been included in the T text. These deal with the name “Maitreya,” the word “Bodhisattva,” and the Chinese proclivity for the abbreviation of Sanskrit words. Lacks identifying chiang-ching-wen formula. Fairly good hand; very few corrections.

Medical text.
