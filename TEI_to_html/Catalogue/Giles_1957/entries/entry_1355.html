金剛般若波羅密經
金剛般若波羅蜜經 Jin gang ban ruo po luo mi jing. 32 secs. With introductory matter (begin, imperfect) and prayer. At end: 西川過家真印本 'From the true printed text of the Guo family in XiChuan'. Dhāraṇī. Colophon: l'. Mediocre MS. Booklet of 29 ff.

Colophon (): 天祐二年歲次乙丑四年廿三日八十二老人手寫此經流傅信士
Colophon (): On the 23rd of the 4th moon of the yichou year, the 2nd of Tianyu [29 May, 905], an old man aged 82 copied this sutra with his own hand for transmission to the faithful.
