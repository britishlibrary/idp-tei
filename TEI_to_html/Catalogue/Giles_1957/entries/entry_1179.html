Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra (N.10; K.V.6c; T.235)
金剛般若波羅密經
*Fo shuo jin gang ban ruo jing. Begin. mtd. Good MS. Thin, light yellow paper. Colophon (in the same hand).

Colophon (): 大隋大業十二年七月廿三日清信優婆夷劉圓淨敬寫此經以茲微善願為一切眾生轉讀聞者敬信皆悟苦空見者受持俱勝[?for 昇]常樂又願劉身早離邊荒速還京輦罪障消除福慶?集
Colophon (): On the 23rd of the seventh month of Day 12 in the Great Sui dynasty [9 Sept., 616] the female lay devotee [upasika] Liu Yuanjing reverently caused a copy of this sutra to be made, praying that by this slight act of merit all those who hear it when read aloud may reverently believe and awaken to the unreality of suffering, and that those who read it may bear its precepts in mind and rise to enduring felicity. She also prays that she herself may soon quit this desert frontier region and speedily return to the Imperial capital; that the barriers of her sin may be broken down, and that manifold blessings may descend upon her.
