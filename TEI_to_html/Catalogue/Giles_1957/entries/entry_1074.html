金剛般若波羅密經
*Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra. Good MS. Crisp yellow paper. With tabulated colophon. Notes 12 sheet of paper, 10 remain.

Colophon (): 咸亨三年五月十九日左春坊楷書吳元禮寫用麻紙十二張潢手解善集 …
Colophon (): Copied on the 19th of the 5th moon of the 3rd year of Xianheng (19 June 672) by Wu Yuanli, writer in the clerkly style attached to the left division of the Crown Prince's staff. 12 sheet of hemp paper used. Mounter and dyer, Xie Shanzhi. Three revisions all by Xiaoyi. The text was minutely perused by Shenfu, Jiashang, the active director Huili, and the spiritual director Daocheng, all of the Taiyuan Monastery. The panguan was Xiang Yigan and the general superintendent Yu Chang, a high official at Chang'an.
