Colophon (): Reverently copied by the Buddhist disciple Wang Chao, firstly, on behalf of his parents of seven previous incarnations, and secondly, for the benefit of his [deceased] wife and daughter, as well as the wriggling and moving creatures of the whole earth, praying that all living things may obtain release from suffering and emancipation from the body, reject the false and return to the true, all acquire profound intelligence and constantly hear the Law of Buddha.
