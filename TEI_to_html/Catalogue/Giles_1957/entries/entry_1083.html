金剛般若波羅密經
*Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra. Very good MS. Crisp yellow paper. With tabuated colophon dated 'The 11th of the intercalary 3rd moon of the 3rd year of Shangyuan' [29 April, 676]. The copyist was Ouyang Xuanzhe, and 12 sheets of paper were used. The panguan (judge) was Li De, and the general superintendent Yan Xuandao. In other respects the colophon is similar to that of Or.8210/S.36. Verso: part of colophon repeated.

Colophon (): Copied on the eleventh of the intercalary third month of Shangyuan 3 [29 April 676] by Ouyang Xuanzhe, writer in the clerkly style, attached to the left division of the Crown Prince's staff. 12 sheets of paper were used. Mounter and dyer, Xie Shanji. The three revisions were all done by Xiao Yi. The text was minutely perused by Shenfu, Jiashang, the active director Huili and the spiritual director Daocheng, all of the Taiyuan Monastery. The judge was Li De. The general superintendent was Yan Xuandao.
