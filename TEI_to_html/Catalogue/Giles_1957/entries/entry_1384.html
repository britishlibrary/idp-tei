佛說閆羅王受記經 Fo shuo yan luo wang shou ji jing. An apocryphal sutra, corresponding partially to K. Supp. C. XXIII 4(9). Longer title at begin. : see 1372 (2). Colophon [? A.D. 911]. ff.10-15R.. Indifferent MS. Booklet of 15ff. Coarse buff paper.

Colophon (): 奉為老耕牛一 頭敬寫金剛一卷受記一卷願此牛身領受功德往生淨土再莫受畜生身天曹地府分明分付莫令更有雔訟 辛未年正月
Colophon (): On behalf of one old ploughing ox I have reverently copied out the Diamond [sutra] in 1 chain and the shou chi in 1 juan, praying that this ox may personally receive the merit therefrom, and be reborn in the Pure Land, never again to come to this life in the body of a domestic animal. May this be clearly ordained by the officers dispensing justice in the underworld, so that there may be no further enmity or quarrel [between the ox and the owner]. In the first moon of the xinwei year.
