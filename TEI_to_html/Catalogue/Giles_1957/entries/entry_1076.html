金剛般若波羅密經
*Jin gang ban ruo bo luo mi jing. Very good MS. Bright yellow paper. First sheet added in a different hand. Colophon. Empress Wu characters in the colophon 聖，年，月，日，國.

Colophon (): 聖曆三年五月廿三日大升拔谷副使上柱國南陽縣開國公陰仁協寫經為金輪聖神皇帝及七世父母合家大小得六品發願月別許寫一卷得五品月別寫經兩卷久為征行末辦紙墨不從本願今辦寫得普為一切轉讀。
Colophon (): On the 23rd day of the fifth month of Shengli 3 [14 June 700] Yin Renxie, Assistant Commissioner at Dashengbagu, 'shangzhuguo', and 'kaiguogong' of Nanyang district, had this sutra copied on behalf of the Holy and Divine Sovereign Lord of the Golden Wheel [i. e. the Empress Wu], his own parents of seven previous incarnations, and all the members of his family, great and small. He made a vow that if he was promoted to the sixth grade he would have one roll copied every month, and that if he was promoted to the fifth grade he would have two rolls of a sutra copied every month. But for a long time, owing to warlike operations, paper and ink have not been procurable, so that he did not fulfil his vow. Now at last, materials having been procured, he has been able to have this copy made, to be unrolled and read by all without exception.
