*Pañcaviṃśatisāhasrikāprajñap﻿āramitasūtra, ch. 17, pin 76-78 [=ch. 17, 18]. Pin 77 is entitled 攝取眾生, and pin 78 拔出眾生, but they agree with the corresponding pin of the Canon, though with different names. Note after title of each pin: 此放光經有廿卷合九十品 'This Fang guang sutra is divided into 20 chapters, comprising 90 pin'. Note at end. Very good MS. of 7th cent. Good brownish-yellow paper.

Colophon (): 此丘尼梵守所供養經
Colophon (): Sutra offered by the bhiksuni Fanshou as an act of worship.
