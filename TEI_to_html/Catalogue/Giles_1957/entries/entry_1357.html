Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra, 32 secs. ff.1-44R (多 is wrongly inserted in the title on the cover and on f.6 r). Preceded by prayers, dhāraṇī, and pen-and-ink drawings of four Bodhisattvas and the Four Lokapāla (Guardians) (bis) ; followed by dhāraṇī. Colophon. Fairly good MS. Thick whitish buff paper. A well preserved booklet of 52 ff. bound with string.

Colophon (): 于時大宋乾德七年己巳歲四月十五日大乘賢者兼當學禪錄何江通發心敬為大小經三筑子計九卷盡夜念誦一心供養故記之耳
Colophon (): On the 15th of the 4th moon of the Jixu year, the 7th of Qiande [really the 2nd of Kaibao] in the Great Sung dynasty [4 May,[969], the Mahānayist worthy and Dhyāna recorder in the school, He Jiang Tong, with pious intent reverently copied out three books of sūtras, large and small, amounting to nine chuan, then recited them all night long, and now whole-heartedly dedicates them, making this record of the fact
