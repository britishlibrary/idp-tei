Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra (N.10; K.V.6c; T.235)
金剛般若波羅密經
*Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra, sec *28-32. At begin: A coloured drawing of Vajrapāṇi, and prayers. At end: 西川過家印真本 'From the true printed text of the Guo family in Xichuan'. Dhāraṇī. Mediocre MS. Buff paper, brittle and discoloured. 6 leaves of a booklet. Note: RAMAN spectroscopy on the ink revealed no traces of blood, but it may be too diluted to detect easily.

Colophon (): 天祐三年丙寅二月三日八十三老人刺左手中指出血以香墨寫此金經流傅信心人一無所願本性實空無有願樂
Colophon (): On the 3rd of the 2nd moon of ping-yin, the 3rd year of Tianyu [28 Feb., 906] an old man of 83 pricked the middle finger of his left hand so as to draw blood, with which he mixed fragrant ink and copied out this Jin [gang] jing for transmission to men to believing heart. There is nothing for which he wishes to pray, his nature having attained absolute vacuity, free from any desire for pleasure.
