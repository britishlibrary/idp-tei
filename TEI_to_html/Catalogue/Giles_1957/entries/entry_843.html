Daśanhasrik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra, 摩訶般若波羅蜜經 Mo he ban ruo bo luo mi jing, ch. 4, pin 9, 10. Very good MS. of about AD 600. Thin golden-yellow paper. A few cols. at begin. are in a later hand on thick yellow paper. Colophon.

Colophon (): 菩薩戒弟子張洪元敬寫流通供養
Colophon (): Respectfully copied for circulation as an act of worship by the disciple of Bodhisattva discipline Zhang Hongyuan.
