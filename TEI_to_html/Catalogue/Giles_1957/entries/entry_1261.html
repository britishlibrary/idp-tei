Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra (N.10; K.V.6c; T.235)
金剛般若波羅密經
*Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra. At the end：金剛經陀羅神呪. Jin gang jing tuo luo ni shen zhou. A dhāraṇī, or incantation, followed by the note [colophon]. Good MS. Thick yellow paper.

Colophon (): 若有人誦此呪一遍勝誦金剛一万九千遍
Colophon (): By reciting this dhāraṇī once, a man will gain as much merit as if he were to recite the Diamond Sūtra 19,000 times.
