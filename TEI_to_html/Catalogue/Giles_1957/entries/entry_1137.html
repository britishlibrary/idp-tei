Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra (N.10; K.V.6c; T.235)
金剛般若波羅密經
*Vajracchedik﻿āprajñap﻿āramitasūtra. Very good MS. of 7th cent. Colophon. Dark yellow paper.

Colophon (): 弟子令狐為龍王行病鬼王怨家債主敬造像二區寫金剛般若一百部法華一部於靈安寺壽禪師院內潔凈寫
Colophon (): The disciple Linghu has reverently caused two images to be made, and a hundred copies of the 'Jin gang ban ruo' and one section of the 'Fa hua' to be prepared, for the benefit of the Dragon King, the King of the disease-circulating demons, and his own enemies and creditors [of a former existence]. Copied after purification, in the Court of the Dhyana Master Shou at the Ling'an Monastery.
